# Lesson 4: The Double Life of Alfred Bloggs

## 课文内容 (Text)

These days, people who do manual work often receive far more money than people who work in offices. People who work in offices are frequently referred to as 'white-collar workers' for the simple reason that they usually wear a collar and tie to go to work. Such is human nature, that a great many people are often willing to sacrifice higher pay for the privilege of becoming white-collar workers.

This can give rise to curious situations, as it did in the case of Alfred Bloggs who worked as a dustman for the Ellesmere Corporation. When he got married, Alf was too embarrassed to say anything to his wife about his job. He simply told her that he worked for the Corporation.

Every morning, he left home dressed in a smart black suit. He then changed into overalls and spent the next eight hours as a dustman. Before returning home at night, he took a shower and changed back into his suit.

Alf did this for over two years and his fellow dustmen kept his secret. Alf's wife has never discovered that she married a dustman and she never will, for Alf has just found another job. He will soon be working in an office.

He will be earning only half as much as he used to, but he feels that his rise in status is well worth the loss of money. From now on, he will wear a suit all day and others will call him 'Mr.Bloggs', not 'Alf'.

## 语法要点解析 (Grammar & Key Structures)

### 1. 双重比较结构与让步 (Double Comparison and Concession)
* **经典原句**：*The manual worker is often paid more than the white-collar worker, but his status is lower.*
* **核心解析**：
**more than** 引导比较状语从句，这里省略了从句中与主句重复的部分。后半句通过连词 `but` 进行转折，对比了体力劳动者与白领在薪水与社会地位上的差异。

### 2. 引导让步状语从句的 though/although
* **经典原句**：*Though he may wear a suit, his work is...*
* **核心解析**：
**Though** 引导让步状语从句，意为“虽然，尽管”。在英语中，`though/although` 不能与 `but` 连用在同一个句子里，但可以与副词 `yet` 或 `still` 连用。

## 写作仿写与思辨口语 (Writing Practice & Critical Thinking)

### 1. ✍️ 提分句型仿写 (Pattern Drill)
* **句型名称**：Though he may... (虽然他可能……但其实……)
* **句型公式**：`Though + [主语] + may + [动词原形], + [主句]`
* **仿写练习 1**：虽然他看起来很严肃，但实际上他非常有幽默感。 (Though he may look serious, he actually has a great sense of humor.)
* **仿写练习 2**：虽然这个项目短期内可能会遇到困难，但它有很大的长期潜力。 (Though the project may encounter difficulties in the short term, it has great long-term potential.)

### 2. 🔍 近义词/易混词辨析 (Word Discrimination)
* **对比辨析**：**profession vs. occupation**
* **使用差异解析**：
`occupation` 是日常生活中“职业”的通称，指任何能赚钱的工作。而 `profession` 特指“**专业性职业**”，指需要接受长期高等教育或获取专业技术认证才能从事的行业（如医生、律师、建筑师）。
* **地道表达搭配**：
* professional ethics (职业道德)
* enter a profession (进入某个行业)
* major occupation (主要工作)

### 3. 💬 课后思辨讨论题 (Speaking & Writing Prompts)
* **Question 1**: Why is there a persistent gap in social status between manual workers and office clerks, even when manual workers earn more?
* **Question 2**: Do you think the division between 'white-collar' and 'blue-collar' jobs is fading in the modern digital economy?
* **高分表达提示 (Hint Words)**: *social status (社会地位), academic qualifications (学术资质), intellectual labor (脑力劳动), income disparity (收入差距)*
