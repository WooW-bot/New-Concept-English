# Lesson 2: Thirteen Equals One

## 课文内容 (Text)

Our vicar is always raising money for one cause or another, but he has never managed to get enough money to have the church clock repaired. The big clock which used to strike the hours day and night was damaged many years ago and has been silent ever since.

One night, however, our vicar woke up with a start:the clock was striking the hours! Looking at his watch, he saw that it was one o'clock, but the bell struck thirteen times before it stopped.

Armed with a torch, the vicar went up into the clock tower to see what was going on. In the torchlight, he caught sight of a figure whom he immediately recognized as Bill Wilkins, our local grocer. 'Whatever are you doing up here Bill?' asked the vicar in surprise.

'I'm trying to repair the bell, ' answered Bill. 'I've been coming up here night after night for weeks now. You see, I was hoping to give you a surprise.' 'You certainly did give me a surprise!' said the vicar.

'You've probably woken up everyone in the village as well. Still, I'm glad the bell is working again.' 'That's the trouble, vicar, 'answered Bill. 'It's working all right, but I'm afraid that at one o'clock it will strike thirteen times and there's nothing I can do about it.' 'We'll get used to that, Bill, 'said the vicar.

'Thirteen is not as good as one, but it's better than nothing. Now let's go downstairs and have a cup of tea.'

## 语法要点解析 (Grammar & Key Structures)

### 1. 现在进行时表经常性动作或说话人感情色彩
* **经典原句**：*The vicar is always losing his keys...*
* **核心解析**：
**is always losing** 为“现在进行时 + 频度副词 always/constantly/forever”结构。
* **语法意义**：该结构不表示此时此刻正在进行的动作，而是表示说话者对某种经常性行为的**抱怨、不赞成、赞赏或惊讶**等强烈情绪。此处表无奈和善意的抱怨。

### 2. 限制性定语从句中的介词前置
* **经典原句**：*The church clock, a famous landmark on which the locals have relied for years, was damaged by gales.*
* **核心解析**：
**on which the locals have relied for years** 是定语从句，修饰 `landmark`。
* **结构解析**：动词短语 `rely on` 中的介词 `on` 被提前到了关系代词 `which` 的前面。这是一种比较正式的英文书面语表达形式。

## 写作仿写与思辨口语 (Writing Practice & Critical Thinking)

### 1. ✍️ 提分句型仿写 (Pattern Drill)
* **句型名称**：It makes no difference whether... or not (无论……与否都无所谓/没有区别)
* **句型公式**：`It makes no/little difference whether + [主谓结构] + or not`
* **仿写练习 1**：无论我们现在出发还是推迟半小时，我们都会遭遇严重的交通拥堵。 (It makes no difference whether we set out now or delay for half an hour; we will encounter heavy traffic.)
* **仿写练习 2**：无论你是否同意他的观点，事实都摆在眼前。 (It makes little difference whether you agree with his opinion or not; the facts are right in front of us.)

### 2. 🔍 近义词/易混词辨析 (Word Discrimination)
* **对比辨析**：**rely on vs. depend on**
* **使用差异解析**：
两者均表示“依赖，依靠”。`depend on` 多指“取决于某事物（因果关系）”，或由于缺乏资源不得不依赖对方。而 `rely on` 更加侧重于“在精神或行动上**信任并寄托希望于某人或某物**”，强调信任度（trustworthiness）。
* **地道表达搭配**：
* rely on someone's word (相信某人的话)
* rely heavily on (严重依赖)
* dependable (可靠的)

### 3. 💬 课后思辨讨论题 (Speaking & Writing Prompts)
* **Question 1**: Why do people in modern society tend to place high value on historical landmarks, even when they require expensive maintenance?
* **Question 2**: How does a shared symbol (like a local clock tower or church) help build a sense of community?
* **高分表达提示 (Hint Words)**: *landmark (地标), preserve (保存), spiritual bond (精神纽带), local heritage (地方遗产), community cohesion (社区凝聚力)*
