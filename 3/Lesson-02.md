# Lesson 2: Thirteen Equals One

## 课文内容 (Text)

Our vicar is always raising money for one cause or another, but he has never managed to get enough money to have the church clock repaired.
我们的牧师总是在为这样或那样的事业筹款，但他却从未能筹到足够的钱来修理教堂的时钟。
The big clock which used to strike the hours day and night was damaged many years ago and has been silent ever since.
那座过去日夜报时的大钟多年前就损坏了，从此一直处于静止状态。

One night, however, our vicar woke up with a start: the clock was striking the hours!
然而有一天夜里，我们的牧师惊醒了：大钟正在报时！
Looking at his watch, he saw that it was one o'clock, but the bell struck thirteen times before it stopped.
他看了看表，发现是凌晨一点钟，但钟声敲了十三下才停住。

Armed with a torch, the vicar went up into the clock tower to see what was going on.
牧师拿着手电筒，爬上钟楼去查看究竟发生了什么事。
In the torchlight, he caught sight of a figure whom he immediately recognized as Bill Wilkins, our local grocer.
在手电筒的光亮下，他看见了一个人影，一眼就认出那是我们当地的杂货商比尔·威尔金斯。
'Whatever are you doing up here Bill?' asked the vicar in surprise.
“比尔，你在这儿干什么呢？”牧师惊讶地问。

'I'm trying to repair the bell,' answered Bill.
“我正在试着修理这口钟，”比尔回答说。
'I've been coming up here night after night for weeks now.'
“几个星期以来，我每天晚上都到这儿来。”
'You see, I was hoping to give you a surprise.'
“你看，我原想给你一个惊喜。”
'You certainly did give me a surprise!' said the vicar.
“你确实给了我一个惊喜！”牧师说。

'You've probably woken up everyone in the village as well.'
“你大概也把全村人都吵醒了。”
'Still, I'm glad the bell is working again.'
“不过，我很高兴钟又响了。”
'That's the trouble, vicar,' answered Bill.
“问题就在这儿，牧师，”比尔回答说。
'It's working all right, but I'm afraid that at one o'clock it will strike thirteen times and there's nothing I can do about it.'
“它确实响了，但我恐怕在一点钟的时候它会敲十三下，对此我实在无能为力。”
'We'll get used to that, Bill,' said the vicar.
“我们会习惯的，比尔，”牧师说。

'Thirteen is not as good as one, but it's better than nothing.'
“十三下虽然不如一下好，但总比不响强。”
'Now let's go downstairs and have a cup of tea.'
“现在我们下楼去喝杯茶吧。”

## 语法要点解析 (Grammar & Key Structures)

### 1. 现在进行时表经常性动作或说话人感情色彩
* **经典原句**：*The vicar is always losing his keys...*
* **核心解析**：
**is always losing** 为“现在进行时 + 频度副词 always/constantly/forever”结构。
* **语法意义**：该结构不表示此时此刻正在进行的动作，而是表示说话者对某种经常性行为的**抱怨、不赞成、赞赏或惊讶**等强烈情绪。此处表无奈和善意的抱怨。

### 2. 限制性定语从句中的介词前置
* **经典原句**：*The church clock, a famous landmark on which the locals have relied for years, was damaged by gales.*
* **核心解析**：
**on which the locals have relied for years** 是定语从句，修饰 `landmark`。
* **结构解析**：动词短语 `rely on` 中的介词 `on` 被提前到了关系代词 `which` 的前面。这是一种比较正式的英文书面语表达形式。

## 写作仿写与思辨口语 (Writing Practice & Critical Thinking)

### 1. ✍️ 提分句型仿写 (Pattern Drill)
* **句型名称**：It makes no difference whether... or not (无论……与否都无所谓/没有区别)
* **句型公式**：`It makes no/little difference whether + [主谓结构] + or not`
* **仿写练习 1**：无论我们现在出发还是推迟半小时，我们都会遭遇严重的交通拥堵。 (It makes no difference whether we set out now or delay for half an hour; we will encounter heavy traffic.)
* **仿写练习 2**：无论你是否同意他的观点，事实都摆在眼前。 (It makes little difference whether you agree with his opinion or not; the facts are right in front of us.)

### 2. 🔍 近义词/易混词辨析 (Word Discrimination)
* **对比辨析**：**rely on vs. depend on**
* **使用差异解析**：
两者均表示“依赖，依靠”。`depend on` 多指“取决于某事物（因果关系）”，或由于缺乏资源不得不依赖对方。而 `rely on` 更加侧重于“在精神或行动上**信任并寄托希望于某人或某物**”，强调信任度（trustworthiness）。
* **地道表达搭配**：
* rely on someone's word (相信某人的话)
* rely heavily on (严重依赖)
* dependable (可靠的)

### 3. 💬 课后思辨讨论题 (Speaking & Writing Prompts)
* **Question 1**: Why do people in modern society tend to place high value on historical landmarks, even when they require expensive maintenance?
* **Question 2**: How does a shared symbol (like a local clock tower or church) help build a sense of community?
* **高分表达提示 (Hint Words)**: *landmark (地标), preserve (保存), spiritual bond (精神纽带), local heritage (地方遗产), community cohesion (社区凝聚力)*
