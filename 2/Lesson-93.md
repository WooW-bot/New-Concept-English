# Lesson 93: A Noble Gift

## 课文内容 (Text)

One of the most famous monuments in the world, the Statue of Liberty, was presented to the USA in the nineteenth century by the people of France.
世界上最著名的纪念碑之一——自由女神像，是法国人民在19世纪赠送给美国的。
The great statue, which was designed by the sculptor Auguste Bartholdi, took ten years to complete.
这座伟大的雕像由雕塑家奥古斯特·巴托尔迪设计，耗时十年才完成。
The actual figure was made of copper supported by a metal framework which had been especially constructed by Eiffel.
雕像主体由铜制成，并由埃菲尔专门建造的金属框架支撑。

Before it could be transported to the United States, a site had to be found for it and a pedestal had to be built.
在它被运往美国之前，必须先为它找到一个安放地点，并建造一个底座。
The site chosen was an island at the entrance of New York Harbour.
选定的地点是纽约港入口处的一座岛屿。
By 1884, a statue which was 151 feet tall had been erected in Paris.
到1884年，一座151英尺高的雕像已在巴黎竖立起来。
The following year, it was taken to pieces and sent to America.
第二年，它被拆解并运往美国。

By the end of October 1886, the statue had been put together again and it was officially presented to the American people by Bartholdi.
到1886年10月底，雕像被重新组装完毕，并由巴托尔迪正式赠送给美国人民。
Ever since then, the great monument has been a symbol of liberty for the millions of people who have passed through New York Harbour to make their homes in America.
从那时起，这座伟大的纪念碑就成了数百万经由纽约港进入美国安家落户的人们心中自由的象征。

## 语法要点解析 (Grammar & Key Structures)

### 1. 过去完成时被动语态 (Past Perfect Passive)
* **经典原句**：*The actual figure was made of copper supported by a metal framework which had been especially constructed by Eiffel.*
* **核心解析**：
**had been constructed** 表示在过去某个时间点（ statue 被完成时）之前，金属框架就已经被建造好了。
* **结构解析**：`had + been + 过去分词`。用于强调在过去某一动作发生之前已经完成的被动动作，体现了动作的时间先后顺序。

### 2. 过去完成时 (Past Perfect Tense)
* **经典原句**：*By the end of October 1886, the statue had been put together again...*
* **核心解析**：
**had been put together** 表示在 1886 年 10 月底之前，组装工作已经完成。
* **结构解析**：`had + done`。过去完成时常与 `by + 过去时间点` 连用，表示“截止到过去某时，某事已经发生”。

### 3. 定语从句 (Attributive Clause)
* **经典原句**：*...the millions of people who have passed through New York Harbour to make their homes in America.*
* **核心解析**：
**who have passed through...** 是由关系代词 `who` 引导的定语从句，修饰先行词 `people`。
* **结构解析**：当先行词为“人”时，使用 `who` 或 `that` 引导从句，在从句中作主语。

## 写作仿写与思辨口语 (Writing Practice & Critical Thinking)

### 1. ✍️ 提分句型仿写 (Pattern Drill)
* **句型名称**：Ever since then, ... has been ... (从那时起，……一直就是……)
* **句型公式**：`Ever since then, [主语] + have/has been + [名词/形容词]`
* **仿写练习 1**：从那时起，这首歌就成了我们友谊的象征。 (Ever since then, this song has been a symbol of our friendship.)
* **仿写练习 2**：从那时起，他一直是我最敬佩的导师。 (Ever since then, he has been the mentor I admire the most.)

### 2. 🔍 近义词/易混词辨析 (Word Discrimination)
* **对比辨析**：**present vs. offer**
* **使用差异解析**：
`present` 侧重于“正式地、隆重地赠送或颁发”，通常带有仪式感，如颁奖、赠送国礼；`offer` 侧重于“主动提出给予某物”，对方可以接受也可以拒绝，不一定有正式仪式。
* **地道表达搭配**：
* present a gift (赠送礼物)
* present an award (颁奖)
* offer help (提供帮助)
* offer a job (提供工作机会)

### 3. 💬 课后思辨讨论题 (Speaking & Writing Prompts)
* **Question 1**: Why do you think the Statue of Liberty is considered a "noble gift" rather than just a piece of art?
* **Question 2**: If you were to design a monument to represent your country, what would it be and why?
* **高分表达提示 (Hint Words)**: *monument (纪念碑), cultural heritage (文化遗产), friendship (友谊), core value (核心价值), artistic design (艺术设计), represent (代表)*
