# Lesson 12: Goodbye and Good Luck

## 课文内容 (Text)

Our neighbour, Captain Charles Alison, will sail from Portsmouth tomorrow. We'll meet him at the harbour early in the morning. He will be in his small boat, Topsail. Topsail is a famous little boat. It has sailed across the Atlantic many times.

Captain Alison will set out at eight o'clock, so we'll have plenty of time. We'll see his boat and then we'll say goodbye to him.

He will be away for two months. We are very proud of him. He will take part in an important race across the Atlantic.

## 语法要点解析 (Grammar & Key Structures)

### 1. 一般将来时 (Future Simple Tense with 'will')
*   **经典原句**：*Our neighbour, Captain Charles Alison, will sail from Portsmouth tomorrow.*
*   **核心解析**：
    `will + 动词原形` 构成一般将来时，表示将来要发生的动作或状态，通常用于预测、意愿或计划。
*   **结构解析**：主语 + `will` + 动词原形。常与表示将来的时间状语（如 `tomorrow`, `next week`, `in two months`）连用。

### 2. 现在完成时 (Present Perfect Tense)
*   **经典原句**：*It has sailed across the Atlantic many times.*
*   **核心解析**：
    `have/has + 过去分词` 构成现在完成时，表示过去发生的动作对现在造成的影响，或表示从过去某个时间点开始一直持续到现在的动作或状态。
*   **结构解析**：主语 + `has` (用于第三人称单数) / `have` (用于其他人称) + 动词的过去分词。常与 `many times`, `ever`, `never`, `already`, `yet`, `for`, `since` 等词连用。

### 3. 动词短语 (Phrasal Verbs)
*   **经典原句**：*Captain Alison will set out at eight o'clock.* / *He will take part in an important race across the Atlantic.*
*   **核心解析**：
    动词短语（Phrasal Verbs）是由一个动词加上一个或多个介词/副词构成，其意义往往与原动词的意义不同。
*   **结构解析**：
    *   `set out`: 意为“出发，启程”。
    *   `take part in`: 意为“参加，参与”。这类短语在英语中非常常见，需要整体记忆其意义和用法。

## 写作仿写与思辨口语 (Writing Practice & Critical Thinking)

### 1. ✍️ 提分句型仿写 (Pattern Drill)
*   **句型名称**：Be proud of... (为...感到骄傲/自豪)
*   **句型公式**：`Subject + be + proud of + [名词/代词/动名词]`
*   **仿写练习 1**：我们为他们克服了所有困难而感到自豪。(We are very proud of them for overcoming all difficulties.)
*   **仿写练习 2**：她为自己能独立完成这个项目而感到骄傲。(She is proud of herself for completing the project independently.)

### 2. 🔍 近义词/易混词辨析 (Word Discrimination)
*   **对比辨析**：**sail vs. set out**
*   **使用差异解析**：
    `sail` 主要指“航行”，特指船只在水上移动，或人乘船旅行。它强调的是移动的方式和介质。而 `set out` 是一个更通用的动词短语，意为“出发，启程”，可以用于任何形式的旅行（陆路、水路、空路），强调的是旅程的开始。
*   **地道表达搭配**：
    *   sail: sail the seas (航海), sail a boat (驾驶船只), set sail (启航)
    *   set out: set out on a journey (启程旅行), set out for work (出发去上班), set out to achieve (着手实现)

### 3. 💬 课后思辨讨论题 (Speaking & Writing Prompts)
*   **Question 1**: Captain Alison is taking part in an important race across the Atlantic. What kind of qualities do you think a person needs to undertake such a challenging journey?
*   **Question 2**: Have you ever felt very proud of someone you know, or of yourself? Describe the situation and why you felt that way.
*   **高分表达提示 (Hint Words)**: *courage (勇气), determination (决心), resilience (韧性), preparation (准备), support system (支持系统), accomplishment (成就), inspiration (启发)*
