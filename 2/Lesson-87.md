# Lesson 87: A Perfect Alibi

## 课文内容 (Text)

'At the time the murder was committed, I was travelling on the 8 o'clock train to London,' said the man.
“谋杀案发生时，我正乘8点钟的火车去伦敦，”那个人说。
'Do you always catch such an early train?' asked the inspector.
“你总是赶这么早的火车吗？”警官问道。
'Of course I do,' answered the man.
“我当然总是赶这趟车，”那个人回答。
'I must be at work at 10 o'clock.
“我必须在10点钟上班。
My employer will confirm that I was there on time.'
我的雇主会证实我准时到了那里。”

'Would a later train get you to work on time?' asked the inspector.
“晚一点的火车能让你准时上班吗？”警官问道。
'I suppose it would, but I never catch a later train.'
“我想可以，但我从不赶晚一点的火车。”
'At what time did you arrive at the station?'
“你是什么时候到达车站的？”
'At ten to eight.
“7点50分。
I bought a paper and waited for the train.'
我买了份报纸，然后等火车。”
'And you didn't notice anything unusual?'
“你没有注意到有什么异常情况吗？”
'Of course not.'
“当然没有。”

'I suggest,' said the inspector, 'that you are not telling the truth.
“我认为，”警官说，“你没有说实话。
I suggest that you did not catch the 8 o'clock train, but that you caught the 8.25 which would still get you to work on time.
我认为你并没有赶8点钟的那趟火车，而是赶了8点25分的车，那趟车也同样能让你准时上班。
You see, on the morning of the murder, the 8 o'clock train did not run at all.
要知道，谋杀案发生的那天早上，8点钟的那趟火车根本就没有开。
It broke down at Ferngreen station and was taken off the line.'
它在芬格林车站出了故障，被停开了。”

## 语法要点解析 (Grammar & Key Structures)

### 1. 虚拟语气在宾语从句中的应用 (Subjunctive Mood)
* **经典原句**：*I suggest that you are not telling the truth.*
* **核心解析**：
当 `suggest` 表示“建议”时，其后的宾语从句通常使用虚拟语气（should + 动词原形，should 可省略）。但当 `suggest` 表示“暗示、表明”时，从句则使用陈述语气。
* **结构解析**：本句中 `suggest` 意为“指出/表明”，是对事实的陈述，故使用 `are not` 而非 `be not`。

### 2. 过去进行时 (Past Continuous Tense)
* **经典原句**：*At the time the murder was committed, I was travelling on the 8 o'clock train to London.*
* **核心解析**：
过去进行时用于描述在过去某一特定时间点正在进行的动作。
* **结构解析**：`was/were + doing`。本句中 `was travelling` 强调在“谋杀发生时”这一背景下，说话人正处于“旅行中”这一持续状态。

### 3. 被动语态 (Passive Voice)
* **经典原句**：*It broke down at Ferngreen station and was taken off the line.*
* **核心解析**：
`was taken off` 是被动语态，强调动作的承受者（火车）。
* **结构解析**：`be + 过去分词`。`take off the line` 是固定搭配，意为“撤出运营/停运”。

## 写作仿写与思辨口语 (Writing Practice & Critical Thinking)

### 1. ✍️ 提分句型仿写 (Pattern Drill)
* **句型名称**：It is [adj] to think that... (想到……真是……)
* **句型公式**：`It is + 形容词 + to think that + [从句]`
* **仿写练习 1**：想到他竟然能编造出如此完美的谎言，真是令人震惊。(It is shocking to think that he could make up such a perfect lie.)
* **仿写练习 2**：想到我们能在如此短的时间内完成任务，真是令人难以置信。(It is incredible to think that we could complete the task in such a short time.)

### 2. 🔍 近义词/易混词辨析 (Word Discrimination)
* **对比辨析**：**suggest vs. advise**
* **使用差异解析**：
`advise` 侧重于“给某人提供专业的建议或忠告”，通常带有“劝告”的意味，后面常接 `sb. to do sth.`；`suggest` 侧重于“提出一个想法、方案或可能性”，供对方参考，不带有强迫性，后面常接 `doing sth.` 或 `that` 从句。
* **地道表达搭配**：
* advise someone against doing something (劝阻某人做某事)
* suggest a solution (提出一个解决方案)

### 3. 💬 课后思辨讨论题 (Speaking & Writing Prompts)
* **Question 1**: Why is an "alibi" so crucial in a criminal investigation? Can you think of a situation where an innocent person might fail to provide an alibi?
* **Question 2**: If you were the inspector, what other questions would you ask the man to expose his lie?
* **高分表达提示 (Hint Words)**: *suspicious (可疑的), evidence (证据), cross-examination (交叉盘问), consistency (一致性), loophole (漏洞)*
