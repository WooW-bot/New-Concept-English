# Lesson 77: A Successful Operation

## 课文内容 (Text)

The mummy of an Egyptian woman who died in 800 B.C. has just had an operation.
一位死于公元前800年的埃及妇女的木乃伊刚刚接受了一次手术。
The mummy is that of Shepenmut who was once a singer in the Temple of Thebes.
这具木乃伊是谢彭穆特的，她曾是底比斯神庙里的一名歌手。
As there were strange marks on the X-ray plates taken of the mummy, doctors have been trying to find out whether the woman died of a rare disease.
由于在为木乃伊拍摄的X光片上有一些奇怪的痕迹，医生们一直试图查明这位妇女是否死于某种罕见的疾病。
The only way to do this was to operate.
要做到这一点的唯一方法就是进行手术。

The operation, which lasted for over four hours, proved to be very difficult because of the hard resin which covered the skin.
手术持续了四个多小时，由于覆盖在皮肤上的坚硬树脂，手术过程非常困难。
The doctors removed a section of the mummy and sent it to a laboratory.
医生们从木乃伊身上取下了一小块组织，并将其送往实验室。
They also found something which the X-ray plates did not show: a small wax figure of the god Duamutef.
他们还发现了一些X光片上没有显示的东西：一尊杜阿木特夫神的小蜡像。
This god, which has the head of a cow, was normally placed inside a mummy.
这位长着牛头的神通常被放置在木乃伊体内。

The doctors have not yet decided how the woman died.
医生们尚未确定这位妇女的死因。
They feared that the mummy would fall to pieces when they cut it open, but fortunately this has not happened.
他们曾担心在切开木乃伊时它会散架，但幸运的是这种情况并没有发生。
The mummy successfully survived the operation.
木乃伊成功地经受住了这次手术。

## 语法要点解析 (Grammar & Key Structures)

### 1. 定语从句 (Attributive Clause)
* **经典原句**：*The mummy of an Egyptian woman who died in 800 B.C. has just had an operation.*
* **核心解析**：
**who died in 800 B.C.** 是引导的限制性定语从句，修饰先行词 `woman`。
* **结构解析**：当先行词指人时，关系代词使用 `who`（作主语或宾语）。在本句中，`who` 在从句中作主语，指代这位埃及女性。

### 2. 过去进行时与现在完成时的对比 (Tense Usage)
* **经典原句**：*...doctors have been trying to find out whether the woman died of a rare disease.*
* **核心解析**：
**have been trying** 使用了现在完成进行时。
* **结构解析**：`have/has + been + v-ing`。该时态强调动作从过去开始，一直持续到现在，且可能还会继续。此处医生们“试图找出死因”这一动作具有持续性，因此使用现在完成进行时比现在完成时更强调过程感。

### 3. 宾语从句 (Object Clause)
* **经典原句**：*They feared that the mummy would fall to pieces when they cut it open...*
* **核心解析**：
**that the mummy would fall to pieces** 是 `feared` 的宾语从句。
* **结构解析**：当主句谓语动词为过去时（feared）时，从句谓语通常需要使用相应的过去形式（would fall），这体现了时态呼应（Sequence of Tenses）。

## 写作仿写与思辨口语 (Writing Practice & Critical Thinking)

### 1. ✍️ 提分句型仿写 (Pattern Drill)
* **句型名称**：...proved to be... (被证明是……)
* **句型公式**：`[主语] + proved to be + [形容词/名词]`
* **仿写练习 1**：这次实验被证明是非常成功的。 (The experiment proved to be very successful.)
* **仿写练习 2**：这个建议被证明是解决问题的唯一方法。 (The suggestion proved to be the only way to solve the problem.)

### 2. 🔍 近义词/易混词辨析 (Word Discrimination)
* **对比辨析**：**operate vs. operation**
* **使用差异解析**：
`operate` 是动词，指“操作、运转”或“动手术”。在医学语境下，常搭配 `operate on someone`（给某人动手术）。
`operation` 是名词，指“操作、行动”或“手术”。常搭配 `have an operation`（做手术）。
* **地道表达搭配**：
* operate a machine (操作机器)
* perform an operation (执行手术 - 医生视角)
* undergo an operation (经历手术 - 患者视角)

### 3. 💬 课后思辨讨论题 (Speaking & Writing Prompts)
* **Question 1**: Do you think it is ethical to perform operations on mummies, or should they be left undisturbed in their tombs?
* **Question 2**: If you were a scientist, what would you hope to discover by examining ancient artifacts?
* **高分表达提示 (Hint Words)**: *archaeology (考古学), ethical dilemma (伦理困境), historical preservation (历史保护), scientific breakthrough (科学突破), respect for the dead (对死者的尊重)*
