# Lesson 20: One Man in a Boat

## 课文内容 (Text)

Fishing is my favourite sport. I often fish for hours without catching anything. But this does not worry me. Some fishermen are unlucky. Instead of catching fish, they catch old boots and rubbish. I am even less lucky. I never catch anything -- not even old boots.

After having spent whole mornings on the river, I always go home with an empty bag. 'You must give up fishing!' my friends say. 'It's a waste of time.' But they don't realize one important thing.

I'm not really interested in fishing. I am only interested in sitting in a boat and doing nothing at all!

## 语法要点解析 (Grammar & Key Structures)

### 1. Instead of + V-ing 结构 (Instead of doing something)
*   **经典原句**：*Instead of catching fish, they catch old boots and rubbish.*
*   **核心解析**：
    `instead of` 是一个复合介词，意为“代替……，而不是……”。它后面可以接名词、代词或动名词（V-ing），表示在两种选择或行为中，选择后者而放弃前者。
*   **结构解析**：`Instead of + 名词/代词/动名词`。这个结构常用于句首或句中，作状语，强调替代性。

### 2. After having done 结构 (After having spent...)
*   **经典原句**：*After having spent whole mornings on the river, I always go home with an empty bag.*
*   **核心解析**：
    `After having done` 是一个完成时态的介词短语，表示“在做完某事之后”。它强调动作的先后顺序，即 `having done` 所表示的动作发生在主句动作之前，并且已经完成。
*   **结构解析**：`After + having + 过去分词`。这个结构通常用于句首，作时间状语，其逻辑主语与主句主语一致。它比 `After doing` 更强调动作的完成性。

### 3. be interested in doing something (对做某事感兴趣)
*   **经典原句**：*I'm not really interested in fishing. I am only interested in sitting in a boat and doing nothing at all!*
*   **核心解析**：
    `be interested in` 是一个固定搭配，表示“对……感兴趣”。`interested` 是一个形容词，用来描述人的感受。`in` 是介词，后面可以接名词或动名词（V-ing）。
*   **结构解析**：`be + interested + in + 名词/动名词`。这个结构常用来表达个人爱好、兴趣或关注点。

## 写作仿写与思辨口语 (Writing Practice & Critical Thinking)

### 1. ✍️ 提分句型仿写 (Pattern Drill)
*   **句型名称**：It's a waste of time (to do something) (做某事是浪费时间)
*   **句型公式**：`It's a waste of time + (to do something / doing something)`
*   **仿写练习 1**：每天花几个小时玩手机是浪费时间。 (It's a waste of time to spend hours on your phone every day.)
*   **仿写练习 2**：争论那些不重要的小事是浪费时间。 (It's a waste of time arguing about trivial matters.)

### 2. 🔍 近义词/易混词辨析 (Word Discrimination)
*   **对比辨析**：**catch vs. capture**
*   **使用差异解析**：
    `catch` 是一个更常用、更广泛的词，通常指通过追逐、陷阱或偶然地“抓住”移动中的物体或人，或指“感染”疾病。它可能不涉及太多策略或武力。
    `capture` 则更正式、更强调“通过努力、策略、武力或技巧成功地捕获或占有”。它常用于军事、犯罪、摄影、记录或引起注意等语境。
*   **地道表达搭配**：
    *   **catch**: catch a ball (接球), catch a cold (感冒), catch a bus (赶公交), catch a thief (抓住小偷)
    *   **capture**: capture a city (攻占城市), capture an image (捕捉影像), capture attention (吸引注意力), capture a moment (定格瞬间)

### 3. 💬 课后思辨讨论题 (Speaking & Writing Prompts)
*   **Question 1**: Do you have a hobby that others might consider a "waste of time"? How would you explain its value or benefits to them?
*   **Question 2**: The author enjoys "doing nothing at all." What are the potential benefits of doing nothing, and how can one truly relax and find peace in today's busy and demanding world?
*   **高分表达提示 (Hint Words)**: *leisure (休闲), relaxation (放松), mental well-being (心理健康), productivity (生产力), mindfulness (正念), escape (逃离), recharge (充电), introspection (内省)*
