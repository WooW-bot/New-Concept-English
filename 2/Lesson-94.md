# Lesson 94: Future Champions

## 课文内容 (Text)

Experiments have proved that children can be instructed in swimming at a very early age. At a special swimming pool in Los Angeles, children become expert at holding their breath under water even before they can walk. Babies of two months old do not appear to be reluctant to enter the water.

It is not long before they are so accustomed to swimming that they can pick up weights from the floor of the pool. A game that is very popular with these young swimmers is the underwater tricycle race. Tricycles are lined up on the floor of the pool seven feet under water. The children compete against each other to reach the other end of the pool.

Many pedal their tricycles but most of them prefer to push or drag them. Some children can cover the whole length of the pool without coming up for breath even once. Whether they will ever become future Olympic champions, only time will tell. Meanwhile, they should encourage those among us who cannot swim five yards before they are gasping for air.

## 语法要点解析 (Grammar & Key Structures)

### 1. It is not long before... (不久之后就……)
* **经典原句**：*It is not long before they are so accustomed to swimming that they can pick up weights...*
* **核心解析**：
这是一个固定句型，表示“没过多久就……”。
* **结构解析**：`It is not long before + 从句`。注意 `before` 引导的从句通常使用一般现在时或一般过去时，表示时间上的先后关系。如果强调时间很短，还可以用 `It won't be long before...`（不久之后将……）。

### 2. so... that... 结构 (如此……以至于……)
* **经典原句**：*...they are so accustomed to swimming that they can pick up weights from the floor of the pool.*
* **核心解析**：
`so + adj./adv. + that + 从句` 是表示程度的常用结构，意为“如此……以至于……”。
* **结构解析**：`so` 修饰形容词 `accustomed`，`that` 引导结果状语从句。该结构常用于强调某种状态达到了某种程度，从而导致了特定的结果。

### 3. Whether... (引导主语从句)
* **经典原句**：*Whether they will ever become future Olympic champions, only time will tell.*
* **核心解析**：
`Whether` 引导主语从句，在句中作 `tell` 的宾语（此处语序倒装，正常语序为：Only time will tell whether they will ever become...）。
* **结构解析**：`Whether` 意为“是否”，引导从句时，从句在主句中充当主语、宾语或表语。在 `tell`, `know`, `decide` 等动词后，`whether` 引导的从句常表示不确定性。

## 写作仿写与思辨口语 (Writing Practice & Critical Thinking)

### 1. ✍️ 提分句型仿写 (Pattern Drill)
* **句型名称**：It is not long before... (没过多久就……)
* **句型公式**：`It is not long before + [主语] + [谓语]`
* **仿写练习 1**：没过多久，他就适应了新的工作环境。 (It is not long before he gets accustomed to the new working environment.)
* **仿写练习 2**：没过多久，春天就来到了，万物复苏。 (It is not long before spring arrives and everything comes back to life.)

### 2. 🔍 近义词/易混词辨析 (Word Discrimination)
* **对比辨析**：**compete against vs. compete for**
* **使用差异解析**：
`compete against` 后面接**人或对手**，强调竞争的“对抗性”；`compete for` 后面接**目标或奖项**，强调竞争的“目的性”。
* **地道表达搭配**：
* compete against the clock (与时间赛跑)
* compete for the championship (争夺冠军)
* compete in a competition (参加比赛)

### 3. 💬 课后思辨讨论题 (Speaking & Writing Prompts)
* **Question 1**: Do you think it is necessary for children to learn skills like swimming at such an early age? What are the pros and cons?
* **Question 2**: "Only time will tell." Do you believe that talent is innate, or can it be cultivated through early training?
* **高分表达提示 (Hint Words)**: *early childhood education (幼儿教育), physical development (身体发育), potential (潜力), innate talent (天赋), perseverance (毅力), water safety (水上安全)*
