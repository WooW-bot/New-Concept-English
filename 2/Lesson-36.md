# Lesson 36: Across the Channel

## 课文内容 (Text)

Debbie Hart is going to swim across the English Channel tomorrow. She is going to set out from the French coast at five o'clock in the morning. Debbie is only eleven years old and she hopes to set up a new world record. She is a strong swimmer and many people feel that she is sure to succeed.

Debbie's father will set out with her in a small boat. Mr. Hart has trained his daughter for years. Tomorrow he will be watching her anxiously as she swims the long distance to England. Debbie intends to take short rests every two hours. She will have something to drink but she will not eat any solid food.

Most of Debbie's school friends will be waiting for her on the English coast. Among them will be Debbie's mother, who swam the Channel herself when she was a girl.

## 语法要点解析 (Grammar & Key Structures)

### 1. 将来进行时 (Future Continuous Tense)
* **经典原句**：*Tomorrow he will be watching her anxiously as she swims the long distance to England.* / *Most of Debbie's school friends will be waiting for her on the English coast.*
* **核心解析**：
**will be watching** 和 **will be waiting** 均为将来进行时。该时态用来表示在将来某一特定时刻或某段时间内正在进行的动作。在本文中，它生动地描绘了明天 Debbie 游泳时，父亲“正焦急地看着她”以及朋友们“正等候着她”的画面，比一般将来时更具画面感和人情味。
* **结构解析**：`will be + 现在分词 (doing)`。常与表示将来的时间状语（如 tomorrow, this time next week）连用。

### 2. 介词短语提前引起的完全倒装 (Full Inversion)
* **经典原句**：*Among them will be Debbie's mother, who swam the Channel herself when she was a girl.*
* **核心解析**：
这是一个典型的完全倒装句。为了使句子结构平衡（避免头重脚轻），或者为了承上启下，将表示地点的介词短语 **Among them**（在他们中间）放到了句首，从而将谓语动词 **will be** 移到了主语 **Debbie's mother** 之前。
* **结构解析**：`介词短语 + 动词 (be/come/go等) + 主语`。注意：当主语是代词时，不进行倒装（例如：Among them she will be. ❌ 错误，代词作主语时不倒装）。

### 3. "be sure to" 结构表必然性 (Expressing Certainty)
* **经典原句**：*...many people feel that she is sure to succeed.*
* **核心解析**：
**be sure to do** 表示说话人对某事发生的必然性有着极强的信心，意为“一定会……”、“必然会……”。
* **结构解析**：`Subject + be sure + to-infinitive`。与 `be bound to` 意思相近，但 `be sure to` 侧重于主观上的确信。

## 写作仿写与思辨口语 (Writing Practice & Critical Thinking)

### 1. ✍️ 提分句型仿写 (Pattern Drill)
* **句型名称**：Among them will be... (在他们当中将会有…… —— 用于场景描写或名单列举)
* **句型公式**：`Among them will be + [主语] + [定语从句/同位语]`
* **仿写练习 1**：在这些志愿者当中将会有李教授，他曾多次为贫困学校捐款。 (Among them will be Professor Li, who has donated to poor schools many times.)
* **仿写练习 2**：在这些获奖者当中将会有那位年轻的科学家，她去年发明了一种新型净水设备。 (Among them will be the young scientist, who invented a new water purification device last year.)

### 2. 🔍 近义词/易混词辨析 (Word Discrimination)
* **对比辨析**：**set out vs. set up**
* **使用差异解析**：
* `set out` 侧重于“出发，启程”（常与 from/for 连用），或者“着手，开始做某事”（set out to do sth.）。
* `set up` 侧重于“建立，创立，设立”（如建立公司、设立记录、搭建帐篷等）。
* **地道表达搭配**：
* **set out** for London (启程去伦敦)
* **set out** to write a novel (着手写一部小说)
* **set up** a business (创业)
* **set up** a new world record (创立新的世界纪录)

### 3. 💬 课后思辨讨论题 (Speaking & Writing Prompts)
* **Question 1**: Debbie Hart is only eleven years old. Do you think it is appropriate for such a young child to undertake such extreme physical challenges? Why or why not?
* **Question 2**: If you were to set a world record, what field or activity would you choose, and what kind of preparation would you need?
* **高分表达提示 (Hint Words)**: *extreme challenge (极端挑战), physical and mental health (身心健康), intensive training (高强度训练), determination (决心), push one's limits (挑战极限), sense of achievement (成就感)*
