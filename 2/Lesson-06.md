# Lesson 06: Percy Buttons

## 课文内容 (Text)

I have just moved to a house in Bridge Street. Yesterday a beggar knocked at my door. He asked me for a meal and a glass of beer. In return for this, the beggar stood on his head and sang songs. I gave him a meal. He ate the food and drank the beer. Then he put a piece of cheese in his pocket and went away.

Later a neighbour told me about him. Everybody knows him. His name is Percy Buttons. He calls at every house in the street once a month and always asks for a meal and a glass of beer.

## 语法要点解析 (Grammar & Key Structures)

### 1. 现在完成时 (Present Perfect Simple)

*   **经典原句**：`I have just moved to a house in Bridge Street.`
*   **核心解析**：现在完成时表示一个刚刚完成的动作，其结果对现在有影响。这里的 `just` 强调动作发生的时间非常近。
*   **结构解析**：`主语 + have/has + 动词的过去分词`。常与 `just, already, yet, ever, never, recently` 等时间副词连用。

### 2. 动词短语 "knock at/on" (敲击)

*   **经典原句**：`Yesterday a beggar knocked at my door.`
*   **核心解析**：`knock at/on` 意为“敲（门、窗等）”。`at` 和 `on` 都可以使用，`on` 更为常见，强调敲击的表面；`at` 则更强调敲击的地点。
*   **结构解析**：`knock + at/on + 某物`。例如：`knock on the door` (敲门), `knock at the window` (敲窗)。

### 3. 短语 "in return for" (作为回报/交换)

*   **经典原句**：`In return for this, the beggar stood on his head and sang songs.`
*   **核心解析**：`in return for sth.` 是一个常用介词短语，表示“作为对某事的回报、交换或补偿”。
*   **结构解析**：`In return for + 名词/代词/动名词`。这个短语可以放在句首、句中或句末。

## 写作仿写与思辨口语 (Writing Practice & Critical Thinking)

### 1. ✍️ 提分句型仿写 (Pattern Drill)

*   **句型名称**：I have just done sth. (我刚刚做了某事)
*   **句型公式**：`主语 + have/has just + [动词过去分词] + [其他成分]`
*   **仿写练习 1**：我刚刚读完了一本非常有趣的书。 (I have just finished reading a very interesting book.)
*   **仿写练习 2**：他刚刚告诉我一个好消息。 (He has just told me some good news.)

### 2. 🔍 近义词/易混词辨析 (Word Discrimination)

*   **对比辨析**：**ask for vs. demand**
*   **使用差异解析**：
    `ask for` 语气更委婉，表示“请求，索要”，通常是礼貌的或带有请求的意味。
    `demand` 语气更强硬，表示“要求，命令”，带有不容拒绝、强制性的意味，通常用于表达权利或强烈不满。
*   **地道表达搭配**：
    *   ask for help (寻求帮助)
    *   ask for directions (问路)
    *   demand an explanation (要求解释)
    *   demand attention (要求关注)

### 3. 💬 课后思辨讨论题 (Speaking & Writing Prompts)

*   **Question 1**: What do you think about Percy Buttons' way of earning a meal? Is it a fair exchange, or is he taking advantage of people's kindness?
*   **Question 2**: Have you ever encountered a unique or memorable person like Percy Buttons in your life? Describe your experience and what made them stand out.
*   **高分表达提示 (Hint Words)**: *begging (乞讨), entertainment (娱乐), self-sufficiency (自给自足), generosity (慷慨), memorable (难忘的), unique (独特的), community (社区), social norms (社会规范)*
