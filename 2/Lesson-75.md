# Lesson 75: SOS

## 课文内容 (Text)

When a light passenger plane flew off course some time ago, it crashed in the mountains and its pilot was killed.
不久前，一架轻型客机偏离了航向，坠毁在山区，飞行员遇难。
The only passengers, a young woman and her two baby daughters, were unhurt.
机上仅有的乘客——一位年轻女子和她的两个幼女——没有受伤。
It was the middle of winter.
当时正值隆冬。
Snow lay thick on the ground.
地上积着厚厚的雪。
The woman knew that the nearest village was miles away.
这位女子知道，最近的村庄也在几英里之外。

When it grew dark, she turned a suitcase into a bed and put the children inside it, covering them with all the clothes she could find.
天黑下来时，她把一只手提箱当作床，把孩子们放进去，并用她能找到的所有衣服盖在她们身上。
During the night, it got terribly cold.
夜里，天气变得异常寒冷。
The woman kept as near as she could to the children and even tried to get into the case herself, but it was too small.
这位女子尽可能地靠近孩子们，甚至试着自己也钻进箱子里，但箱子太小了。

Early next morning, she heard planes passing overhead and wondered how she could send a signal.
第二天清晨，她听到头顶上有飞机飞过，便琢磨着怎样才能发出求救信号。
Then she had an idea.
接着，她想出了一个主意。
She stamped out the letters 'SOS' in the snow.
她在雪地上踩出了“SOS”这几个字母。
Fortunately, a pilot saw the signal and sent a message by radio to the nearest town.
幸运的是，一位飞行员看到了这个信号，并通过无线电向最近的城镇发出了信息。
It was not long before a helicopter arrived on the scene to rescue the survivors of the plane crash.
没过多久，一架直升机就赶到了现场，营救了空难的幸存者。

| **passenger** | `/ˈpæsɪndʒə(r)/` | n. 乘客 | 源自古法语 'passager'，由 'passage'（经过，通过）演变而来，意为“过路人”。 |

## 语法要点解析 (Grammar & Key Structures)

### 1. 时间状语从句 (Adverbial Clause of Time)
* **经典原句**：*When a light passenger plane flew off course some time ago, it crashed in the mountains...*
* **核心解析**：
由 `when` 引导的时间状语从句，用于说明主句动作发生的时间背景。
* **结构解析**：`When + 主语 + 谓语`。在叙事性文章中，`when` 引导的从句常用于描述一系列连续发生的动作或背景情况。

### 2. 动词不定式作目的状语 (Infinitive of Purpose)
* **经典原句**：*...she heard planes passing overhead and wondered how she could send a signal.*
* **核心解析**：
`to send a signal` 在此处作目的状语，解释“wondered”（想知道）的内容或目的。
* **结构解析**：`to + 动词原形`。不定式常用来表示动作的目的，回答“为什么做某事”的问题。

### 3. It is/was not long before... (不久之后就...)
* **经典原句**：*It was not long before a helicopter arrived on the scene...*
* **核心解析**：
这是一个非常地道的句型，表示“没过多久就……”。
* **结构解析**：`It + be + 时间段 + before + 从句`。注意 `before` 引导的从句中谓语动词通常使用一般过去时。

## 写作仿写与思辨口语 (Writing Practice & Critical Thinking)

### 1. ✍️ 提分句型仿写 (Pattern Drill)
* **句型名称**：It was not long before... (没过多久就……)
* **句型公式**：`It was not long before + [主语] + [动词过去式]`
* **仿写练习 1**：没过多久，大雨就停了，太阳从云层中露了出来。(It was not long before the heavy rain stopped and the sun came out from behind the clouds.)
* **仿写练习 2**：没过多久，他就意识到自己犯了一个严重的错误。(It was not long before he realized that he had made a serious mistake.)

### 2. 🔍 近义词/易混词辨析 (Word Discrimination)
* **对比辨析**：**survivor vs. victim**
* **使用差异解析**：
`survivor` 强调在灾难、事故或疾病中“活下来的人”，侧重于生命力的延续；而 `victim` 侧重于在灾难或犯罪中“遭受伤害、损失或死亡的人”。
* **地道表达搭配**：
* the sole survivor (唯一的幸存者)
* a victim of the earthquake (地震的受害者)
* fall victim to... (成为……的受害者)

### 3. 💬 课后思辨讨论题 (Speaking & Writing Prompts)
* **Question 1**: If you were stranded in a remote area, what three items would you hope to have in your suitcase to help you survive?
* **Question 2**: The woman in the story showed great courage. What qualities do you think are most important when facing a life-threatening emergency?
* **高分表达提示 (Hint Words)**: *resourcefulness (足智多谋), composure (镇定), survival instinct (生存本能), emergency kit (应急包), signal for help (发出求救信号)*
