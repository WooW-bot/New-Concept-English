# Lesson 45: A Clear Conscience

## 课文内容 (Text)

The whole village soon learnt that a large sum of money had been lost. Sam Benton, the local butcher, had lost his wallet while taking his savings to the post office. Sam was sure that the wallet must have been found by one of the villagers, but it was not returned to him.

Three months passed, and then one morning, Sam found his wallet outside his front door. It had been wrapped up in newspaper and it contained half the money he had lost, together with a note which said: 'A thief, yes, but only 50 per cent a thief!'

Two months later, some more money was sent to Sam with another note: 'Only 25 per cent a thief now!' In time, all Sam's money was paid back in this way. The last note said: 'I am 100 per cent honest now!'

## 语法要点解析 (Grammar & Key Structures)

### 1. 情态动词 + have been done 表对过去被动行为的肯定推测 (Must have been done)
* **经典原句**：*Sam was sure that the wallet must have been found by one of the villagers...*
* **核心解析**：
  `must have done` 表示对过去发生的事情进行可能性极大的肯定推测（意为“想必已经…… / 一定已经……”）。当推测的动作含有被动关系时，则需要使用被动语态结构 `must have been done`（意为“想必已经被…… / 一定已经被……”）。
* **结构解析**：`主语 + must + have + been + 过去分词`。在本句中，钱包（the wallet）与发现（find）之间是被动关系，且动作发生在过去，因此使用 `must have been found`。

### 2. 过去完成时被动语态 (Past Perfect Passive)
* **经典原句**：*The whole village soon learnt that a large sum of money had been lost.*
* **核心解析**：
  `had been lost` 是过去完成时的被动语态。
* **结构解析**：`had + been + 过去分词`。主句的谓语动词是 `learnt`（过去时），而“丢钱”（lose）这一动作发生在“村民知道”之前，即“过去的过去”，且“钱”与“丢失”是被动关系，故使用过去完成时的被动语态。

### 3. 时间状语从句中的省略结构 (Ellipsis in Adverbial Clause)
* **经典原句**：*Sam Benton, the local butcher, had lost his wallet while taking his savings to the post office.*
* **核心解析**：
  在以 `while`、`when`、`if`、`although` 等引导的状语从句中，如果从句的主语与主句的主语一致，且从句的谓语动词包含 `be` 动词，则可以省略“主语 + be 动词”。
* **结构解析**：原句完整的表达为：*...while he was taking his savings...*。由于从句主语 `he` 指代主句主语 `Sam Benton`，因此省去了 `he was`，直接保留现在分词短语 `taking...`，使句子显得更加紧凑、地道。

## 写作仿写与思辨口语 (Writing Practice & Critical Thinking)

### 1. ✍️ 提分句型仿写 (Pattern Drill)
* **句型名称**：...together with... (连同……一起，以及……)
* **句型公式**：`[主句/主语] + together with + [名词短语]`
* **仿写练习 1**：他寄给我一封道歉信，连同一张他亲手制作的卡片。 (He sent me a letter of apology, together with a handmade card.)
* **仿写练习 2**：警方找到了那辆丢失的自行车，连同藏在车座下的一些现金。 (The police found the missing bicycle, together with some cash hidden under the seat.)

### 2. 🔍 近义词/易混词辨析 (Word Discrimination)
* **对比辨析**：**steal vs. rob** (偷窃 与 抢劫)
* **使用差异解析**：
  * `steal` 侧重于“悄悄地拿走”，其宾语通常是**被偷的物品**（steal something from someone/some place）。
  * `rob` 侧重于“通过暴力、威胁或非法手段剥夺”，其宾语通常是**被抢的人或场所**（rob someone of something / rob a bank）。
* **地道表达搭配**：
  * *steal* a wallet (偷钱包) / *steal* a glance (偷看一眼)
  * *rob* a traveler (抢劫一位旅客) / *rob* a jewelry store (抢劫一家珠宝店)

### 3. 💬 课后思辨讨论题 (Speaking & Writing Prompts)
* **Question 1**: Why do you think the thief returned the money in installments (50%, then 25%, then the rest) instead of returning it all at once? What does this reveal about his psychological state?
* **Question 2**: Is a person who steals but eventually returns everything still considered a "thief"? How does society balance justice and forgiveness in such cases?
* **高分表达提示 (Hint Words)**: *guilt (内疚), psychological struggle (心理挣扎), redemption (救赎), installment (分期付款), legal responsibility (法律责任), clear conscience (问心无愧)*
