# Lesson 96: The Dead Return

## 课文内容 (Text)

A Festival for the Dead is held once a year in Japan. This festival is a cheerful occasion, for on this day, the dead are said to return to their homes and they are welcomed by the living. As they are expected to be hungry after their long journey, food is laid out for them. Specially-made lanterns are hung outside each house to help the dead to find their way.

All night long, people dance and sing. In the early morning the food that had been laid out for the dead is thrown into a river or into the sea as it is considered unlucky for anyone living to eat it. 

In towns that are near the sea, the tiny lanterns which had been hung in the streets the night before, are placed into the water when the festival is over. Thousands of lanterns slowly drift out to sea guiding the dead on their return journey to the other world. This is a moving spectacle, for crowds of people stand on the shore watching the lanterns drifting away until they can be seen no more.

## 语法要点解析 (Grammar & Key Structures)

### 1. 被动语态的多种时态 (Passive Voice in Various Tenses)
* **经典原句**：*A Festival for the Dead is held once a year... food is laid out for them... the food that had been laid out... is thrown into a river.*
* **核心解析**：
本文大量使用了被动语态，强调动作的承受者（如 Festival, food, lanterns）。
* **结构解析**：
1. **一般现在时被动**：`is/am/are + 过去分词` (is held, is laid out)。用于描述客观事实或规律。
2. **过去完成时被动**：`had been + 过去分词` (had been laid out, had been hung)。用于描述在过去某一动作之前已经完成的被动动作。

### 2. 过去分词作后置定语 (Past Participle as Post-modifier)
* **经典原句**：*...the tiny lanterns which had been hung in the streets the night before...*
* **核心解析**：
`which had been hung...` 是定语从句，修饰 `lanterns`。在英语中，当被修饰词与动词是被动关系时，也可以简化为过去分词短语作后置定语。
* **结构解析**：
例如：*the lanterns (which were) hung in the streets*。这种结构使句子更加紧凑，是高级写作中常用的句式简化手段。

### 3. 动词不定式作目的状语 (Infinitive of Purpose)
* **经典原句**：*Specially-made lanterns are hung outside each house to help the dead to find their way.*
* **核心解析**：
`to help the dead to find their way` 在句中作目的状语，解释“为什么要挂灯笼”。
* **结构解析**：
`to do` 结构表示动作的目的。本句中 `help` 后面接了双重不定式结构，即 `help sb. (to) do sth.`，其中的 `to` 可以省略。

## 写作仿写与思辨口语 (Writing Practice & Critical Thinking)

### 1. ✍️ 提分句型仿写 (Pattern Drill)
* **句型名称**：...is considered + adj./n. (被认为/被视为……)
* **句型公式**：`Subject + is/are considered + [adj./n./to be...]`
* **仿写练习 1**：在某些文化中，打破镜子被认为是不吉利的。 (Breaking a mirror is considered unlucky in some cultures.)
* **仿写练习 2**：这种古老的仪式被视为当地文化遗产的重要组成部分。 (This ancient ritual is considered an important part of the local cultural heritage.)

### 2. 🔍 近义词/易混词辨析 (Word Discrimination)
* **对比辨析**：**spectacle vs. scene**
* **使用差异解析**：
`scene` 指“场景、现场”，是一个中性词，可以指电影中的一幕，也可以指事故现场。而 `spectacle` 特指“壮观的、引人注目的景象”，通常带有视觉上的震撼力或表演性质。
* **地道表达搭配**：
* make a spectacle of oneself (出洋相/丢人现眼)
* a scene of the crime (犯罪现场)
* a moving spectacle (感人的壮观场面)

### 3. 💬 课后思辨讨论题 (Speaking & Writing Prompts)
* **Question 1**: Do you think it is important to maintain traditional festivals that honor the dead? Why?
* **Question 2**: If you could create a new festival for your community, what would it celebrate and how would people participate?
* **高分表达提示 (Hint Words)**: *commemorate (纪念), cultural identity (文化认同), ancestral roots (祖先根源), passing down traditions (传承传统), solemn yet cheerful (庄重而愉悦)*
