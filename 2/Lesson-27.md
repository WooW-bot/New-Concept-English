# Lesson 27: A Wet Night

## 课文内容 (Text)

Late in the afternoon, the boys put up their tent in the middle of a field. As soon as this was done, they cooked a meal over an open fire. They were all hungry and the food smelled good. After a wonderful meal, they told stories and sang songs by the campfire.

But some time later it began to rain. The boys felt tired so they put out the fire and crept into their tent. Their sleeping bags were warm and comfortable, so they all slept soundly.

In the middle of the night, two boys woke up and began shouting. The tent was full of water! They all leapt out of their sleeping bags and hurried outside. It was raining heavily and they found that a stream had formed in the field. The stream wound its way across the field and then flowed right under their tent!

## 语法要点解析 (Grammar & Key Structures)

### 1. 时间状语从句：As soon as... (一...就...)
*   **经典原句**：*As soon as this was done, they cooked a meal over an open fire.*
*   **核心解析**：`As soon as` 引导时间状语从句，表示主句的动作紧接着从句的动作发生，强调动作的即时性。
*   **结构解析**：`As soon as + 从句 (通常用一般过去时或过去完成时) + 主句 (一般过去时)`。注意，当主句和从句都发生在过去时，从句的动作通常是先发生的，但由于 `as soon as` 强调紧密连接，从句也常用一般过去时。

### 2. 过去完成时 (Past Perfect Tense)
*   **经典原句**：*...they found that a stream had formed in the field.*
*   **核心解析**：过去完成时 `had + 过去分词` 用于表示在过去某一时间点之前已经完成的动作或状态。它强调“过去的过去”。
*   **结构解析**：`had + 动词的过去分词`。在本句中，“小溪已经形成”发生在“他们发现”这一过去动作之前，因此使用过去完成时。

### 3. 动词 + 介词短语表示移动方向 (Verbs of Movement with Prepositions)
*   **经典原句**：*...they put out the fire and crept into their tent.* / *They all leapt out of their sleeping bags and hurried outside.*
*   **核心解析**：动词（如 `creep`, `leap`, `hurry`）与介词（如 `into`, `out of`, `across`, `under`）或副词（如 `outside`）结合，清晰地表达了动作的方向和方式。
*   **结构解析**：`动词 + 介词/副词 + (名词)`。这种结构生动地描述了物体或人物在空间中的移动轨迹。例如：`crept into` (悄悄进入), `leapt out of` (跳出), `wound its way across` (蜿蜒穿过), `flowed right under` (直接流到下面)。

## 写作仿写与思辨口语 (Writing Practice & Critical Thinking)

### 1. ✍️ 提分句型仿写 (Pattern Drill)
*   **句型名称**：As soon as... (一...就...)
*   **句型公式**：`As soon as + [从句], [主句]` (从句和主句时态通常一致，或从句先发生)
*   **仿写练习 1**：他一到家，就立刻打开了电视。 (As soon as he got home, he immediately turned on the TV.)
*   **仿写练习 2**：我们一听到警报声，就迅速跑出了大楼。 (As soon as we heard the alarm, we quickly ran out of the building.)

### 2. 🔍 近义词/易混词辨析 (Word Discrimination)
*   **对比辨析**：**creep vs. walk**
*   **使用差异解析**：
    `walk` 是最普通的动词，表示用脚行走，不带有特定的方式或目的。而 `creep` 强调“悄悄地、缓慢地、小心翼翼地移动”，通常是为了不被发现，或因为害怕、疲惫等原因。它暗示了一种低姿态或隐秘的移动方式。
*   **地道表达搭配**：
    *   creep up on someone (悄悄靠近某人)
    *   creep into one's mind (悄悄进入某人脑海)
    *   walk briskly (轻快地走)
    *   walk hand in hand (手牵手走)

### 3. 💬 课后思辨讨论题 (Speaking & Writing Prompts)
*   **Question 1**: Have you ever had an unexpected problem or emergency during a trip or outdoor activity? How did you deal with it?
*   **Question 2**: What are the most important things to prepare for a camping trip to ensure safety and comfort, especially considering potential bad weather?
*   **高分表达提示 (Hint Words)**: *unexpected (意想不到的), emergency (紧急情况), preparedness (准备), shelter (庇护所), waterproof (防水的), survival skills (生存技能), adaptability (适应能力)*
