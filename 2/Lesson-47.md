# Lesson 47: A Thirsty Ghost

## 课文内容 (Text)

A public house which was recently bought by Mr. Ian Thompson is up for sale.
伊恩·汤普森先生最近买下的一家酒吧又要出售了。
Mr. Thompson is going to sell it because it is haunted.
汤普森先生打算把它卖掉，因为这房子闹鬼。
He told me that he could not go to sleep one night because he heard a strange noise coming from the bar.
他告诉我，有一天晚上他无法入睡，因为他听到酒吧里传出一种奇怪的声音。

The next morning, he found that the doors had been blocked by chairs and the furniture had been moved.
第二天早晨，他发现门被椅子堵住了，家具也被挪动了。
Though Mr. Thompson had turned the lights off before he went to bed, they were on in the morning.
虽然汤普森先生睡觉前关了灯，但早晨灯却亮着。
He also said that he had found five empty whisky bottles which the ghost must have drunk the night before.
他还说他发现了5个空威士忌酒瓶，那一定是鬼魂前一天晚上喝掉的。

When I suggested that some villagers must have come in for a free drink, Mr. Thompson shook his head.
当我提出这一定是村里人进来喝了免费酒时，汤普森先生摇了摇头。
The villagers have told him that they will not accept the pub even if he gives it away.
村民们已经告诉他，即使他把酒吧白送给他们，他们也不会要。

## 语法要点解析 (Grammar & Key Structures)

### 1. 情态动词 + have done 表对过去事情的推测 (Must have done)
* **经典原句**：*...which the ghost must have drunk the night before.* / *When I suggested that some villagers must have come in for a free drink...*
* **核心解析**：
`must have + 过去分词` 用于对过去发生的事情进行肯定的推测，意为“想必已经 / 一定已经……”。
* **结构解析**：情态动词 `must` 加上动词的完成式 `have + 过去分词`。如果是否定推测（“过去不可能做过某事”），则不能用 *must not have done*，而必须使用 `can't have done` 或 `couldn't have done`。

### 2. 过去完成时在叙事中的应用 (Past Perfect Tense)
* **经典原句**：*The next morning, he found that the doors had been blocked by chairs and the furniture had been moved.*
* **核心解析**：
过去完成时（`had + done`）表示在过去某一时间或动作之前已经发生或完成了的动作，即“过去的过去”。
* **结构解析**：主句动词是过去式 `found`，而宾语从句中的动作“门被堵住”（`had been blocked`）和“家具被移动”（`had been moved`）都发生在“发现”（`found`）之前，因此必须使用过去完成时，且此处结合了被动语态（`had been + 过去分词`）。

### 3. 让步状语从句 (Concession Clause with "even if")
* **经典原句**：*The villagers have told him that they will not accept the pub even if he gives it away.*
* **核心解析**：
`even if` 引导让步状语从句，意为“即使，即便”，用来表示假设一种极端的情况，但主句的结果依然不会改变。
* **结构解析**：`even if + 从句 (一般现在时表将来), 主句 (will/would + 动词原形)`。在 `even if` 引导的让步状语从句中，如果主句使用将来时，从句通常用一般现在时表示将来（主将从现）。

## 写作仿写与思辨口语 (Writing Practice & Critical Thinking)

### 1. ✍️ 提分句型仿写 (Pattern Drill)
* **句型名称**：even if... (即使……也……)
* **句型公式**：`Subject + will not + [verb] + even if + [subject] + [verb in present tense]`
* **仿写练习 1**：即使他向我道歉，我也不会原谅他。 (I will not forgive him even if he apologizes to me.)
* **仿写练习 2**：即使明天下大雨，我们也会按时出发。 (We will set off on time even if it rains heavily tomorrow.)

### 2. 🔍 近义词/易混词辨析 (Word Discrimination)
* **对比辨析**：**accept vs. receive**
* **使用差异解析**：
`receive` 是一个客观动作，指“收到，接到”某物，不涉及主观上是否愿意接受。
`accept` 则强调主观上的“接受，同意，认可”，表示愿意收下或赞同。
* **地道表达搭配**：
* receive a letter / gift (收到一封信/一份礼物)
* accept an apology / invitation (接受道歉/邀请)
* accept a challenge (接受挑战)

### 3. 💬 课后思辨讨论题 (Speaking & Writing Prompts)
* **Question 1**: Do you believe in the existence of ghosts or supernatural phenomena? Why do you think stories about haunted houses are so popular worldwide?
* **Question 2**: If you were Mr. Thompson, would you try to find a scientific explanation for the strange events in the pub, or would you sell it immediately?
* **高分表达提示 (Hint Words)**: *supernatural (超自然的), rational explanation (合理的解释), prank (恶作剧), illusion (幻觉), skepticism (怀疑态度), curiosity (好奇心)*
