# Lesson 63: She Was Not Amused

## 课文内容 (Text)

Jeremy Hampden has a large circle of friends and is very popular at parties. Everybody admires him for his great sense of humour--everybody, that is, except his six-year-old daughter, Jenny.

Recently, one of Jeremy's closest friends asked him to make a speech at a wedding reception. This is the sort of thing that Jeremy loves. He prepared the speech carefully and went to the wedding with Jenny. He had included a large number of funny stories in the speech and, of course, it was a great success.

As soon as he had finished, Jenny told him she wanted to go home. Jeremy was a little disappointed by this but he did as his daughter asked. On the way home, he asked Jenny if she had enjoyed the speech. To his surprise, she said she hadn't. Jeremy asked her why this was so and she told him that she did not like to see so many people laughing at him!

## 重点词汇及词源背景 (Vocabulary & Etymology)

| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |

## 语法要点解析 (Grammar & Key Structures)

### 1. 时间状语从句 (Adverbial Clause of Time)
* **经典原句**：*As soon as he had finished, Jenny told him she wanted to go home.*
* **核心解析**：
**As soon as** 引导时间状语从句，表示“一……就……”。
* **结构解析**：当主句动词为过去时，从句通常使用过去完成时（had finished）来强调动作的先后顺序，即“演讲结束”这一动作发生在“Jenny提出回家”之前。

### 2. 宾语从句 (Object Clause)
* **经典原句**：*Jeremy asked her why this was so and she told him that she did not like to see so many people laughing at him!*
* **核心解析**：
本句包含两个宾语从句。第一个是 `why this was so`，作 `asked` 的宾语；第二个是 `that she did not like...`，作 `told` 的宾语。
* **结构解析**：宾语从句在陈述句中通常由 `that` 引导（可省略），在疑问句中由连接代词或连接副词（如 `why`, `how`, `what`）引导，且从句必须使用**陈述语序**（主语+谓语）。

### 3. 过去完成时 (Past Perfect)
* **经典原句**：*He had included a large number of funny stories in the speech...*
* **核心解析**：
**had included** 表示“过去的过去”。
* **结构解析**：`had + 过去分词`。在本文中，Jeremy“准备演讲并加入故事”的行为发生在“参加婚礼”这一过去动作之前，因此使用过去完成时来体现时间上的先后逻辑。

## 写作仿写与思辨口语 (Writing Practice & Critical Thinking)

### 1. ✍️ 提分句型仿写 (Pattern Drill)
* **句型名称**：As soon as... (一……就……)
* **句型公式**：`As soon as + [主语] + had done, [主语] + did...`
* **仿写练习 1**：他一做完作业，就跑出去踢球了。 (As soon as he had finished his homework, he ran out to play football.)
* **仿写练习 2**：我一听到那个好消息，就立刻给父母打了电话。 (As soon as I had heard the good news, I called my parents immediately.)

### 2. 🔍 近义词/易混词辨析 (Word Discrimination)
* **对比辨析**：**admire vs. appreciate**
* **使用差异解析**：
`admire` 侧重于对他人的品质、成就或外貌表示“钦佩、仰慕”，带有敬意；而 `appreciate` 侧重于对某事物的价值有深刻的认识，表示“欣赏、感激”，常用于艺术品、他人的帮助或某种处境。
* **地道表达搭配**：
* admire one's courage (钦佩某人的勇气)
* appreciate the beauty of nature (欣赏大自然的美)
* I would appreciate it if... (如果……我将不胜感激)

### 3. 💬 课后思辨讨论题 (Speaking & Writing Prompts)
* **Question 1**: Why do you think children often interpret adult social behaviors differently than we expect?
* **Question 2**: Jeremy thought his speech was a success, but Jenny felt sad. Is it important to consider the audience's perspective before we act?
* **高分表达提示 (Hint Words)**: *misunderstanding (误解), perspective (视角), social etiquette (社交礼仪), innocence (天真), emotional intelligence (情商), intention (意图)*
