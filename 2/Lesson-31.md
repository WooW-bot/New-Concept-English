# Lesson 31: Success Story

## 课文内容 (Text)

Yesterday afternoon Frank Hawkins was telling me about his experiences as a young man. Before he retired, Frank was the head of a very large business company, but as a boy he used to work in a small shop. It was his job to repair bicycles and at that time he used to work fourteen hours a day. He saved money for years and in 1958 he bought a small workshop of his own.

In his twenties Frank used to make spare parts for aeroplanes. At that time he had two helpers. In a few years the small workshop had become a large factory which employed seven hundred and twenty-eight people. Frank smiled when he remembered his hard early years and the long road to success.

He was still smiling when the door opened and his wife came in. She wanted him to repair their grandson's bicycle!

## 语法要点解析 (Grammar & Key Structures)

### 1. 过去常常做某事 (Used to do sth.)
* **经典原句**：*...but as a boy he used to work in a small shop.* / *In his twenties Frank used to make spare parts for aeroplanes.*
* **核心解析**：
  **used to do** 表示过去经常发生、而现在已经不再持续的动作或状态。
* **结构解析**：
  `used to + 动词原形`。
  * 否定形式通常为：*didn't use to do* 或 *used not to do*。
  * 疑问形式通常为：*Did you use to...?*。
  * **对比辨析**：注意不要与 `be/get used to doing sth.`（习惯于做某事）相混淆，后者中的 *to* 是介词，后接动名词或名词。

### 2. 过去完成时 (Past Perfect Tense)
* **经典原句**：*In a few years the small workshop had become a large factory...*
* **核心解析**：
  **had become** 为过去完成时，表示在过去某一特定时间或动作之前已经完成的动作，即“过去的过去”。
* **结构解析**：
  `had + 过去分词`。在本句中，作者叙述的基准时间是过去（Frank 讲述往事、退休等），而“小作坊变成大工厂”这一过程发生在 Frank 讲述这些往事之前，因此使用过去完成时。

### 3. "when" 引导的时间状语从句表示“就在那时，突然” (when as a coordinating conjunction)
* **经典原句**：*He was still smiling when the door opened and his wife came in.*
* **核心解析**：
  这里的 `when` 并非普通的时间状语从句引导词（译为“当……时”），而是并列连词，意为“就在那时，突然”（and suddenly at that moment）。它常与过去进行时或 *was/were about to do* 连用。
* **结构解析**：
  `was/were doing sth. + when + 过去时`。表示某事正在进行，突然被另一件意料之外的事情打断。

## 写作仿写与思辨口语 (Writing Practice & Critical Thinking)

### 1. ✍️ 提分句型仿写 (Pattern Drill)
* **句型名称**：used to do..., but now... (过去常常……，但现在……)
* **句型公式**：`Subject + used to + [动词原形], but now + [一般现在时/现在完成时从句]`
* **仿写练习 1**：他过去常常熬夜玩电脑游戏，但现在他养成了早睡早起的健康习惯。 (He used to stay up late playing computer games, but now he has developed a healthy habit of sleeping and waking up early.)
* **仿写练习 2**：这个小镇过去常常依靠传统农业，但现在它已经发展成了一个繁荣的技术中心。 (The town used to rely on traditional agriculture, but now it has grown into a thriving technology hub.)

### 2. 🔍 近义词/易混词辨析 (Word Discrimination)
* **对比辨析**：**job vs. work**
* **使用差异解析**：
  * `job` 是**可数名词**，通常指具体的、有报酬的“职业、工作、职位”或某项具体任务（如 *repairing bicycles was his job*）。
  * `work` 是**不可数名词**（作“著作、工厂”时除外），是更通用的词，指任何体力或脑力劳动，不一定有报酬。
* **地道表达搭配**：
  * apply for a job (申请一份工作)
  * do a good job (干得好)
  * go to work (去上班)
  * out of work (失业)

### 3. 💬 课后思辨讨论题 (Speaking & Writing Prompts)
* **Question 1**: Frank Hawkins achieved success through long hours of hard work and saving money. Do you think this traditional path to success is still practical for young people in the modern digital age? Why or why not?
* **Question 2**: At the end of the story, Frank's wife asks him to repair their grandson's bicycle. What does this humorous ending tell us about the relationship between professional success and family life?
* **高分表达提示 (Hint Words)**: *entrepreneurship (创业), diligence (勤勉), work-life balance (工作与生活平衡), humble beginnings (寒微的出身), technological disruption (技术变革), stay grounded (保持接地气/不忘本)*
