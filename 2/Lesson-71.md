# Lesson 71: A Famous Clock

## 课文内容 (Text)

When you visit London, one of the first things you will see is Big Ben, the famous clock which can be heard all over the world on the B.B.C. If the Houses of Parliament had not been burned down in 1834, the great clock would never have been erected.

Big Ben takes its name from Sir Benjamin Hall who was responsible for the making of the clock when the new Houses of Parliament were being built. It is not only of immense size, but is extremely accurate as well. Officials from Greenwich Observatory have the clock checked twice a day.

On the B.B.C. you can hear the clock when it is actually striking because microphones are connected to the clock tower. Big Ben has rarely gone wrong. Once, however, it failed to give the correct time. A painter who had been working on the tower hung a pot of paint on one of the hands and slowed it down!

| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |

| **accurate** | `/ˈækjərət/` | adj. 精确的 | 源自拉丁语 'accuratus'，由 ad-（去）+ curare（照料，关心）构成，意为“经过精心照料的”，引申为“准确无误的”。 |

## 语法要点解析 (Grammar & Key Structures)

### 1. 虚拟语气用于过去事实的假设 (Conditional Sentences - Past)
* **经典原句**：*If the Houses of Parliament had not been burned down in 1834, the great clock would never have been erected.*
* **核心解析**：
这是对过去事实的虚拟假设。因为国会大厦在1834年确实被烧毁了，所以这里用虚拟语气表示与过去事实相反的假设。
* **结构解析**：`If + 主语 + had done (过去完成时), 主语 + would/could/might + have done (完成式)`。

### 2. 不定代词与定语从句 (Relative Clauses)
* **经典原句**：*Big Ben takes its name from Sir Benjamin Hall who was responsible for the making of the clock...*
* **核心解析**：
**who was responsible for...** 是一个非限制性定语从句，修饰先行词 Sir Benjamin Hall。
* **结构解析**：关系代词 `who` 在从句中作主语，指代人。`be responsible for` 是固定搭配，意为“对……负责”。

### 3. Have + 宾语 + 过去分词 (Have something done)
* **经典原句**：*Officials from Greenwich Observatory have the clock checked twice a day.*
* **核心解析**：
表示“请/让别人做某事”，强调动作的结果而非执行者。
* **结构解析**：`have + sth. + done`。此处 `checked` 是过去分词，表示“时钟被检查”这一被动状态。

## 写作仿写与思辨口语 (Writing Practice & Critical Thinking)

### 1. ✍️ 提分句型仿写 (Pattern Drill)
* **句型名称**：Not only... but... as well (不仅……而且……)
* **句型公式**：`Not only + [形容词/动词/从句], but + [主语] + [谓语] + as well`
* **仿写练习 1**：这座图书馆不仅规模宏大，而且藏书极其丰富。 (The library is not only of immense size, but it is extremely rich in collection as well.)
* **仿写练习 2**：他不仅在学术上表现优异，在体育方面也同样出色。 (He is not only excellent in academics, but he is outstanding in sports as well.)

### 2. 🔍 近义词/易混词辨析 (Word Discrimination)
* **对比辨析**：**accurate vs. precise**
* **使用差异解析**：
`accurate` 侧重于“符合事实、没有错误”，强调结果的正确性（如：准确的时间、准确的报告）。
`precise` 侧重于“精确到细节、严谨”，强调程度的细致和规格的严苛（如：精确的测量、精确的仪器）。
* **地道表达搭配**：
* accurate information (准确的信息)
* precise measurement (精确的测量)

### 3. 💬 课后思辨讨论题 (Speaking & Writing Prompts)
* **Question 1**: Big Ben is a global symbol of London. Do you think historical landmarks should be preserved exactly as they are, or should they be modernized?
* **Question 2**: If you were in charge of a famous historical site, what is one thing you would do to ensure it remains "accurate" and well-maintained for future generations?
* **高分表达提示 (Hint Words)**: *landmark (地标), preservation (保护), maintenance (维护), historical significance (历史意义), modernization (现代化), heritage (遗产)*
