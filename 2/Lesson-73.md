# Lesson 73: The Record-Holder

## 课文内容 (Text)

Children who play truant from school are unimaginative. A quiet day's fishing, or eight hours in a cinema seeing the same film over and over again, is usually as far as they get. They have all been put to shame by a boy who, while playing truant, travelled 1,600 miles.

He hitchhiked to Dover and towards evening went into a boat to find somewhere to sleep. When he woke up next morning, he discovered that the boat had, in the meantime, travelled to Calais. No one noticed the boy as he crept off. From there, he hitchhiked to Paris in a lorry. The driver gave him a few biscuits and a cup of coffee and left him just outside the city.

The next car the boy stopped did not take him into the centre of Paris as he hoped it would, but to Perpignan on the French-Spanish border. There he was picked up by a policeman and sent back to England by the local authorities. He has surely set up a record for the thousands of children who dream of evading school.

## 语法要点解析 (Grammar & Key Structures)

### 1. 时间状语从句中的省略 (Ellipsis in Adverbial Clauses)
* **经典原句**：*...a boy who, while playing truant, travelled 1,600 miles.*
* **核心解析**：
在由 `while`, `when`, `if`, `though` 等引导的时间或条件状语从句中，如果从句的主语与主句主语一致，且从句谓语包含 `be` 动词，则可以省略主语和 `be` 动词。
* **结构解析**：`while + doing`（主动）或 `while + done`（被动）。此处 `while playing truant` 完整形式为 `while he was playing truant`。

### 2. 现在完成时 (Present Perfect Tense)
* **经典原句**：*He has surely set up a record for the thousands of children who dream of evading school.*
* **核心解析**：
**has set up** 使用了现在完成时，表示该动作（创下记录）发生在过去，但其影响一直持续到现在，且强调“记录”这一结果对当下的意义。
* **结构解析**：`have/has + 过去分词`。常与 `surely`, `already`, `just`, `yet` 等副词连用，强调动作的完成性或结果。

### 3. 不定式作目的状语 (Infinitive of Purpose)
* **经典原句**：*...went into a boat to find somewhere to sleep.*
* **核心解析**：
**to find somewhere to sleep** 是不定式短语作目的状语，说明去船上的目的。
* **结构解析**：`to + 动词原形`。此处 `to sleep` 是不定式作定语，修饰前面的不定代词 `somewhere`。

## 写作仿写与思辨口语 (Writing Practice & Critical Thinking)

### 1. ✍️ 提分句型仿写 (Pattern Drill)
* **句型名称**：...is usually as far as they get. (……通常就是他们能做到的极限了。)
* **句型公式**：`[某事/某范围] + is usually as far as + [主语] + get.`
* **仿写练习 1**：对于大多数初学者来说，学会简单的问候语就是他们能做到的极限了。(Learning simple greetings is usually as far as most beginners get.)
* **仿写练习 2**：对于这个项目，仅仅完成初步的调查就是我们目前能做到的极限了。(For this project, finishing the preliminary investigation is usually as far as we can get for now.)

### 2. 🔍 近义词/易混词辨析 (Word Discrimination)
* **对比辨析**：**evade vs. avoid**
* **使用差异解析**：
`avoid` 是通用词，指通过避开某事来防止其发生（如 avoid traffic, avoid mistakes）。`evade` 语气更强，通常带有“故意、狡猾地逃避”的意味，常用于逃避法律、税收、责任或困难的问题（如 evade taxes, evade the question）。
* **地道表达搭配**：
* avoid doing something (避免做某事)
* evade the law (逃避法律制裁)
* evade responsibility (推卸责任)

### 3. 💬 课后思辨讨论题 (Speaking & Writing Prompts)
* **Question 1**: Do you think the boy's 1,600-mile journey was an act of "bravery" or "irresponsibility"? Why?
* **Question 2**: If you were the boy's parent, how would you handle this situation once he returned home?
* **高分表达提示 (Hint Words)**: *reckless (鲁莽的), adventurous (冒险的), parental supervision (家长监管), consequences (后果), curiosity (好奇心), discipline (纪律/管教)*
