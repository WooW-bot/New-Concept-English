# Lesson 79: By Air

## 课文内容 (Text)

I used to travel by air a great deal when I was a boy.
我小时候经常乘飞机旅行。
My parents used to live in South America and I used to fly there from Europe in the holidays.
我的父母过去住在南美洲，假期里我常从欧洲飞往那里。
A flight attendant would take charge of me and I never had an unpleasant experience.
总会有空中小姐照顾我，我从未遇到过什么不愉快的事情。
I am used to travelling by air and only on one occasion have I ever felt frightened.
我已经习惯了乘飞机旅行，但只有一次我感到过害怕。

After taking off, we were flying low over the city and slowly gaining height, when the plane suddenly turned round and flew back to the airport.
飞机起飞后，在城市上空低空飞行并缓慢爬高，这时飞机突然调转机头，飞回了机场。
While we were waiting to land, a flight attendant told us to keep calm and to get off the plane quietly as soon as it had touched down.
在我们等待降落时，一位空中小姐告诉我们要保持镇静，飞机一着陆就安静地撤离。
Everybody on board was worried and we were curious to find out what had happened.
机上的每个人都很担心，我们都急于想知道究竟发生了什么事。

Later we learnt that there was a very important person on board.
后来我们得知，机上有一位非常重要的人物。
The police had been told that a bomb had been planted on the plane.
有人向警方报告说，飞机上被安放了炸弹。
After we had landed, the plane was searched thoroughly.
飞机降落后，被彻底搜查了一遍。
Fortunately, nothing was found and five hours later we were able to take off again.
幸运的是，什么也没发现，5小时后我们又重新起飞了。

## 语法要点解析 (Grammar & Key Structures)

### 1. Used to do 与 be used to doing 的辨析
* **经典原句**：*I used to travel by air a great deal when I was a boy. / I am used to travelling by air...*
* **核心解析**：
`used to do` 表示“过去常常做某事”，强调过去的状态或习惯，现在已不再发生；`be used to doing` 表示“习惯于做某事”，强调目前已经适应了某种状态。
* **结构解析**：
1. `used to + 动词原形`：仅用于过去时。
2. `be (am/is/are/get) used to + 名词/动名词`：表示习惯，`used` 在此为形容词。

### 2. 过去完成时 (Past Perfect Tense)
* **经典原句**：*The police had been told that a bomb had been planted on the plane.*
* **核心解析**：
`had been told` 和 `had been planted` 均使用了过去完成时的被动语态。
* **结构解析**：
`had + been + 过去分词`。用于表示在过去某一时间点（landed）之前已经发生的动作，即“过去的过去”。在本句中，警方接到通知和炸弹被放置这两个动作都发生在飞机降落之前。

### 3. 过去进行时与一般过去时的结合
* **经典原句**：*While we were waiting to land, a flight attendant told us...*
* **核心解析**：
用 `while` 引导的过去进行时（were waiting）表示一个较长的背景动作，而一般过去时（told）表示在该背景下发生的一个短暂动作。
* **结构解析**：
`was/were + doing` (背景) + `did` (突发事件)。这种结构常用于叙事，增加故事的画面感和紧迫感。

## 写作仿写与思辨口语 (Writing Practice & Critical Thinking)

### 1. ✍️ 提分句型仿写 (Pattern Drill)
* **句型名称**：...as soon as... (一……就……)
* **句型公式**：`[主句] + as soon as + [从句(一般过去时/过去完成时)]`
* **仿写练习 1**：老师一走进教室，学生们就安静了下来。(The students kept quiet as soon as the teacher entered the classroom.)
* **仿写练习 2**：我一收到那封信，就立刻给朋友打了电话。(I called my friend as soon as I had received the letter.)

### 2. 🔍 近义词/易混词辨析 (Word Discrimination)
* **对比辨析**：**take charge of vs. take care of**
* **使用差异解析**：
`take charge of` 强调“负责、掌管、控制”，侧重于权力和责任的行使；`take care of` 强调“照顾、照料、处理”，侧重于关心、保护或解决问题。
* **地道表达搭配**：
* take charge of the project (负责项目)
* take charge of the situation (控制局面)
* take care of the children (照顾孩子)
* take care of the bill (付账/处理账单)

### 3. 💬 课后思辨讨论题 (Speaking & Writing Prompts)
* **Question 1**: How do you usually handle anxiety or fear when traveling? Do you think modern security measures (like airport checks) make you feel safer or more stressed?
* **Question 2**: If you were in a situation where you were told there was a potential danger on your flight, what is the most important thing to keep in mind?
* **高分表达提示 (Hint Words)**: *security check (安检), panic (恐慌), keep calm (保持冷静), emergency landing (紧急降落), precautionary measures (预防措施)*
