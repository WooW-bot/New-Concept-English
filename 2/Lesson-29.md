# Lesson 29: Taxi

## 课文内容 (Text)

Captain Ben Fawcett has bought an unusual taxi and has begun a new service. The 'taxi' is a small Swiss aeroplane called a 'Pilatus Porter'. This wonderful plane can carry seven passengers. The most surprising thing about it, however, is that it can land anywhere: on snow, water, or even on a ploughed field.

Captain Fawcett's first passenger was a doctor who flew from Birmingham to a lonely village in the Welsh mountains. Since then, Captain Fawcett has flown passengers to many unusual places. Once he landed on the roof of a block of flats and on another occasion, he landed in a deserted car park.

Captain Fawcett has just refused a strange request from a businessman. The man wanted to fly to Rockall, a lonely island in the Atlantic Ocean, but Captain Fawcett did not take him because the trip was too dangerous.

## 语法要点解析 (Grammar & Key Structures)

### 1. 现在完成时 (Present Perfect Tense)
* **经典原句**：*Captain Ben Fawcett has bought an unusual taxi and has begun a new service.* / *Since then, Captain Fawcett has flown passengers to many unusual places.*
* **核心解析**：
本课多次使用现在完成时（*has bought, has begun, has flown, has refused*）。现在完成时主要有两种用法：
1. 表示过去发生的动作对现在产生的影响或结果（如买下了飞机，现在开始运营服务）。
2. 表示从过去某时开始一直延续到现在的动作或状态，常与时间状语 *since then*（从那时起）或 *for* 引导的时间段连用。
* **结构解析**：`主语 + have/has + 过去分词 (done)`。

### 2. 同位语 (Apposition)
* **经典原句**：*The man wanted to fly to Rockall, a lonely island in the Atlantic Ocean...*
* **核心解析**：
**a lonely island in the Atlantic Ocean** 是名词短语，作为地名 **Rockall** 的同位语。
* **结构解析**：同位语紧跟在被说明的名词或代词后面，起解释、补充说明的作用，在句法上与被说明的词地位相同。使用同位语可以避免使用繁琐的定语从句（如 *which is a lonely island...*），使行文更加紧凑、地道。

### 3. 定语从句 (Attributive Clause)
* **经典原句**：*Captain Fawcett's first passenger was a doctor who flew from Birmingham to a lonely village in the Welsh mountains.*
* **核心解析**：
**who flew from Birmingham...** 是由关系代词 `who` 引导的限制性定语从句，修饰先行词 `a doctor`。
* **结构解析**：当先行词指人，且关系代词在定语从句中作主语时，通常使用 `who`（口语或非正式文体中也可用 `that`）。

## 写作仿写与思辨口语 (Writing Practice & Critical Thinking)

### 1. ✍️ 提分句型仿写 (Pattern Drill)
* **句型名称**：The most surprising thing about..., however, is that... (然而，关于……最令人吃惊的是……)
* **句型公式**：`The most surprising/interesting/exciting thing about + [名词/代词], however, is that + [从句]`
* **仿写练习 1**：然而，关于这个新软件最令人吃惊的是，它能自动翻译十几种语言。 (The most surprising thing about this new software, however, is that it can automatically translate over a dozen languages.)
* **仿写练习 2**：然而，关于这位年轻作家最有趣的是，他从未接受过任何正规的写作训练。 (The most interesting thing about this young writer, however, is that he has never received any formal training in writing.)

### 2. 🔍 近义词/易混词辨析 (Word Discrimination)
* **对比辨析**：**refuse vs. decline**
* **使用差异解析**：
  * `refuse` 语气较强，表示坚决、直接地“拒绝”某项请求、提议或邀请，有时带有不客气或不妥协的态度。后接名词或动词不定式（*refuse to do something*）。
  * `decline` 语气较委婉、礼貌，常用于正式场合，表示“谢绝”邀请、帮助或提议。
* **地道表达搭配**：
  * refuse an offer (拒绝提议) / refuse to cooperate (拒绝合作)
  * decline an invitation (谢绝邀请) / decline to comment (谢绝评论/拒绝置评)

### 3. 💬 课后思辨讨论题 (Speaking & Writing Prompts)
* **Question 1**: If you had the chance, would you like to travel in Captain Fawcett's "Pilatus Porter" taxi? Why or why not?
* **Question 2**: Captain Fawcett refused the businessman's request because the trip was too dangerous. In business, do you think safety should always come before profit?
* **高分表达提示 (Hint Words)**: *thrilling experience (刺激的体验), safety precautions (安全防范措施), risk assessment (风险评估), prioritize safety (安全第一), commercial profit (商业利润)*
