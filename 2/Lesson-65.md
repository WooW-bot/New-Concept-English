# Lesson 65: Jumbo Versus the Police

## 课文内容 (Text)

Last Christmas, the circus owner, Jimmy Gates, decided to take some presents to a children's hospital. Dressed up as Father Christmas and accompanied by a 'guard of honour' of six pretty girls, he set off down the main street of the city riding a baby elephant called Jumbo.

He should have known that the police would never allow this sort of thing. A policeman approached Jimmy and told him he ought to have gone along a side street as Jumbo was holding up the traffic. Though Jimmy agreed to go at once, Jumbo refused to move. Fifteen policemen had to push very hard to get him off the main street.

The police had a difficult time, but they were most amused. 'Jumbo must weigh a few tons,' said a policeman afterwards, 'so it was fortunate that we didn't have to carry him. Of course, we should arrest him, but as he has a good record, we shall let him off this time.'

## 语法要点解析 (Grammar & Key Structures)

### 1. 情态动词 + have done 表对过去行为的责备或建议 (Should/Ought to have done)
* **经典原句**：*He should have known that the police would never allow this sort of thing.* / *...he ought to have gone along a side street...*
* **核心解析**：
**should have done** 和 **ought to have done** 用于表示“本应该做某事，但实际上没做”，带有责备、遗憾或后悔的语气。
* **结构解析**：`should/ought to + have + 过去分词`。在否定句中（shouldn't have done），表示“本不该做某事，但实际上做了”。

### 2. 过去进行时 (Past Continuous Tense)
* **经典原句**：*...as Jumbo was holding up the traffic.*
* **核心解析**：
**was holding up** 表示在过去某一时刻或某一段时间内正在进行的动作。
* **结构解析**：`was/were + v-ing`。在本句中，它描述了导致警察介入的背景情况，即 Jumbo 当时正在阻碍交通这一持续状态。

### 3. 动词短语 let off (宽恕/放过)
* **经典原句**：*...we shall let him off this time.*
* **核心解析**：
**let off** 在此意为“从轻发落、宽恕、免除惩罚”。
* **结构解析**：这是一个典型的动词+副词短语，宾语为代词时必须放在中间（let him off），不能说 let off him。

## 写作仿写与思辨口语 (Writing Practice & Critical Thinking)

### 1. ✍️ 提分句型仿写 (Pattern Drill)
* **句型名称**：...should have done (本应该做某事)
* **句型公式**：`Subject + should have + done + ... (but didn't)`
* **仿写练习 1**：我本应该早点出发，这样就不会错过火车了。(I should have set off earlier, so I wouldn't have missed the train.)
* **仿写练习 2**：你本应该在考试前多复习一遍笔记的。(You should have reviewed your notes one more time before the exam.)

### 2. 🔍 近义词/易混词辨析 (Word Discrimination)
* **对比辨析**：**amused vs. funny**
* **使用差异解析**：
`amused` 是形容词，通常描述人的心理状态，即“感到好笑的、被逗乐的”；而 `funny` 描述事物的属性，即“滑稽的、有趣的、令人发笑的”。
* **地道表达搭配**：
* be amused by the situation (被情况逗乐)
* a funny story (一个有趣的故事)
* find it amusing (觉得它很有趣)

### 3. 💬 课后思辨讨论题 (Speaking & Writing Prompts)
* **Question 1**: Do you think the police were right to let Jumbo off, or should they have fined Jimmy Gates? Why?
* **Question 2**: If you were the circus owner, how would you ensure the safety of both the public and the animals when organizing such a parade?
* **高分表达提示 (Hint Words)**: *responsibility (责任), public order (公共秩序), animal welfare (动物福利), lenient (宽容的), traffic congestion (交通拥堵), precautionary measures (预防措施)*
