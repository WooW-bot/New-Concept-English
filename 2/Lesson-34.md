# Lesson 34: Quick Work

## 课文内容 (Text)

Dan Robinson has been worried all week. Last Tuesday he received a letter from the local police. In the letter he was asked to call at the station. Dan wondered why he was wanted by the police, but he went to the station yesterday and now he is not worried anymore.

At the station, he was told by a smiling policeman that his bicycle had been found. Five days ago, the policeman told him, the bicycle was picked up in a small village four hundred miles away. It is now being sent to his home by train.

Dan was most surprised when he heard the news. He was amused too, because he never expected the bicycle to be found. It was stolen twenty years ago when Dan was a boy of fifteen!

## 语法要点解析 (Grammar & Key Structures)

### 1. 被动语态的多时态呈现 (Passive Voice in Various Tenses)
* **经典原句**：
  1. *In the letter he **was asked** to call at the station.* (一般过去时被动)
  2. *...that his bicycle **had been found**.* (过去完成时被动)
  3. *It **is now being sent** to his home by train.* (现在进行时被动)
* **核心解析**：
  本课是学习英语被动语态的经典篇章。被动语态用于强调动作的承受者，或者在动作执行者未知、不重要时使用。
* **结构解析**：
  被动语态的基本结构为 `be + 过去分词 (done)`。其时态变化完全体现在 `be` 动词上：
  * **一般过去时被动** (`was/were + done`)：表示过去发生的被动动作。
  * **过去完成时被动** (`had been + done`)：表示在过去某一动作（如 policeman told him）之前已经完成的被动动作（“过去的过去”）。
  * **现在进行时被动** (`am/is/are + being + done`)：表示此时此刻正在进行的被动动作。

### 2. 动词不定式的复合结构与被动式 (Infinitive with Object / Passive Infinitive)
* **经典原句**：*...because he never expected the bicycle to be found.*
* **核心解析**：
  `expect` 后面可以接“宾语 + 动词不定式”结构（即复合宾语），表示“预料/期待某人或某物做某事”。因为宾语“自行车（bicycle）”与动词“寻找（find）”之间是被动关系，所以不定式必须使用被动式 `to be found`。
* **结构解析**：
  * 主动结构：`expect sb. to do sth.` (预料某人做某事)
  * 被动结构：`expect sth. to be done` (预料某事被做)

## 写作仿写与思辨口语 (Writing Practice & Critical Thinking)

### 1. ✍️ 提分句型仿写 (Pattern Drill)
* **句型名称**：现在进行时的被动语态 (Something is being done)
* **句型公式**：`Subject + am/is/are + being + [过去分词] + (by...)`
* **仿写练习 1**：别担心，你的汽车现在正在被修理。  
  (Don't worry, your car is being repaired now.)
* **仿写练习 2**：随着新政策的实施，许多历史建筑目前正受到保护。  
  (With the implementation of the new policy, many historical buildings are being protected at present.)

### 2. 🔍 近义词/易混词辨析 (Word Discrimination)
* **对比辨析**：**receive vs. accept**
* **使用差异解析**：
  * `receive` 强调“收到”这一**客观事实**，不涉及收件人的主观态度（即“收到了，但不见得接受”）。
  * `accept` 强调“接受”这一**主观态度**，表示同意、认可或乐意收下。
* **地道表达搭配**：
  * *He **received** an invitation to the party, but he didn't **accept** it.* (他收到了派对邀请，但他没有接受。)
  * **receive** a letter / **receive** education (收到信 / 接受教育 - 习惯用法)
  * **accept** an apology / **accept** a gift (接受道歉 / 接受礼物)

### 3. 💬 课后思辨讨论题 (Speaking & Writing Prompts)
* **Question 1**: The story shows an extreme case of a stolen bicycle being found after 20 years. Do you think local police should spend time and resources investigating minor thefts like bicycle stealing? Why or why not?
* **Question 2**: How has technology (such as GPS trackers, shared bikes, and surveillance cameras) changed the way we prevent and solve petty crimes today compared to 20 years ago?
* **高分表达提示 (Hint Words)**: *allocate resources (分配资源), petty theft (轻微盗窃), surveillance camera (监控摄像头), GPS tracking (GPS定位), efficiency (效率), public security (公共安全)*
