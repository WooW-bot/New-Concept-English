# Lesson 91: Three Men in a Basket

## 课文内容 (Text)

A pilot noticed a balloon which seemed to be making for a Royal Air Force station nearby.
一位飞行员发现一个气球似乎正朝附近的一个皇家空军基地飞去。
He informed the station at once, but no one there was able to explain the mystery.
他立即通知了基地，但那里没有人能解释这一神秘现象。
The officer in the control tower was very angry when he heard the news, because balloons can be a great danger to aircraft.
控制塔台的军官听到这个消息后非常生气，因为气球对飞机来说可能构成巨大的危险。
He said that someone might be spying on the station and the pilot was ordered to keep track of the strange object.
他说可能有人在监视基地，于是飞行员接到命令，要求跟踪那个不明物体。

The pilot managed to circle the balloon for some time.
飞行员设法绕着气球盘旋了一段时间。
He could make out three men in a basket under it and one of them was holding a pair of binoculars.
他能辨认出气球下方吊篮里有三个人，其中一人正拿着一副双筒望远镜。
When the balloon was over the station, the pilot saw one of the men taking photographs.
当气球飞到基地上空时，飞行员看到其中一个人在拍照。
Soon afterwards, the balloon began to descend and it landed near an airfield.
不久之后，气球开始下降，并在一个机场附近着陆了。

The police were called in, but they could not arrest anyone, for the basket contained two Members of Parliament and the Commanding Officer of the station!
警察被叫来了，但他们无法逮捕任何人，因为吊篮里坐着两名国会议员和该基地的指挥官！
As the Commanding Officer explained later, one half of the station did not know what the other half was doing!
正如那位指挥官后来解释的那样，基地的一半人根本不知道另一半人在干什么！

## 语法要点解析 (Grammar & Key Structures)

### 1. 情态动词表推测 (Modal Verbs for Speculation)
* **经典原句**：*He said that someone might be spying on the station...*
* **核心解析**：
**might be spying** 表示对正在进行的动作进行不确定的推测（“可能正在……”）。
* **结构解析**：`might + be + doing`。情态动词 `might` 比 `may` 语气更弱，表示可能性较低。此处用于推测当时正在发生的动作，强调“可能正在进行中”。

### 2. 宾语从句 (Object Clause)
* **经典原句**：*As the Commanding Officer explained later, one half of the station did not know what the other half was doing!*
* **核心解析**：
**what the other half was doing** 是一个宾语从句，充当动词 `know` 的宾语。
* **结构解析**：宾语从句由连接代词 `what` 引导，在从句中作 `doing` 的宾语。注意宾语从句必须使用陈述语序（主语+谓语）。

### 3. 过去完成时 (Past Perfect Tense)
* **经典原句**：*The police were called in, but they could not arrest anyone, for the basket had contained...* (注：原句为一般过去时，若强调“在警察到达前就已经包含”，可使用过去完成时)。
* **核心解析**：
过去完成时用于表示“过去的过去”。在本课中，如果强调“篮子里的人在警察赶到之前就已经坐在那里了”，则使用 `had contained`。它帮助理清了动作发生的先后顺序，即“包含”这一状态先于“逮捕”这一动作。

## 写作仿写与思辨口语 (Writing Practice & Critical Thinking)

### 1. ✍️ 提分句型仿写 (Pattern Drill)
* **句型名称**：...did not know what the other half was doing (一方不知道另一方在做什么——形容内部沟通不畅)
* **句型公式**：`[Subject A] did not know what [Subject B] was doing.`
* **仿写练习 1**：由于部门间缺乏沟通，市场部根本不知道研发部在开发什么新产品。(Due to the lack of communication, the marketing department did not know what the R&D department was developing.)
* **仿写练习 2**：那家大公司内部管理混乱，左手不知道右手在干什么。(The big company was poorly managed; one hand did not know what the other hand was doing.)

### 2. 🔍 近义词/易混词辨析 (Word Discrimination)
* **对比辨析**：**inform vs. notify**
* **使用差异解析**：
`inform` 是通用词，指提供信息或事实，通常带有一定的正式感；`notify` 则侧重于“正式通知”，通常指为了某种特定目的（如法律、行政要求）而告知某人某事。
* **地道表达搭配**：
* inform someone of something (通知某人某事)
* notify the police (报警/通知警方)
* keep someone informed (让某人随时了解情况)

### 3. 💬 课后思辨讨论题 (Speaking & Writing Prompts)
* **Question 1**: Was the Commanding Officer's explanation a valid excuse, or was it just a way to avoid responsibility?
* **Question 2**: In the age of drones and high-tech surveillance, how can we balance national security with personal privacy?
* **高分表达提示 (Hint Words)**: *accountability (责任), miscommunication (沟通失误), surveillance (监视), privacy infringement (侵犯隐私), national security (国家安全), bureaucratic (官僚的)*
