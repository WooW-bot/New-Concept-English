# Lesson 85: Never Too Old To Learn

## 课文内容 (Text)

I have just received a letter from my old school, informing me that my former headmaster, Mr. Stuart Page, will be retiring next week. Pupils of the school, old and new, will be sending him a present to mark the occasion. All those who have contributed towards the gift will sign their names in a large album which will be sent to the headmaster's home.

We shall all remember Mr. Page for his patience and understanding and for the kindly encouragement he gave us when we went so unwillingly to school. A great many former pupils will be attending a farewell dinner in his honour next Thursday. It is a curious coincidence that the day before his retirement, Mr. Page will have been teaching for a total of forty years.

After he has retired he will devote himself to gardening. For him, this will be an entirely new hobby. But this does not matter, for, as he has often remarked, one is never too old to learn.

## 语法要点解析 (Grammar & Key Structures)

### 1. 将来完成时 (Future Perfect Tense)
* **经典原句**：*...Mr. Page will have been teaching for a total of forty years.*
* **核心解析**：
**will have been teaching** 表示到将来某个时间点（退休前一天），动作已经持续了一段时间。
* **结构解析**：`will + have + been + 现在分词`。将来完成进行时强调动作在未来某个时间点之前已经持续了多久，侧重于动作的延续性和累积效果。

### 2. 定语从句 (Attributive Clause)
* **经典原句**：*All those who have contributed towards the gift will sign their names in a large album which will be sent to the headmaster's home.*
* **核心解析**：
本句包含两个定语从句。第一个 `who have contributed...` 修饰先行词 `those`；第二个 `which will be sent...` 修饰先行词 `album`。
* **结构解析**：关系代词 `who` 指代人，`which` 指代物。在从句中，它们都充当主语，引导从句对先行词进行限定说明。

### 3. 动名词作介词宾语 (Gerund as Object of Preposition)
* **经典原句**：*...remember Mr. Page for his patience and understanding and for the kindly encouragement he gave us when we went so unwillingly to school.*
* **核心解析**：
`understanding` 在此处是动名词，意为“理解力/体谅”。
* **结构解析**：介词 `for` 后面接名词或动名词短语。在英语中，动名词常用来表达抽象概念或行为，此处与 `patience`（名词）并列，共同构成 `remember...for...` 的结构。

## 写作仿写与思辨口语 (Writing Practice & Critical Thinking)

### 1. ✍️ 提分句型仿写 (Pattern Drill)
* **句型名称**：It is a curious coincidence that... (……真是个奇妙的巧合)
* **句型公式**：`It is a curious/remarkable/strange coincidence that + [从句]`
* **仿写练习 1**：我们俩在同一天生日，这真是个奇妙的巧合。(It is a curious coincidence that we both have our birthdays on the same day.)
* **仿写练习 2**：我们竟然在异国他乡的同一家餐厅偶遇，这真是个奇妙的巧合。(It is a curious coincidence that we happened to meet in the same restaurant in a foreign country.)

### 2. 🔍 近义词/易混词辨析 (Word Discrimination)
* **对比辨析**：**contribute to vs. devote to**
* **使用差异解析**：
`contribute to` 侧重于“贡献、捐赠”，强调将某物（金钱、时间、建议）投入到某项事业中，是整体的一部分；`devote to` 侧重于“致力于、献身于”，强调将全部的时间、精力或情感倾注于某人或某事，语气比 contribute 更强烈，带有情感色彩。
* **地道表达搭配**：
* contribute to the success of... (为……的成功做出贡献)
* devote one's life to science (将一生奉献给科学)

### 3. 💬 课后思辨讨论题 (Speaking & Writing Prompts)
* **Question 1**: The headmaster believes one is "never too old to learn." Do you agree? What are the benefits of learning a new skill in your later years?
* **Question 2**: If you were to organize a farewell gift for a mentor, what kind of gift would be the most meaningful? Why?
* **高分表达提示 (Hint Words)**: *lifelong learning (终身学习), cognitive health (认知健康), personal fulfillment (个人成就感), sentimental value (情感价值), mentor (导师), legacy (遗产/精神财富)*
