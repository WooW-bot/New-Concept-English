# Lesson 38: Everything Except the Weather

## 课文内容 (Text)

My old friend, Harrison, had lived in the Mediterranean for many years before he returned to England.
我的老朋友哈里森在回到英国之前，曾在地中海地区住了多年。
He had often dreamed of retiring in England and had planned to settle down in the country.
他曾多次梦想在英国退休，并计划在乡间定居。
He had no sooner returned than he bought a house and went to live there.
他刚一回来就买了一栋房子，并搬进去住了。

Almost immediately he began to complain about the weather, for even though it was still summer, it rained continually and it was often bitterly cold.
几乎立刻他就开始抱怨起天气来，因为尽管当时还是夏天，却雨水不断，而且常常冷得刺骨。
After so many years of sunshine, Harrison got a shock.
在享受了这么多年的阳光之后，哈里森感到非常不适应。
He acted as if he had never lived in England before.
他的表现就好像他以前从未在英国住过一样。

In the end, it was more than he could bear.
最后，他实在无法忍受了。
He had hardly had time to settle down when he sold the house and left the country.
他还没来得及安顿下来，就把房子卖掉离开了这个国家。
The dream he had had for so many years ended there.
他做了这么多年的梦就这样结束了。
Harrison had thought of everything except the weather.
哈里森什么都想到了，就是没考虑到天气。

## 语法要点解析 (Grammar & Key Structures)

### 1. "一……就……" 的时间副词结构 (No sooner... than... / Hardly... when...)
* **经典原句**：
  * *He had no sooner returned than he bought a house...*
  * *He had hardly had time to settle down when he sold the house...*
* **核心解析**：
  这两个句型均表示“一……就……”，用于紧凑地连接两个先后发生的过去动作。
  * **时态搭配**：主句动作发生在前，用**过去完成时**（`had done`）；从句动作发生在后，用**一般过去时**（`did`）。
  * **倒装用法**：若将 `No sooner` 或 `Hardly` 置于句首以示强调，主句须部分倒装（助动词 `had` 提前至主语前）。例如：*No sooner had he returned than he bought a house.*
* **结构解析**：
  * `主语 + had + no sooner + 过去分词 + than + 主语 + 一般过去时`
  * `主语 + had + hardly + 过去分词 + when + 主语 + 一般过去时`

### 2. as if 引导的虚拟语气 (Subjunctive Mood with 'as if')
* **经典原句**：*He acted as if he had never lived in England before.*
* **核心解析**：
  `as if`（或 `as though`）意为“仿佛，好像”。当从句所表达的内容与事实相反或是不可能发生的情况时，需使用虚拟语气。
  * 本句中，Harrison 过去确实在英国生活过，但他的举止却像“从未住过”一样，这与过去的事实相反，因此从句使用**过去完成时**（`had never lived`）来表示对过去的虚拟。
* **结构解析**：`主语 + 动词 + as if + 主语 + had (not) + 过去分词`（对过去的虚拟）。

### 3. 过去完成时表示“过去的过去” (Past Perfect Tense)
* **经典原句**：*My old friend, Harrison, had lived in the Mediterranean for many years before he returned to England.*
* **核心解析**：
  过去完成时（`had + done`）的基本用法是表示在过去某一动作或时间之前已经发生或完成的动作。
  * 句中 Harrison “住在地中海（had lived）”这一状态，发生在“回到英国（returned）”这一过去动作之前，属于典型的“过去的过去”。

## 写作仿写与思辨口语 (Writing Practice & Critical Thinking)

### 1. ✍️ 提分句型仿写 (Pattern Drill)
* **句型名称**：No sooner... than... (一……就……)
* **句型公式**：`主语 + had no sooner + 过去分词 + than + 主语 + 一般过去时`（或句首倒装：`No sooner had + 主语 + 过去分词 + than...`）
* **仿写练习 1**：他刚一踏入办公室，电话就响了。
  *(He had no sooner stepped into the office than the phone rang. / No sooner had he stepped into the office than the phone rang.)*
* **仿写练习 2**：新产品刚一上市，就被抢购一空。
  *(The new product had no sooner been launched than it was sold out. / No sooner had the new product been launched than it was sold out.)*

### 2. 🔍 近义词/易混词辨析 (Word Discrimination)
* **对比辨析**：**continually vs. continuously**
* **使用差异解析**：
  * `continually` 强调“**频频地，屡次地**”，指动作断断续续地重复发生，中间有停顿。课文中 *it rained continually* 指雨下下停停，连日不断。
  * `continuously` 强调“**连续不断地**”，指在时间或空间上没有任何中断、一气呵成。例如：*The machine has been running continuously for 48 hours.*（机器不间断地运转了48小时）。
* **地道表达搭配**：
  * complain continually (不停地抱怨/碎碎念)
  * grow continuously (持续不断地增长)

### 3. 💬 课后思辨讨论题 (Speaking & Writing Prompts)
* **Question 1**: Why do you think Harrison experienced such a strong "shock" upon returning to his home country? Have you ever experienced "reverse culture shock" after being away from a place for a long time?
* **Question 2**: How much does the weather or climate of a city affect your productivity, mood, and overall happiness? Would you relocate to another country solely for better weather?
* **高分表达提示 (Hint Words)**: *reverse culture shock (逆向文化冲击), idealize (理想化), adapt to (适应), seasonal affective disorder (季节性情绪失调), geographic preference (地理偏好), relocate (迁移/重新安置)*
