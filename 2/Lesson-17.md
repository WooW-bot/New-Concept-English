# Lesson 17: Always Young

## 课文内容 (Text)

My aunt Jennifer is an actress. She must be at least thirty-five years old. In spite of this, she often appears on the stage as a young girl. Jennifer will have to take part in a new play soon. This time, she will be a girl of seventeen.

In the play, she must appear in a bright red dress and long black stockings. Last year in another play, she had to wear short socks and a bright, orange-coloured dress.

If anyone ever asks her how old she is, she always answers, 'Darling, it must be terrible to be grown up!'

## 语法要点解析 (Grammar & Key Structures)

### 1. 情态动词 `must` 表示推测 (Must for Deduction)
*   **经典原句**：*She must be at least thirty-five years old.* / *it must be terrible to be grown up!*
*   **核心解析**：
    情态动词 `must` 在这里不表示“必须”，而是表示对现在或将来的情况进行肯定的推测，意为“一定，想必是”。这种推测是基于某种证据或逻辑判断。
*   **结构解析**：`主语 + must + 动词原形`。如果推测的是过去发生的事情，则用 `must have done`。

### 2. 情态动词 `have to` 表示义务/必要性 (Have to for Obligation)
*   **经典原句**：*Jennifer will have to take part in a new play soon.* / *she had to wear short socks...*
*   **核心解析**：
    `have to` 表示客观上的义务、必要性或被迫做某事，通常是由于外部环境、规则或他人要求。与 `must`（主观义务）不同，`have to` 可以用于各种时态，如将来时 `will have to` 和过去时 `had to`。
*   **结构解析**：`主语 + (will/had) + have to + 动词原形`。

### 3. `in spite of` 表示让步 (In spite of for Concession)
*   **经典原句**：*In spite of this, she often appears on the stage as a young girl.*
*   **核心解析**：
    `in spite of` 是一个复合介词，意为“尽管，不顾”，用来引导一个让步状语，表示尽管存在某种情况，但另一情况仍然发生。它后面通常接名词、代词或动名词短语。
*   **结构解析**：`In spite of + 名词/代词/动名词短语`。

## 写作仿写与思辨口语 (Writing Practice & Critical Thinking)

### 1. ✍️ 提分句型仿写 (Pattern Drill)
*   **句型名称**：It must be terrible/wonderful to be... (做...一定是可怕/美好的)
*   **句型公式**：`It must be + 形容词 (如terrible/wonderful) + to be + [身份/状态]`
*   **仿写练习 1**：想到每天早起通勤，一定是件痛苦的事。 (It must be terrible to get up early and commute every day.)
*   **仿写练习 2**：成为一名受人尊敬的医生，一定是件美好的事。 (It must be wonderful to be a respected doctor.)

### 2. 🔍 近义词/易混词辨析 (Word Discrimination)
*   **对比辨析**：**appear vs. seem**
*   **使用差异解析**：
    `appear` 强调“显现，呈现出某种外表”，有时暗示这种外表可能不是真实的，或者指物理上的出现（如“登台演出”）。它更侧重于视觉上的呈现。
    `seem` 更侧重于主观的判断或感觉，表示“看起来好像，似乎”，通常基于观察或印象，强调一种推测或不确定性。
*   **地道表达搭配**：
    *   appear on stage (登台演出)
    *   appear to be (似乎是，看起来是)
    *   seem to be (似乎是，好像是)
    *   It seems that... (似乎...)

### 3. 💬 课后思辨讨论题 (Speaking & Writing Prompts)
*   **Question 1**: Do you think it's important for actors to look their age in plays or films? Why or why not?
*   **Question 2**: What are the advantages and disadvantages of being "grown up"? Do you agree with Jennifer's statement?
*   **高分表达提示 (Hint Words)**: *appearance (外表), authenticity (真实性), role (角色), experience (经验), responsibility (责任), freedom (自由), perception (看法), youth (青春), maturity (成熟)*
