# Lesson 66: Sweet as Honey

## 课文内容 (Text)

In 1963 a Lancaster bomber crashed on Wallis Island, a remote place in the South Pacific, a long way west of Samoa. The plane wasn't too badly damaged, but over the years the crash was forgotten and the wreck remained undisturbed.

Then in 1989, twenty-six years after the crash, the plane was accidentally rediscovered in an aerial survey of the island. By this time, a Lancaster bomber in reasonable condition was rare and worth rescuing. The French authorities had the plane packaged and moved in parts back to France.

Now a group of enthusiasts are going to have the plane restored. It has four Rolls-Royce Merlin engines but the group will need to have only three of them rebuilt. Imagine their surprise and delight when they broke open the packing cases and found that the fourth engine was sweet as honey--still in perfect condition. A colony of bees had turned the engine into a hive and it was totally preserved in beeswax!

## 语法要点解析 (Grammar & Key Structures)

### 1. Have something done (使役用法)
* **经典原句**：*The French authorities had the plane packaged and moved in parts back to France.*
* **核心解析**：
**have + 宾语 + 过去分词** 表示“请/让别人做某事”。此处强调动作的结果，即飞机被包装并被运走，动作的执行者是“French authorities”雇佣的专业人员，而非当局自己动手。
* **结构解析**：`have` 在此作为使役动词，后接宾语（the plane），再接过去分词（packaged/moved）作宾语补足语，表示宾语与动作之间是被动关系。

### 2. 过去完成时 (Past Perfect Tense)
* **经典原句**：*...the crash was forgotten and the wreck remained undisturbed.*
* **核心解析**：
课文中描述了 1963 年坠机后，直到 1989 年被发现之间的状态。虽然文中多用一般过去时叙述，但隐含了“在 1989 年之前，飞机一直处于被遗忘的状态”。
* **结构解析**：`had + 过去分词`。用于表示在过去某一时间点（1989年）之前已经发生或持续的状态。

### 3. 比较状语从句 (Comparative Clause)
* **经典原句**：*...found that the fourth engine was sweet as honey.*
* **核心解析**：
**as...as...** 结构用于同级比较。在此处，“sweet as honey”是一个习语用法，形容事物状态极好、完好无损，且带有生动形象的修辞色彩。

## 写作仿写与思辨口语 (Writing Practice & Critical Thinking)

### 1. ✍️ 提分句型仿写 (Pattern Drill)
* **句型名称**：Imagine their surprise and delight when... (当……时，想象一下他们有多么惊讶和高兴)
* **句型公式**：`Imagine their [emotion 1] and [emotion 2] when + [从句]`
* **仿写练习 1**：当他们打开那份尘封已久的遗嘱并发现里面藏着巨额财富时，想象一下他们有多么惊讶和兴奋。(Imagine their surprise and excitement when they opened the long-forgotten will and found a huge fortune hidden inside.)
* **仿写练习 2**：当他在荒岛上生活了三年后终于看到救援船只出现时，想象一下他有多么震惊和狂喜。(Imagine his shock and ecstasy when he finally saw the rescue ship appear after living on the deserted island for three years.)

### 2. 🔍 近义词/易混词辨析 (Word Discrimination)
* **对比辨析**：**remote vs. distant**
* **使用差异解析**：
`remote` 侧重于地理位置的“偏僻、荒凉、难以到达”，通常暗示与文明中心隔绝；`distant` 则是一个中性词，侧重于“距离上的远”，既可以是物理距离，也可以是时间或心理上的距离。
* **地道表达搭配**：
* a remote village (偏远山村)
* a remote possibility (极小的可能性)
* a distant relative (远房亲戚)
* in the distant future (在遥远的未来)

### 3. 💬 课后思辨讨论题 (Speaking & Writing Prompts)
* **Question 1**: If you discovered a historical relic in your backyard, would you report it to the authorities or keep it for yourself? Why?
* **Question 2**: Do you think it is worth spending a huge amount of money to restore old machines like this bomber? What is the value of such "relics"?
* **高分表达提示 (Hint Words)**: *historical significance (历史意义), cultural heritage (文化遗产), preservation (保护), financial cost (经济成本), sentimental value (情感价值), archaeological discovery (考古发现)*
