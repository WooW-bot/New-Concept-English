# Lesson 09: A Cold Welcome

## 课文内容 (Text)

On Wednesday evening, we went to the Town Hall. It was the last day of the year and a large crowd of people had gathered under the Town Hall clock. It would strike twelve in twenty minutes' time.

Fifteen minutes passed and then, at five to twelve, the clock stopped. The big minute hand did not move. We waited and waited, but nothing happened. Suddenly someone shouted, 'It's two minutes past twelve! The clock has stopped!' I looked at my watch. It was true. The big clock refused to welcome the New Year. At that moment, everybody began to laugh and sing.

## 语法要点解析 (Grammar & Key Structures)

### 1. "would" 表示过去将来时 (Future in the Past with 'would')
*   **经典原句**：*It would strike twelve in twenty minutes' time.*
*   **核心解析**：
    `would` 在这里表示从过去某个时间点看，将来会发生的事情。它通常用于叙述过去的故事，表达当时预期的未来动作。
*   **结构解析**：`主语 + would + 动词原形`。这里的 `would` 是 `will` 的过去形式，表示在过去时间点上对未来事件的预测或计划。

### 2. 现在完成时 (Present Perfect Tense)
*   **经典原句**：*'The clock has stopped!'*
*   **核心解析**：
    `has stopped` 是现在完成时，表示一个发生在过去的动作，但其结果或影响持续到现在，或者与现在有关。在这里，钟表停了，这个状态一直持续到说话的“现在”。
*   **结构解析**：`主语 + have/has + 动词的过去分词`。常用于表示：1) 动作发生在过去，但对现在有影响；2) 动作从过去开始一直持续到现在；3) 动作在过去不确定时间发生过。

### 3. 时间表达方式 (Expressions of Time)
*   **经典原句**：*It would strike twelve in twenty minutes' time.* / *at five to twelve*
*   **核心解析**：
    课文中使用了多种表达时间的方式，例如 `in... time` 表示“在...时间之后”，`at... to...` 表示“差...到...点”。
*   **结构解析**：
    *   `in + 时间段 + 's time`：表示从现在算起，在某个时间段之后。例如：`in an hour's time` (一小时后)。
    *   `at + 分钟数 + to/past + 小时数`：表示具体的时间点。`to` 表示“差”，`past` 表示“过”。例如：`at ten to three` (两点五十分)，`at ten past three` (三点十分)。

## 写作仿写与思辨口语 (Writing Practice & Critical Thinking)

### 1. ✍️ 提分句型仿写 (Pattern Drill)
*   **句型名称**：Refuse to do something (拒绝做某事)
*   **句型公式**：`Subject + refuse(s) to + Verb (base form)`
*   **仿写练习 1**：他拒绝承认自己的错误，即使证据确凿。 (He refused to admit his mistake, even though the evidence was clear.)
*   **仿写练习 2**：我的旧打印机拒绝工作，所以我不得不买一台新的。 (My old printer refused to work, so I had to buy a new one.)

### 2. 🔍 近义词/易混词辨析 (Word Discrimination)
*   **对比辨析**：**wait vs. wait for**
*   **使用差异解析**：
    `wait` 是一个不及物动词，表示“等待”这个动作本身，后面通常不直接跟宾语。如果需要指出等待的对象，则必须加上介词 `for`。`wait for` 是一个动词短语，表示“等待某人/某事”。
*   **地道表达搭配**：
    *   wait patiently (耐心等待)
    *   wait in line (排队等候)
    *   wait for a bus (等公交车)
    *   wait for an opportunity (等待机会)

### 3. 💬 课后思辨讨论题 (Speaking & Writing Prompts)
*   **Question 1**: Have you ever experienced a public event or celebration that didn't go as planned? How did people react, and what was the atmosphere like?
*   **Question 2**: The story describes people laughing and singing despite the clock stopping. Why do you think they reacted this way instead of being disappointed or angry?
*   **高分表达提示 (Hint Words)**: *anticipation (期待), unexpected (意想不到的), disappointment (失望), spontaneous (自发的), resilience (韧性), festive atmosphere (节日气氛)*
