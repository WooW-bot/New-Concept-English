# Lesson 61: Trouble with the Hubble

## 课文内容 (Text)

The Hubble telescope was launched into space by NASA on April 20, 1990 at a cost of over a billion dollars.
1990年4月20日，美国国家航空航天局将哈勃望远镜发射到了太空，耗资超过10亿美元。
Right from the start there was trouble with the Hubble.
哈勃望远镜从一开始就出了问题。
The pictures it sent us were very disappointing because its main mirror was faulty!
它传回的照片非常令人失望，因为它的主镜有瑕疵！

NASA is now going to put the telescope right, so it will soon be sending up four astronauts to repair it.
美国国家航空航天局现在准备修复这架望远镜，因此很快将派遣4名宇航员上去进行修理。
The shuttle Endeavour will be taking the astronauts to the Hubble.
“奋进”号航天飞机将把宇航员送往哈勃望远镜处。
A robot-arm from the Endeavour will grab the telescope and hold it while the astronauts make the necessary repairs.
“奋进”号上的一只机械臂将抓住并固定住望远镜，以便宇航员进行必要的修理。

Of course, the Hubble is above the earth's atmosphere, so it will soon be sending us the clearest pictures of the stars and distant galaxies that we have ever seen.
当然，哈勃望远镜位于地球大气层之外，因此它很快就会传回我们所见过的关于恒星和遥远星系的最清晰的照片。
The Hubble will tell us a great deal about the age and size of the universe.
哈勃望远镜将告诉我们许多有关宇宙的年龄和大小的信息。
By the time you read this, the Hubble's eagle eye will have sent us thousands and thousands of wonderful pictures.
当你读到这篇文章时，哈勃那敏锐的“鹰眼”已经传回了成千上万张精彩的照片。

## 语法要点解析 (Grammar & Key Structures)

### 1. 将来完成时 (Future Perfect Tense)
* **经典原句**：*By the time you read this, the Hubble's eagle eye will have sent us thousands and thousands of wonderful pictures.*
* **核心解析**：
**will have sent** 是将来完成时。它表示在将来某一时间点之前已经完成的动作。
* **结构解析**：`will + have + 过去分词`。常与 `by the time...` 或 `by + 时间状语` 连用，强调动作在未来某个时间点“已经完成”的状态。

### 2. 动词不定式作目的状语 (Infinitive of Purpose)
* **经典原句**：*...it will soon be sending up four astronauts to repair it.*
* **核心解析**：
**to repair it** 在句中作目的状语，说明发送宇航员的目的。
* **结构解析**：`to + 动词原形`。在英语中，不定式常用来解释动作发生的动机或目的，常可翻译为“为了……”。

### 3. 现在分词作伴随状语 (Present Participle as Adverbial)
* **经典原句**：*The shuttle Endeavour will be taking the astronauts to the Hubble.*
* **核心解析**：
此处使用了将来进行时（will be taking），表示在未来某个阶段正在进行的动作，强调过程感。

## 写作仿写与思辨口语 (Writing Practice & Critical Thinking)

### 1. ✍️ 提分句型仿写 (Pattern Drill)
* **句型名称**：By the time + [从句/时间点], [主句用将来完成时] (到……时候，……将已经完成)
* **句型公式**：`By the time + [主语 + 一般现在时], [主语 + will have done + sth.]`
* **仿写练习 1**：到下周这个时候，我们将已经完成了所有的期末复习。 (By the time this time next week, we will have finished all our final reviews.)
* **仿写练习 2**：到你毕业时，你将已经在这所学校度过了四年时光。 (By the time you graduate, you will have spent four years in this school.)

### 2. 🔍 近义词/易混词辨析 (Word Discrimination)
* **对比辨析**：**faulty vs. broken**
* **使用差异解析**：
`faulty` 侧重于“设计或制造上的缺陷、瑕疵”，即东西虽然能用，但功能不正常或不准确（如镜片磨制不当）。而 `broken` 侧重于“损坏、破碎、无法运作”，通常指物理上的断裂或彻底瘫痪。
* **地道表达搭配**：
* a faulty connection (连接不良)
* a faulty argument (有逻辑漏洞的论点)
* broken heart (心碎) / broken window (打破的窗户)

### 3. 💬 课后思辨讨论题 (Speaking & Writing Prompts)
* **Question 1**: Space exploration costs billions of dollars. Do you think this money should be spent on space or on solving problems on Earth? Why?
* **Question 2**: If you were an astronaut, would you be willing to go on a repair mission in space? What qualities do you think an astronaut needs most?
