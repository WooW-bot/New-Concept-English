# Lesson 14: Do You Speak English

## 课文内容 (Text)

I had an amusing experience last year. After I had left a small village in the south of France, I drove on to the next town. On the way, a young man waved to me. I stopped and he asked me for a lift. As soon as he had got into the car, I said good morning to him in French and he replied in the same language.

Apart from a few words, I do not know any French at all. Neither of us spoke during the journey. I had nearly reached the town, when the young man suddenly said, very slowly, 'Do you speak English?' As I soon learnt, he was English himself!

## 语法要点解析 (Grammar & Key Structures)

### 1. 过去完成时 (Past Perfect Tense)

*   **经典原句**：*After I had left a small village in the south of France, I drove on to the next town.*
*   **核心解析**：
    过去完成时用来表示在过去某个时间点或某个动作之前已经完成的动作。它强调“过去的过去”。
*   **结构解析**：`had + 动词的过去分词`。在复合句中，通常与一般过去时连用，表示先发生的动作（过去完成时）是后发生的动作（一般过去时）的基础或前提。

### 2. "Neither of us" 结构 (The structure "Neither of us")

*   **经典原句**：*Neither of us spoke during the journey.*
*   **核心解析**：
    `Neither of + 复数名词/代词` 意为“两者都不”，强调在两个人或两件事物中，没有一个符合描述。
*   **结构解析**：`Neither of + us/them/the two students + 单数谓语动词`。尽管 `us/them` 是复数，但 `neither` 强调“没有一个”，因此谓语动词通常用单数形式。在口语中，有时也会见到复数谓语动词。

### 3. "Apart from" 的用法 (Usage of "Apart from")

*   **经典原句**：*Apart from a few words, I do not know any French at all.*
*   **核心解析**：
    `Apart from` 是一个介词短语，表示“除了……之外”。它可以有两种含义：
    1.  **除了……之外（还有）**：表示在提及的事物之外，还有其他事物。
    2.  **除了……之外（没有/不包括）**：表示排除了提及的事物。
    在本课例句中，它表示“除了几个词之外，我一点法语都不会”，属于第二种含义。
*   **结构解析**：`Apart from + 名词/名词短语/动名词短语, 主句`。

## 写作仿写与思辨口语 (Writing Practice & Critical Thinking)

### 1. ✍️ 提分句型仿写 (Pattern Drill)

*   **句型名称**：As soon as... (一...就...)
*   **句型公式**：`As soon as + 过去完成时从句, 过去简单时主句` (强调前一动作的完成是后一动作的直接前提)
*   **仿写练习 1**：他一到家，就立刻给他父母打了电话。(As soon as he had arrived home, he immediately called his parents.)
*   **仿写练习 2**：我一读完那本书，就把它借给了我的朋友。(As soon as I had finished reading the book, I lent it to my friend.)

### 2. 🔍 近义词/易混词辨析 (Word Discrimination)

*   **对比辨析**：**amusing vs. funny**
*   **使用差异解析**：
    `amusing` 指“有趣的，好笑的”，通常带有轻松愉快、令人感到愉悦的意味，笑点可能不那么强烈，更偏向于“消遣性”的乐趣。
    `funny` 指“滑稽的，可笑的”，通常指能引起大笑、更直接的幽默感，有时也指“奇怪的，不寻常的”。
*   **地道表达搭配**：
    *   **amusing**: an amusing story/anecdote (有趣的故事/轶事), find something amusing (觉得某事有趣)
    *   **funny**: a funny joke/story (好笑的笑话/故事), a funny feeling (一种奇怪的感觉), make funny faces (做鬼脸)

### 3. 💬 课后思辨讨论题 (Speaking & Writing Prompts)

*   **Question 1**: Have you ever experienced a situation where you were mistaken for someone else's nationality or language ability, or you mistook someone else's? How did you react?
*   **Question 2**: What are some common stereotypes people have about different nationalities or language speakers, and how can we overcome them to foster better understanding?
*   **高分表达提示 (Hint Words)**: *misunderstanding (误解), assumption (假设), stereotype (刻板印象), nationality (国籍), language barrier (语言障碍), cultural awareness (文化意识), open-minded (思想开放的)*
