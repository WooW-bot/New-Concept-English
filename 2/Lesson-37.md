# Lesson 37: The Olympic Games

## 课文内容 (Text)

The Olympic Games will be held in our country in four years' time. As a great many people will be visiting the country, the government will be building new hotels, an immense stadium, and a new Olympic-standard swimming pool. They will also be building new roads and a special railway line.

The Games will be held just outside the capital and the whole area will be called 'Olympic City'. Workers will have completed the new roads by the end of this year. By the end of next year, they will have finished work on the new stadium.

The fantastic modern buildings have been designed by Kurt Gunter. Everybody will be watching anxiously as the new buildings go up. We are all very excited and are looking forward to the Olympic Games because they have never been held before in this country.

## 语法要点解析 (Grammar & Key Structures)

### 1. 将来进行时 (Future Continuous Tense)
* **经典原句**：*As a great many people will be visiting the country, the government will be building new hotels...*
* **核心解析**：
**will be visiting** 和 **will be building** 均为将来进行时。
* **结构解析**：`will + be + 动词的-ing形式`。
  * 在本课中，将来进行时并不一定强调“在将来某一时刻正在发生”，而是用来表示**按计划、安排或日程必然会发生的事**。
  * 这种时态比一般将来时（*will build*）听起来更加委婉、客观，暗示这些建设和游客的到来是随着奥运会的临近而自然发生的发展趋势。

### 2. 将来完成时 (Future Perfect Tense)
* **经典原句**：*Workers will have completed the new roads by the end of this year.*
* **核心解析**：
**will have completed** 为将来完成时。
* **结构解析**：`will + have + 过去分词`。
  * 该时态用于表示**在将来某一时间点之前已经完成的动作**。
  * 它常与表示将来时间的截止状语连用，如本句中的 **by the end of this year**（到今年年底之前）以及下一句中的 **by the end of next year**。

### 3. 现在完成时被动语态 (Present Perfect Passive)
* **经典原句**：*The fantastic modern buildings have been designed by Kurt Gunter.*
* **核心解析**：
**have been designed** 为现在完成时的被动语态。
* **结构解析**：`have/has + been + 过去分词`。
  * 此处强调“设计”这一动作在过去已经完成，而其成果（这些奇妙的现代建筑）在现在依然存在并呈现在人们眼前。使用被动语态是为了将“建筑（buildings）”作为话语的焦点。

## 写作仿写与思辨口语 (Writing Practice & Critical Thinking)

### 1. ✍️ 提分句型仿写 (Pattern Drill)
* **句型名称**：By the end of..., [subject] will have done... (到……底/之前，某人/某事将已经完成……)
* **句型公式**：`By the end of + [时间名词], + [主语] + will have + [过去分词] + [其他]`
* **仿写练习 1**：到下个月底，我们团队将完成这个软件的所有测试工作。 (By the end of next month, our team will have finished all the testing work on this software.)
* **仿写练习 2**：到今年年底，政府将已经建好那条连接两座城市的高速公路。 (By the end of this year, the government will have built the highway connecting the two cities.)

### 2. 🔍 近义词/易混词辨析 (Word Discrimination)
* **对比辨析**：**immense vs. huge vs. vast**
* **使用差异解析**：
  * `huge` 是最常用的普通词，侧重于物理体积、尺寸、数量或程度的“巨大”（如 *a huge dog*, *a huge success*）。
  * `immense` 带有文学和庄重色彩，侧重于“无法估量、无边无际”的巨大，常用于形容空间、面积、数量或程度（如 *an immense stadium*, *immense wealth*）。
  * `vast` 侧重于“辽阔、广袤”，常用来形容面积、范围、空间或知识的宽广（如 *a vast desert*, *vast knowledge*）。
* **地道表达搭配**：
  * make a **huge** difference (产生巨大影响)
  * under **immense** pressure (承受巨大的压力)
  * a **vast** area of land (一片广袤的土地)

### 3. 💬 课后思辨讨论题 (Speaking & Writing Prompts)
* **Question 1**: Hosting the Olympic Games brings both great opportunities and heavy financial burdens. Do you think it is worth it for a country to host the Olympics? Why or why not?
* **Question 2**: How do mega-events like the Olympics affect the daily lives of local citizens during the construction and preparation phases?
* **高分表达提示 (Hint Words)**: *infrastructure (基础设施), economic boost (经济推动), financial burden (财政负担), tourism (旅游业), traffic congestion (交通拥堵), national prestige (国家声誉)*
