# Lesson 59: In or Out

## 课文内容 (Text)

Our dog, Rex, used to sit outside our front gate and bark. Every time he wanted to come into the garden he would bark until someone opened the gate. As the neighbours complained of the noise, my husband spent weeks training him to press his paw on the latch to let himself in. Rex soon became an expert at opening the gate.

However, when I was going out shopping last week, I noticed him in the garden near the gate. This time he was barking so that someone would let him out! Since then, he has developed another bad habit. As soon as he opens the gate from the outside, he comes into the garden and waits until the gate shuts. Then he sits and barks until someone lets him out. After this he immediately lets himself in and begins barking again.

Yesterday my husband removed the gate and Rex got so annoyed we have not seen him since.

## 语法要点解析 (Grammar & Key Structures)

### 1. Used to do 表示过去习惯性动作
* **经典原句**：*Our dog, Rex, used to sit outside our front gate and bark.*
* **核心解析**：
`used to do` 用于描述过去经常发生但现在已经不再发生的动作或存在的状态。
* **结构解析**：`used to + 动词原形`。注意与 `be used to doing`（习惯于做某事）和 `be used to do`（被用来做某事）进行区分。

### 2. 目的状语从句 (Adverbial Clause of Purpose)
* **经典原句**：*This time he was barking so that someone would let him out!*
* **核心解析**：
`so that` 引导目的状语从句，表示“为了……，以便……”。
* **结构解析**：当从句中含有情态动词（如 `would`, `could`, `can`, `may`）时，`so that` 引导的从句通常表示目的。若没有情态动词，则往往表示结果（“以至于……”）。

### 3. 时间状语从句 (Adverbial Clause of Time)
* **经典原句**：*As soon as he opens the gate from the outside, he comes into the garden...*
* **核心解析**：
`as soon as` 引导时间状语从句，表示“一……就……”。
* **结构解析**：在时间状语从句中，若主句是一般将来时或祈使句，从句通常用一般现在时表示将来（主将从现）。本句中主句为一般现在时，从句也用一般现在时，表示一种反复发生的规律。

## 写作仿写与思辨口语 (Writing Practice & Critical Thinking)

### 1. ✍️ 提分句型仿写 (Pattern Drill)
* **句型名称**：...so that... (为了……)
* **句型公式**：`[主句动作] + so that + [从句目的]`
* **仿写练习 1**：我每天早起是为了能有更多时间复习功课。 (I get up early every day so that I can have more time to review my lessons.)
* **仿写练习 2**：他大声说话是为了让后排的同学也能听见。 (He spoke loudly so that the students in the back row could hear him.)

### 2. 🔍 近义词/易混词辨析 (Word Discrimination)
* **对比辨析**：**annoyed vs. angry**
* **使用差异解析**：
`angry` 是一个较强的词，表示愤怒，通常伴随着强烈的负面情绪；而 `annoyed` 程度较轻，表示因琐事或持续的干扰而感到“烦躁、恼火、不耐烦”。
* **地道表达搭配**：
* get annoyed at/with someone (对某人感到恼火)
* be annoyed by the noise (被噪音弄得心烦)
* a flash of anger (一阵怒火)

### 3. 💬 课后思辨讨论题 (Speaking & Writing Prompts)
* **Question 1**: Do you think training pets to perform human-like tasks is a sign of affection or a form of interference with their natural behavior?
* **Question 2**: Rex’s behavior shows he learned to manipulate his owners. Have you ever observed "clever" behavior in animals? Share an example.
* **高分表达提示 (Hint Words)**: *manipulate (操纵), intelligence (智力), instinct (本能), companionship (陪伴), behavioral conditioning (行为调节)*
