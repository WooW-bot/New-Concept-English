# Lesson 68: Persistent

## 课文内容 (Text)

I crossed the street to avoid meeting him but he saw me and came running towards me. It was no use pretending that I had not seen him, so I waved to him. I never enjoy meeting Nigel Dykes. He never has anything to do. No matter how busy you are, he always insists on coming with you.

I had to think of a way of preventing him from following me around all morning. 'Hello, Nigel,' I said 'Fancy meeting you here!' 'Hi, Elizabeth,' Nigel answered. 'I was just wondering how to spend the morning--until I saw you. You're not busy doing anything, are you?'

'No, not at all,' I answered. 'I'm going to...' 'Would you mind my coming with you?' he asked, before I had finished speaking. 'Not at all,' I lied, 'but I'm going to the dentist.' 'Then I'll come with you,' he answered. 'There's always plenty to read in the waiting room!'

## 语法要点解析 (Grammar & Key Structures)

### 1. It is no use doing sth. (做某事是没用的)
* **经典原句**：*It was no use pretending that I had not seen him, so I waved to him.*
* **核心解析**：
**It is no use doing sth.** 是一个固定句型，表示“做某事毫无意义或徒劳”。
* **结构解析**：`It` 为形式主语，真正的主语是后面的动名词短语 `pretending...`。类似的表达还有 `It is no good doing sth.` 或 `There is no point in doing sth.`。

### 2. 动名词作介词宾语 (Gerund as Object of Preposition)
* **经典原句**：*I had to think of a way of preventing him from following me around all morning.*
* **核心解析**：
在介词 `of` 和 `from` 之后，必须使用动名词（doing）形式。
* **结构解析**：`prevent sb. from doing sth.` 是一个非常重要的固定搭配，意为“阻止某人做某事”。注意 `from` 后面接动名词，表示动作的持续状态。

### 3. 动名词的复合结构 (Gerund with Possessive)
* **经典原句**：*'Would you mind my coming with you?' he asked...*
* **核心解析**：
`my coming` 是动名词的复合结构，`my` 是动名词的逻辑主语。
* **结构解析**：在正式英语中，动名词的逻辑主语通常用物主代词（my, your, his等）。但在非正式口语中，也常使用人称代词宾格（me, you, him等）。

## 写作仿写与思辨口语 (Writing Practice & Critical Thinking)

### 1. ✍️ 提分句型仿写 (Pattern Drill)
* **句型名称**：No matter how + adj./adv. + [主谓结构] (无论……多么……)
* **句型公式**：`No matter how + adj./adv. + S + V, ...`
* **仿写练习 1**：无论你工作多么繁忙，都应该抽出时间锻炼身体。 (No matter how busy you are with your work, you should spare some time to exercise.)
* **仿写练习 2**：无论这道题看起来多么简单，你都必须仔细检查。 (No matter how simple the question seems, you must check it carefully.)

### 2. 🔍 近义词/易混词辨析 (Word Discrimination)
* **对比辨析**：**prevent vs. avoid**
* **使用差异解析**：
`avoid` 侧重于“避开、躲开”，指通过行动使自己不接触某人或某事（如：avoid meeting him）。
`prevent` 侧重于“阻止、预防”，指通过采取措施使某事无法发生或某人无法做某事（如：prevent him from following me）。
* **地道表达搭配**：
* avoid a collision (避免碰撞)
* prevent a disease (预防疾病)

### 3. 💬 课后思辨讨论题 (Speaking & Writing Prompts)
* **Question 1**: Is it ever acceptable to tell a "white lie" like Elizabeth did to avoid an awkward social situation? Why?
* **Question 2**: How would you politely but firmly tell someone that you need some personal space when they are being too persistent?
* **高分表达提示 (Hint Words)**: *social etiquette (社交礼仪), white lie (善意的谎言), personal boundaries (个人边界), assertive (坚定自信的), awkward (尴尬的)*
