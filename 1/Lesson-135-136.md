# Lesson 135-136: The Latest Report

## 课文内容 (Text)

Is Karen Marsh going to retire, do you think?
Are you really going to retire, Miss Marsh?
I may.
I can't make up my mind.
I will have to ask my future husband.
He won't let me make another film.
Your future husband, Miss Marsh?
Yes.
Let me introduce him to you.
His name is Carlos.
We're going to get married next week.
Look, Liz!
Here's another report about Karen Marsh.
Listen: 'Karen Marsh: The latest.
At her London Hotel today Miss Marsh told reporters she might retire.
She said she couldn't make up her mind.
She said she would have to ask her future husband.
She said her future husband would not let her make another film.
Then she introduced us to Carlos and told us they would get married next week.'
That's sensational news, isn't it, Kate?
It certainly is.
He'll be her sixth husband!

## 重点词汇及词源背景 (Vocabulary & Etymology)

| 单词 (Word) | 音标 (IPA) | 词性与中文意思 (Meaning) | 词源故事与背景 (Etymology) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **retire** | `/rɪˈtaɪə(r)/` | v. 退休；退役 | 源自法语 *retirer*，由 *re-*（向后）+ *tirer*（拉，拖）组成。原意是“撤退、退后”，引申为从工作岗位上“撤出”，即退休。 |
| **introduce** | `/ˌɪntrəˈdjuːs/` | v. 介绍；引进 | 源自拉丁语 *introducere*，由 *intro-*（向内）+ *ducere*（引导）组成。字面意思是“引导进入”，即把某人带入社交圈或把某物带入市场。 |
| **sensational** | `/senˈseɪʃənl/` | adj. 轰动性的；耸人听闻的 | 源自名词 *sensation*（感觉），词根为拉丁语 *sensus*（感觉）。在新闻语境中，指那些能引起强烈感官刺激或轰动效应的消息。 |
| **certainly** | `/ˈsɜːtnli/` | adv. 当然；肯定地 | 源自古法语 *certain*，最终源于拉丁语 *certus*（确定的）。其核心含义是“没有怀疑的”，在对话中常用于表示坚定的赞同。 |

## 语法要点解析 (Grammar & Key Structures)

### 1. 直接引语变间接引语 (Direct Speech to Indirect Speech)
* **经典原句**：*She said she couldn't make up her mind.*
* **核心解析**：
当引述他人的话时，如果主句谓语动词是过去式（said），从句中的时态通常需要作相应的“向后推移”。例如：`can` 变为 `could`，`will` 变为 `would`。
* **结构解析**：`主语 + said (that) + 从句（时态后移）`。例如：*She said, "I will retire."* -> *She said that she would retire.*

### 2. 动词短语 make up one's mind (下定决心)
* **经典原句**：*I can't make up my mind.*
* **核心解析**：
这是一个固定搭配，意为“决定”、“下决心”。`make up` 在此处意为“组成、形成”，与 `mind`（心智/主意）连用，形象地表达了将各种想法汇聚成一个决定的过程。
* **结构解析**：`make up + 物主代词 + mind`。常用于否定句或疑问句中，表示犹豫不决。

## 写作仿写与思辨口语 (Writing Practice & Critical Thinking)

### 1. ✍️ 提分句型仿写 (Pattern Drill)
* **句型名称**：...told [someone] that... (告诉某人……)
* **句型公式**：`Subject + told + object + that + clause (past tense).`
* **仿写练习 1**：她告诉记者她下周要结婚。(She told the reporters that she would get married next week.)
* **仿写练习 2**：他告诉我们他不能参加聚会。(He told us that he couldn't attend the party.)

### 2. 🔍 近义词/易混词辨析 (Word Discrimination)
* **对比辨析**：**retire vs. resign**
* **使用差异解析**：
  * `retire` 指因年龄、工龄达到标准而**正式退休**，通常伴随着养老金或福利。
  * `resign` 指**辞职**，通常是因为个人原因、不满或寻求新机会而主动放弃职位，不一定与年龄有关。
* **地道表达搭配**：
  * retire from the company (从公司退休)
  * resign from one's post (辞去某人的职务)

### 3. 💬 课后思辨讨论题 (Speaking & Writing Prompts)
* **Question 1**: Do you think celebrities should share their personal lives (like marriage or retirement) with the public? Why or why not?
* **Question 2**: If you were a reporter, what kind of questions would you ask a famous person to get a "sensational" headline?
* **高分表达提示 (Hint Words)**: *privacy (隐私), public interest (公众利益), media coverage (媒体报道), celebrity culture (名人文化), professional boundaries (职业界限)*
