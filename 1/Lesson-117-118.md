# Lesson 117-118: Tommy's Breakfast

## 课文内容 (Text)

When my husband was going into the dining room this morning, he dropped some coins on the floor.
There were coins everywhere.
We looked for them, but we could not find them all.
While we were having breakfast, our little boy, Tommy, found two small coins on the floor.
He put them both into his mouth.
We both tried to get the coins, but it was too late.
Tommy had already swallowed them!
Late that morning, when I was doing the housework, my husband phoned me from the office.
'How's Tommy?' he asked.
'I don't know,' I answered, 'Tommy's been to the toilet three times this morning, but I haven't had any change yet!'

## 语法要点解析 (Grammar & Key Structures)

### 1. 过去进行时与一般过去时的交替 (Past Continuous vs. Past Simple)
* **经典原句**：*When my husband was going into the dining room this morning, he dropped some coins on the floor.*
* **核心解析**：
过去进行时（was going）表示过去某一时刻正在进行的动作，而一般过去时（dropped）表示在该动作过程中发生的瞬间动作。当一个较长的动作被一个较短的动作打断时，通常用 `When + 过去进行时, 一般过去时` 的结构。
* **结构解析**：`When + 主语 + was/were + doing + ..., 主语 + did + ...`

### 2. 过去完成时 (Past Perfect Tense)
* **经典原句**：*Tommy had already swallowed them!*
* **核心解析**：
过去完成时表示在过去某一时间点之前已经发生的动作，即“过去的过去”。在本句中，父母试图抢夺硬币的行为发生在过去，而 Tommy 吞下硬币的行为发生在抢夺之前，因此使用 `had + 过去分词`。
* **结构解析**：`主语 + had + already/yet + 过去分词 (swallowed)`。常与 `already`, `just`, `before` 等词连用，强调动作的完成状态。

## 写作仿写与思辨口语 (Writing Practice & Critical Thinking)

### 1. ✍️ 提分句型仿写 (Pattern Drill)
* **句型名称**：When I was doing..., [someone] did... (当我正在做……时，某人做了……)
* **句型公式**：`When + 主语 + was/were + doing + [动词ing], + 主语 + did + [动词过去式].`
* **仿写练习 1**：当我正在做作业时，电话响了。(When I was doing my homework, the phone rang.)
* **仿写练习 2**：当我正在洗澡时，有人敲门。(When I was taking a shower, someone knocked at the door.)

### 2. 🔍 近义词/易混词辨析 (Word Discrimination)
* **对比辨析**：**housework vs. homework**
* **使用差异解析**：
  * `housework` 指家务劳动，如扫地、洗碗、做饭（cleaning, cooking, laundry）。
  * `homework` 指学校布置的家庭作业（assignments, exercises）。
* **地道表达搭配**：
  * do the housework (做家务)
  * finish one's homework (完成作业)

### 3. 💬 课后思辨讨论题 (Speaking & Writing Prompts)
* **Question 1**: The story ends with a humorous pun about "change". Why do you think the author chose to end the story this way? Does it make the situation less stressful?
* **Question 2**: Accidents involving children often happen at home. What are some common safety precautions parents should take to prevent children from swallowing small objects?
* **高分表达提示 (Hint Words)**: *humor (幽默), pun (双关语), safety hazard (安全隐患), supervision (监督), swallow (吞咽), choking (窒息), preventative measures (预防措施)*
