# Lesson 67-68: The Weekend

## 课文内容 (Text)

Hello. Were you at the butcher's?
Yes, I was.
Were you at the butcher's, too?
No, I wasn't.
I was at the greengrocer's.
How's Jimmy today?
He's very well, thank you.
Was he absent from school last week?
Yes, he was.
He was absent on Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday and Thursday.
How are you all keeping?
Very well, thank you.
We're going to spend three days in the country!
We're going to stay at my mother's for the weekend.
Friday, Saturday and Sunday in the country!
Aren't you lucky!

## 语法要点解析 (Grammar & Key Structures)

### 1. 过去时一般疑问句与回答 (Past Simple Yes/No Questions and Answers)
* **经典原句**：*Were you at the butcher's? - Yes, I was. / No, I wasn't.*
* **核心解析**：
在过去时态中，使用 `be` 动词（was/were）构成一般疑问句。当主语是第一人称（I）或第三人称单数（he, she, it）时，用 `was`；当主语是第二人称（you）或复数（we, they）时，用 `were`。回答时，使用 `Yes, + 主语 + was/were` 或 `No, + 主语 + wasn't/weren't`。
* **结构解析**：`Was/Were + 主语 + (not) + 表语?` -> `Yes, + 主语 + was/were.` 或 `No, + 主语 + wasn't/weren't.`

### 2. be going to 结构表示将来计划 (The 'be going to' Structure for Future Plans)
* **经典原句**：*We're going to spend three days in the country!*
* **核心解析**：
`be going to` 结构用于表达说话人已经计划好的未来行动。它表示一种意图或决心。
* **结构解析**：`主语 + be (am/is/are) + going to + 动词原形 + 其他成分`

## 写作仿写与思辨口语 (Writing Practice & Critical Thinking)

### 1. ✍️ 提分句型仿写 (Pattern Drill)
* **句型名称**：Were you at...? - Yes, I was. / No, I wasn't. (您在……吗？- 是的，我在。/ 不，我不在。)
* **句型公式**：`Were you at + [地点]? - Yes, I was. / No, I wasn't.`
* **仿写练习 1**：你昨天在公园吗？- 是的，我在。 (Were you at the park yesterday? - Yes, I was.)
* **仿写练习 2**：你今天早上在学校吗？- 不，我不在。 (Were you at school this morning? - No, I wasn't.)

### 2. 🔍 近义词/易混词辨析 (Word Discrimination)
* **对比辨析**：**absent vs. away**
* **使用差异解析**：
  * `absent` 通常指因故（如生病、请假）而**缺席**某个地方或活动，尤其是在学校或工作场所。它强调的是“不在场”的状态。
  * `away` 是一个更通用的词，表示“离开”、“不在”某个地方。它可以指去度假、出差，或者仅仅是暂时离开。
* **地道表达搭配**：
  * be absent from school/work (缺席学校/工作)
  * be away on holiday (去度假)
  * be away for the weekend (周末不在家)

### 3. 💬 课后思辨讨论题 (Speaking & Writing Prompts)
* **Question 1**: What are your plans for the upcoming weekend? Where are you going to go and what are you going to do?
* **Question 2**: Imagine you are going to spend three days in the countryside. What are some things you would like to do there? What would you pack?
* **高分表达提示 (Hint Words)**: *plans (计划), activities (活动), relax (放松), explore (探索), nature (大自然), pack (打包), essential items (必需品)*
