# Lesson 123-124: A Trip to Australia

## 课文内容 (Text)

Look, Scott.
This is a photograph I took during my trip to Australia.
Let me see it, Mike.
This is a good photograph.
They're people I met during the trip.
That's the ship we travelled on.
What a beautiful ship!
That's the man I told you about.
Remember?
Ah yes.
The one who offered you a job in Australia.
That's right.
Guess!
That's right.
I grew a beard during the trip, but I shaved it off when I came home.
Why did you shave it off?
My wife didn't like it!

## 语法要点解析 (Grammar & Key Structures)

### 1. 关系代词引导的定语从句 (Relative Clauses)
* **经典原句**：*This is the ship we travelled on.* / *That's the man I told you about.*
* **核心解析**：
当先行词（ship, man）在从句中作介词（on, about）的宾语时，关系代词（that/which/who）在口语中常被省略。这种结构用于对先行词进行补充说明，使句子表达更加紧凑。
* **结构解析**：`先行词 (the ship/man) + (关系代词) + 主语 + 谓语 + 介词`。注意介词通常保留在从句末尾。

### 2. 动词短语的搭配与时态 (Phrasal Verbs & Tense)
* **经典原句**：*I grew a beard during the trip, but I shaved it off when I came home.*
* **核心解析**：
`shave off` 是一个典型的动词+副词短语。当宾语是代词（it）时，代词必须放在动词和副词之间（shave it off），而不能说 shave off it。同时，句子使用一般过去时，描述过去发生的具体动作。
* **结构解析**：`动词 (shave) + 代词宾语 (it) + 副词 (off)`。

## 写作仿写与思辨口语 (Writing Practice & Critical Thinking)

### 1. ✍️ 提分句型仿写 (Pattern Drill)
* **句型名称**：The [noun] + (that) + I [verb]... (我所……的那个……)
* **句型公式**：`The + [名词] + (that) + I + [动词/动词短语]...`
* **仿写练习 1**：这是我昨天买的那本书。(This is the book that I bought yesterday.)
* **仿写练习 2**：那是我在聚会上见到的那个人。(That is the person I met at the party.)

### 2. 🔍 近义词/易混词辨析 (Word Discrimination)
* **对比辨析**：**trip vs. travel vs. journey**
* **使用差异解析**：
  * `trip`：指短途或有特定目的的旅行（如商务出差、度假），强调往返过程。
  * `travel`：是一个广义词，既可作动词也可作名词，指长途跋涉或旅行这一行为本身。
  * `journey`：侧重于从一地到另一地的过程，通常指长途旅行，带有某种艰辛或探索的意味。
* **地道表达搭配**：
  * go on a trip (去旅行)
  * travel abroad (出国旅行)
  * a long journey (漫长的旅程)

### 3. 💬 课后思辨讨论题 (Speaking & Writing Prompts)
* **Question 1**: Why do people like to take photographs during their trips? Do you prefer to capture moments with a camera or experience them with your own eyes?
* **Question 2**: The man in the story shaved off his beard because his wife didn't like it. Do you think we should change our appearance to please others? Why or why not?
* **高分表达提示 (Hint Words)**: *memories (回忆), preserve (保存), personal style (个人风格), self-expression (自我表达), compromise (妥协), aesthetic preference (审美偏好)*
