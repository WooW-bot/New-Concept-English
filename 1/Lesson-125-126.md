# Lesson 125-126: Tea for Two

## 课文内容 (Text)

Can't you come in and have tea now, Peter?
Not yet.
I must water the garden first.
Do you have to water it now?
I'm afraid I must.
Look at it!
It's terribly dry.
What a nuisance!
Last summer it was very dry, too.
Don't you remember?
I had to water it every day.
Well, I'll have tea by myself.
That was quick!
Have you finished already?
Yes.
Look out of the window.
It's raining!
That means you don't need to water the garden.
That was a pleasant surprise.
It means I can have tea, instead.

## 语法要点解析 (Grammar & Key Structures)

### 1. 情态动词 have to 与 must 的辨析 (Must vs. Have to)
* **经典原句**：*I must water the garden first. / Do you have to water it now?*
* **核心解析**：
`must` 通常表示说话人主观认为“有必要”做某事；`have to` 则侧重于客观环境或外部规则要求“不得不”做某事。在疑问句中，通常使用 `Do you have to...?` 来询问对方是否被客观情况所迫。
* **结构解析**：`Must + 动词原形` (主观) vs. `Do/Does + 主语 + have to + 动词原形` (客观/询问)。

### 2. 动词 mean 的用法 (The Usage of 'Mean')
* **经典原句**：*That means you don't need to water the garden.*
* **核心解析**：
`mean` 在此处意为“意味着”，后面常接宾语从句。它常用于解释某个事实所带来的逻辑后果或推论。
* **结构解析**：`主语 (That) + mean(s) + (that) + 从句 (you don't need to...)`。

## 写作仿写与思辨口语 (Writing Practice & Critical Thinking)

### 1. ✍️ 提分句型仿写 (Pattern Drill)
* **句型名称**：That means... (那意味着……)
* **句型公式**：`That means + [从句].`
* **仿写练习 1**：那意味着我们明天不用去学校了。(That means we don't need to go to school tomorrow.)
* **仿写练习 2**：那意味着他已经完成了工作。(That means he has finished the work.)

### 2. 🔍 近义词/易混词辨析 (Word Discrimination)
* **对比辨析**：**must not vs. don't have to**
* **使用差异解析**：
  * `must not` (mustn't) 表示“禁止，千万别”，语气强烈，涉及规则或禁令。
  * `don't have to` 表示“没有必要，不必”，指没有义务或无需做某事。
* **地道表达搭配**：
  * You mustn't smoke here. (你禁止在这里吸烟。)
  * You don't have to pay for this. (你不必为此付钱。)

### 3. 💬 课后思辨讨论题 (Speaking & Writing Prompts)
* **Question 1**: How do you feel when your plans are suddenly changed by external factors (like the weather)? Is it always a bad thing?
* **Question 2**: Do you prefer to do your chores (like watering plants or cleaning) immediately, or do you prefer to wait until the last minute? Why?
* **高分表达提示 (Hint Words)**: *spontaneous (自发的), flexibility (灵活性), unexpected (意想不到的), productivity (生产力), stress management (压力管理), change of plans (计划变更)*
