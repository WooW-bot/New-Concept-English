# Lesson 71-72: He's Awful

## 课文内容 (Text)

He's awful!
How did Pauline answer the telephone at nine o'clock?
He's awful!
He telephoned me four times yesterday and three times the day before yesterday.
He telephoned the office yesterday morning and yesterday afternoon.
My boss answered the telephone.
He said, 'Pauline is typing letters.
She can't speak to you now!'
Then I arrived home at six o'clock yesterday evening.
He telephoned again.
But I didn't answer the phone!
Did he telephone again last night?
Yes, he did.
He telephoned at nine o'clock.
I said, 'This is Pauline's mother.
Please don't telephone my daughter again!'
Did he telephone again?
No, he didn't!

## 语法要点解析 (Grammar & Key Structures)

### 1. 过去简单时态的疑问句与否定句 (Past Simple Questions and Negatives)
* **经典原句**：*Did he telephone again last night? - Yes, he did. / No, he didn't!*
* **核心解析**：
过去简单时态用于描述过去发生的动作或状态。构成疑问句时，需要助动词 `did` 提前，动词恢复原形；构成否定句时，需要助动词 `did not` (didn't) + 动词原形。简略回答使用 `did` 或 `didn't`。
* **结构解析**：
    * 疑问句：`Did + 主语 + 动词原形 + ...?`
    * 否定句：`主语 + did not (didn't) + 动词原形 + ...`

### 2. 过去简单时态的肯定句 (Past Simple Affirmative)
* **经典原句**：*He telephoned me four times yesterday.*
* **核心解析**：
过去简单时态的肯定句通常是在动词词尾添加 `-ed`（规则动词），或者使用不规则动词的过去式形式。这表示动作在过去已经完成。
* **结构解析**：`主语 + 动词过去式 (+ -ed) + ...`

## 写作仿写与思辨口语 (Writing Practice & Critical Thinking)

### 1. ✍️ 提分句型仿写 (Pattern Drill)
* **句型名称**：He/She telephoned... (他/她打电话……)
* **句型公式**：`[主语] + telephoned + [对象] + [时间/频率].`
* **仿写练习 1**：他昨天给我打了三次电话。(He telephoned me three times yesterday.)
* **仿写练习 2**：她上周五给我打了电话。(She telephoned me last Friday.)

### 2. 🔍 近义词/易混词辨析 (Word Discrimination)
* **对比辨析**：**telephone vs. call**
* **使用差异解析**：
  * `telephone`（v.）是一个比较正式的用词，特指使用电话进行通话。
  * `call`（v.）是一个更常用、更口语化的词，可以指通过电话、手机或其他方式进行呼叫或联系。在日常对话中，`call` 更为普遍。
* **地道表达搭配**：
  * give someone a call (给某人打个电话)
  * make a telephone call (打一个电话)

### 3. 💬 课后思辨讨论题 (Speaking & Writing Prompts)
* **Question 1**: Imagine you are Pauline. How would you politely tell someone to stop calling you so often? What are some polite ways to refuse unwanted calls?
* **Question 2**: The story describes someone being very persistent and annoying. What are some strategies for dealing with persistent people in real life, both in personal and professional contexts?
* **高分表达提示 (Hint Words)**: *persistent (坚持不懈的), annoying (恼人的), boundaries (界限), polite refusal (礼貌拒绝), assertiveness (自信果断), harassment (骚扰)*
