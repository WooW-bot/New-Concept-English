# Lesson 127-128: A Famous Actress

## 课文内容 (Text)

Can you recognize that woman, Liz?
I think I can, Kate.
It must be Karen Marsh, the actress.
I thought so.
That must be Conrad Reeves.
Conrad Reeves, the actor?
It can't be.
Let me have another look.
I think you're right!
Isn't he her third husband?
No.
He must be her fourth or fifth.
Doesn't Karen Marsh look old!
She does, doesn't she!
I read she's twenty-nine, but she must be at least forty.
I'm sure she is.
She was a famous actress when I was still at school.
That was a long time ago, wasn't it?
Not that long ago!
I'm not more than twenty-nine myself.

## 语法要点解析 (Grammar & Key Structures)

### 1. 表示推测的情态动词 (Modal Verbs of Deduction)
* **经典原句**：*It must be Karen Marsh, the actress. / It can't be.*
* **核心解析**：
在英语中，我们使用 `must` 表示“很有把握的肯定推测”（译为“一定是”），使用 `can't` 表示“很有把握的否定推测”（译为“不可能是”）。这种推测基于说话人对客观事实的观察和判断。
* **结构解析**：`情态动词 (must/can't) + be + 名词/形容词`。注意：在推测中，`must` 不表示“必须”，而是表示“逻辑上的必然”。

### 2. 反意疑问句 (Tag Questions)
* **经典原句**：*She does, doesn't she! / That was a long time ago, wasn't it?*
* **核心解析**：
反意疑问句用于对陈述句所叙述的事实表示赞同或寻求确认。其基本原则是“前肯后否”或“前否后肯”。当陈述部分是肯定句时，附加疑问部分用否定形式；反之亦然。
* **结构解析**：`陈述句 + 逗号 + be动词/助动词 + not (缩写) + 主语代词?`。若陈述句为肯定，附加疑问句需用否定缩写（如 *doesn't she*）。

## 写作仿写与思辨口语 (Writing Practice & Critical Thinking)

### 1. ✍️ 提分句型仿写 (Pattern Drill)
* **句型名称**：It must be... (那一定是……)
* **句型公式**：`It must be + [人名/名词短语].`
* **仿写练习 1**：看那个人，他一定是我们的新老师。(Look at that man, it must be our new teacher.)
* **仿写练习 2**：那是你的钥匙吗？那一定是你的钥匙。(Is that your key? It must be your key.)

### 2. 🔍 近义词/易混词辨析 (Word Discrimination)
* **对比辨析**：**look vs. seem**
* **使用差异解析**：
  * `look` 侧重于通过**视觉观察**得出的印象，强调外表（如：She looks tired. 她看起来很累）。
  * `seem` 侧重于**主观感觉或逻辑判断**，不仅限于视觉，也可基于某种情况或氛围（如：It seems that they are happy. 看起来他们很幸福）。
* **地道表达搭配**：
  * look old (看起来显老)
  * seem to be (似乎是)

### 3. 💬 课后思辨讨论题 (Speaking & Writing Prompts)
* **Question 1**: Do you think it is polite to gossip about a celebrity's age or personal life in public? Why or why not?
* **Question 2**: How does the media influence our perception of beauty and aging? Is it fair to judge someone based on their appearance?
* **高分表达提示 (Hint Words)**: *public figure (公众人物), privacy (隐私), appearance-oriented (外貌导向的), social pressure (社会压力), superficial (肤浅的), objective (客观的)*
