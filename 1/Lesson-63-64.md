# Lesson 63-64: Thank You, Doctor.

## 课文内容 (Text)

Thank you, doctor.
How's Jimmy today?
Better.
Thank you, doctor.
Can I see him please, Mrs. Williams?
Certainly, doctor.
Come upstairs.
You look very well, Jimmy.
You are better now, but you mustn't get up yet.
You must stay in bed for another two days.
The boy mustn't go to school yet, Mrs. Williams.
And he mustn't eat rich food.
Does he have a temperature, doctor?
No, he doesn't.
Must he stay in bed?
Yes. He must remain in bed for another two days.
He can get up for about two hours each day, but you must keep the room warm.
He's in bed, doctor.
Can you see him please?
He has a bad cold, too!

## 重点词汇及词源背景 (Vocabulary & Etymology)

| 单词 (Word) | 音标 (IPA) | 词性与中文意思 (Meaning) | 词源故事与背景 (Etymology) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **doctor** | `/ˈdɒktə(r)/` | n. 医生 | 源自拉丁语 *docere*（教导）。在古代，*doctor* 指的是“老师”或“学者”。直到中世纪后期，它才开始指代拥有医学博士学位的人，演变为现代英语中“医生”的含义。 |
| **upstairs** | `/ˌʌpˈsteəz/` | adv. 在楼上，往楼上 | 由 *up*（向上）和 *stair*（台阶，源自古英语 *stæger*，意为“攀登”）组成。原指建筑结构中通往上层的台阶，现常用于描述位置或动作方向。 |
| **temperature** | `/ˈtemprətʃə(r)/` | n. 温度；体温 | 源自拉丁语 *temperare*（调节，混合）。古人认为人体健康取决于体内四种“体液”的平衡。当体温升高时，被视为这种平衡被打破，因此用该词指代“体温”。 |
| **remain** | `/rɪˈmeɪn/` | v. 保持，逗留 | 源自拉丁语 *re-*（向后/依然）+ *manere*（停留）。它强调的是在某种状态下“持续存在”或“留下”，在医学语境中常用于强调病人需要持续卧床。 |

## 语法要点解析 (Grammar & Key Structures)

### 1. 情态动词 must 与 mustn't 的用法 (Modal Verbs: Must & Mustn't)
* **经典原句**：*You must stay in bed for another two days. / The boy mustn't go to school yet.*
* **核心解析**：
`must` 表示“必须”，侧重于说话人的主观意志或客观的必要性。`mustn't` 表示“禁止，千万别”，语气比 `don't have to`（不必）强得多，表示如果做了某事会产生负面后果。
* **结构解析**：`主语 + must/mustn't + 动词原形`。注意 `mustn't` 表达的是一种强烈的禁令，而非“不必”。

### 2. 询问身体状况的句型 (Asking about Health)
* **经典原句**：*How's Jimmy today? / Does he have a temperature, doctor?*
* **核心解析**：
询问他人健康状况时，最常用 `How is/are + 人名/代词 + today?`。询问是否发烧时，习惯搭配 `have a temperature`（注意此处 temperature 前通常加不定冠词 a，指代“发烧”这一症状）。
* **结构解析**：`How + be动词 + 主语 + 时间状语?` 或 `Does + 主语 + have a temperature?`

## 写作仿写与思辨口语 (Writing Practice & Critical Thinking)

### 1. ✍️ 提分句型仿写 (Pattern Drill)
* **句型名称**：You must + [动词原形] + [时间/地点]. (你必须……)
* **句型公式**：`You must + [动词原形] + [时间状语/地点状语].`
* **仿写练习 1**：你必须在两点前完成作业。(You must finish your homework before two o'clock.)
* **仿写练习 2**：你必须在图书馆保持安静。(You must keep quiet in the library.)

### 2. 🔍 近义词/易混词辨析 (Word Discrimination)
* **对比辨析**：**mustn't vs. don't have to**
* **使用差异解析**：
  * `mustn't` 表示“禁止，不允许”，强调规则或禁令（如：You mustn't smoke here.）。
  * `don't have to` 表示“不必，没必要”，强调没有义务去做某事（如：You don't have to go to school on Sunday.）。
* **地道表达搭配**：
  * must stay in bed (必须卧床休息)
  * have a high temperature (发高烧)

### 3. 💬 课后思辨讨论题 (Speaking & Writing Prompts)
* **Question 1**: Why is it important to follow a doctor's advice strictly when you are sick? What are the consequences of ignoring medical instructions?
* **Question 2**: In your opinion, what are the most effective ways to stay healthy during the winter season?
* **高分表达提示 (Hint Words)**: *recovery (康复), medical advice (医嘱), immune system (免疫系统), balanced diet (均衡饮食), physical exercise (体育锻炼), strictly follow (严格遵守)*
