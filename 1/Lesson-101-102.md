# Lesson 101-102: A Card From Jimmy

## 课文内容 (Text)

Read Jimmy's card to me please, Penny.
'I have just arrived in Scotland and I'm staying at a Youth Hostel.'
Eh?
He says he's just arrived in Scotland.
He says he's staying at a Youth Hostel.
You know he's a member of the Y.H.A.
The Y.H.A., Mum.
The Youth Hostels Association.
'I'll write a letter soon.
I hope you are all well.'
What? Speak up, Penny.
I'm afraid I can't hear you.
He says he'll write a letter soon.
He hopes we are all well.
'Love, Jimmy.'
Is that all?
He doesn't say very much, does he?
He can't write very much on a card, Mum.

## 语法要点解析 (Grammar & Key Structures)

### 1. 宾语从句的转述 (Reported Speech: Indirect Speech)
* **经典原句**：*He says he's just arrived in Scotland.*
* **核心解析**：
当我们需要转述别人的话时，通常使用宾语从句。在英语中，当主句谓语动词是 `says`（一般现在时）时，从句的时态通常保持原样。本课中 Penny 在转述 Jimmy 的信件内容时，频繁使用 `He says (that)...` 结构。
* **结构解析**：`主语 + says + (that) + 从句主语 + 从句谓语`。注意：在口语中，引导词 `that` 常被省略。

### 2. 反意疑问句 (Tag Questions)
* **经典原句**：*He doesn't say very much, does he?*
* **核心解析**：
反意疑问句用于对陈述的事实进行确认或寻求对方的赞同。其基本原则是“前肯后否，前否后肯”。本句前半部分为否定句 `doesn't say`，因此反意疑问部分使用肯定形式 `does he`。
* **结构解析**：`陈述句部分 (主语 + 助动词/系动词) + 逗号 + 简略疑问部分 (助动词/系动词 + 主语代词)?`

## 写作仿写与思辨口语 (Writing Practice & Critical Thinking)

### 1. ✍️ 提分句型仿写 (Pattern Drill)
* **句型名称**：He says (that) he... (他说他……)
* **句型公式**：`He says (that) he + [动词短语].`
* **仿写练习 1**：他说他正在学习英语。(He says he is studying English.)
* **仿写练习 2**：他说他明天会写信。(He says he will write a letter tomorrow.)

### 2. 🔍 近义词/易混词辨析 (Word Discrimination)
* **对比辨析**：**say vs. tell**
* **使用差异解析**：
  * `say` 侧重于说话的内容本身，后面通常不直接跟人（如 *He says he is well*）。如果一定要跟人，需加 `to`（如 *He says to me*）。
  * `tell` 侧重于告诉某人某事，后面必须跟人（如 *Tell me the truth* 或 *He told me that...*）。
* **地道表达搭配**：
  * say something (说某事)
  * tell a lie / tell a story (撒谎/讲故事)

### 3. 💬 课后思辨讨论题 (Speaking & Writing Prompts)
* **Question 1**: In the digital age, people prefer sending instant messages or emails over postcards. Do you think postcards are still meaningful? Why or why not?
* **Question 2**: The grandmother in the story has trouble hearing. How can we communicate more effectively and patiently with elderly people who have hearing or vision impairments?
* **高分表达提示 (Hint Words)**: *sentimental value (情感价值), handwritten (手写的), instant gratification (即时满足), patience (耐心), empathy (同理心), clear articulation (清晰的表达)*
