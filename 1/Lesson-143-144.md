# Lesson 143-144: A Walk Through the Woods

## 课文内容 (Text)

I live in a very old town which is surrounded by beautiful woods.
It is a famous beauty spot.
On Sundays, hundreds of people come from the city to see our town and to walk through the woods.
Visitors have been asked to keep the woods clean and tidy.
Litter baskets have been placed under the trees, but people still throw their rubbish everywhere.
Last Wednesday, I went for a walk in the woods.
What I saw made me very sad.
I counted seven old cars and three old refrigerators.
The little baskets were empty and the ground was covered with pieces of paper, cigarette ends, old tyres, empty bottles and rusty tins.
Among the rubbish, I found a sign which said, 'Anyone who leaves litter in these woods will be prosecuted!'

## 重点词汇及词源背景 (Vocabulary & Etymology)

| 单词 (Word) | 音标 (IPA) | 词性与中文意思 (Meaning) | 词源故事与背景 (Etymology) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **surround** | `/səˈraʊnd/` | v. 包围，环绕 | 源自古法语 *suronder*，由 *sur-*（在……之上）+ *onder*（波浪）组成，原意是“像波浪一样漫过”，后引申为“从四面八方包围”。 |
| **litter** | `/ˈlɪtə(r)/` | n. 垃圾，废弃物 | 源自古法语 *litiere*，原意是“垫草、褥子”，因为古代人们常把垫在马厩里的干草铺在地上，后来引申为“散落在地上的杂物”。 |

| **prosecute** | `/ˈprɒsɪkjuːt/` | v. 起诉，检举 | 源自拉丁语 *prosequi*，由 *pro-*（向前）+ *sequi*（跟随）组成，字面意思是“追随到底”，在法律语境下指“追究法律责任”。 |

## 语法要点解析 (Grammar & Key Structures)

### 1. 被动语态的现在完成时 (Present Perfect Passive Voice)
* **经典原句**：*Visitors have been asked to keep the woods clean and tidy.*
* **核心解析**：
当动作的执行者不明确或不重要，而强调动作承受者时，使用被动语态。现在完成时的被动语态结构为 `have/has been + 过去分词`，表示过去发生的动作对现在造成的影响或持续的状态。
* **结构解析**：`主语 (Visitors) + have been + 过去分词 (asked) + 不定式补足语 (to keep...)`。

### 2. 定语从句 (Relative Clauses)
* **经典原句**：*I live in a very old town which is surrounded by beautiful woods.*
* **核心解析**：
定语从句用于修饰名词或代词。在此句中，`which` 引导从句修饰先行词 `town`。`which` 在从句中作主语，指代物。
* **结构解析**：`先行词 (town) + 关系代词 (which) + 谓语 (is surrounded) + 其他成分`。

## 写作仿写与思辨口语 (Writing Practice & Critical Thinking)

### 1. ✍️ 提分句型仿写 (Pattern Drill)
* **句型名称**：The ground was covered with... (地面上覆盖着……)
* **句型公式**：`[某地] + be + covered with + [杂物/物质]`
* **仿写练习 1**：沙滩上覆盖着许多贝壳。(The beach was covered with many shells.)
* **仿写练习 2**：桌子上覆盖着厚厚的灰尘。(The table was covered with thick dust.)

### 2. 🔍 近义词/易混词辨析 (Word Discrimination)
* **对比辨析**：**rubbish vs. litter**
* **使用差异解析**：
  * `rubbish` 是英国英语中“垃圾”的总称，指任何无用的东西，也可以指“废话”或“胡说八道”。
  * `litter` 特指在公共场所（如公园、街道）被随意丢弃的纸屑、瓶罐等废弃物，侧重于“乱扔”这一行为后果。
* **地道表达搭配**：
  * *drop litter* (乱扔垃圾)
  * *clear away the rubbish* (清理垃圾)

### 3. 💬 课后思辨讨论题 (Speaking & Writing Prompts)
* **Question 1**: Why do you think people still throw rubbish everywhere even when there are litter baskets nearby? What measures could the town take to solve this problem?
* **Question 2**: Do you think the threat of being "prosecuted" is an effective way to protect the environment? Why or why not?
* **高分表达提示 (Hint Words)**: *environmental awareness (环保意识), civic duty (公民责任), penalty (惩罚), public facilities (公共设施), sustainable lifestyle (可持续生活方式)*
