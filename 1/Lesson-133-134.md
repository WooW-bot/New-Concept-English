# Lesson 133-134: Sensational News

## 课文内容 (Text)

Sensational news!
Have you just made a new film, Miss Marsh?
Yes, I have.
Are you going to make another?
No, I'm not.
I'm going to retire.
I feel very tired.
I don't want to make another film for a long time.
Let's buy a newspaper, Liz.
Listen to this!
'Karen Marsh: Sensational News!
By our reporter, Alan Jones.
Karen Marsh arrived at London Airport today.
She was wearing a blue dress and a mink coat.
She told me she had just made a new film.
She said she was not going to make another.
She said she was going to retire.
She told reporters she felt very tired and didn't want to make another film for a long time.'
I wonder why!

## 语法要点解析 (Grammar & Key Structures)

### 1. 宾语从句中的时态呼应 (Tense Sequence in Reported Speech)
* **经典原句**：*She told me she had just made a new film.*
* **核心解析**：
当主句谓语动词是过去式（如 `told`, `said`）时，宾语从句的时态通常需要向过去推移（时态一致原则）。本句中，直接引语的 `have just made`（现在完成时）在转述时变为 `had just made`（过去完成时）。
* **结构解析**：`主句 (Subject + Past Tense Verb) + that (可省略) + 从句 (Past Perfect/Past Tense)`。

### 2. 意图与计划的表达 (Expressing Future Intentions)
* **经典原句**：*I'm going to retire.* / *She said she was not going to make another.*
* **核心解析**：
`be going to` 结构用于表达说话人经过考虑后的打算或计划。在转述他人计划时，`be` 动词需根据主语和时态进行调整（如 `am going to` 变为 `was going to`）。
* **结构解析**：`主语 + be动词 + going to + 动词原形`。

## 写作仿写与思辨口语 (Writing Practice & Critical Thinking)

### 1. ✍️ 提分句型仿写 (Pattern Drill)
* **句型名称**：She said she was going to... (她说她打算……)
* **句型公式**：`She/He said (that) she/he was going to + [动词原形].`
* **仿写练习 1**：她说她打算下个月去旅行。(She said she was going to travel next month.)
* **仿写练习 2**：他说他打算学习一门新语言。(He said he was going to learn a new language.)

### 2. 🔍 近义词/易混词辨析 (Word Discrimination)
* **对比辨析**：**say vs. tell**
* **使用差异解析**：
  * `say` 强调说话的内容，后接从句时通常不带间接宾语（如 `She said she was tired`）。若要加人，需用 `say to sb.`。
  * `tell` 强调告知某人某事，后必须接人称宾语（如 `She told me/reporters...`）。
* **地道表达搭配**：
  * say: say hello to, say goodbye, say for sure.
  * tell: tell a lie (撒谎), tell the truth (讲真话), tell a story (讲故事).

### 3. 💬 课后思辨讨论题 (Speaking & Writing Prompts)
* **Question 1**: Why do you think celebrities often choose to retire at the peak of their careers? Is it for personal happiness or external pressure?
* **Question 2**: How does the media influence our perception of famous people? Do you think "sensational news" is always reliable?
* **高分表达提示 (Hint Words)**: *public attention (公众关注), privacy (隐私), sensationalism (耸人听闻), mental health (心理健康), credibility (可信度), under the spotlight (在聚光灯下)*
