# Lesson 65-66: Not a Baby

## 课文内容 (Text)

I'm going to meet some friends, Dad.
You mustn't come home late.
You must be home at half past ten.
I can't get home so early, Dad!
Can I have the key to the front door, please?
No, you can't.
Jill's eighteen years old, Tom.
She's not a baby.
Give her the key.
She always comes home early.
Oh, all right!
Here you are.
But you mustn't come home after a quarter past eleven.
Do you hear?
Yes, Dad.
Thanks, Mum.
That's all right.
Goodbye.
Enjoy yourself!
We always enjoy ourselves, Mum.
Bye-bye.

## 语法要点解析 (Grammar & Key Structures)

### 1. 情态动词 must 与 mustn't 的用法 (Modal Verbs: Obligation and Prohibition)
* **经典原句**：*You must be home at half past ten. / You mustn't come home late.*
* **核心解析**：
`must` 表示主观的必要性或义务，意为“必须”。而 `mustn't`（must not）则表示强烈的禁止，意为“禁止”、“不准”，语气比 `don't have to`（不必）要严厉得多。
* **结构解析**：`Subject + must/mustn't + verb (原形) + ...`

### 2. 反身代词的用法 (Reflexive Pronouns)
* **经典原句**：*Enjoy yourself! / We always enjoy ourselves, Mum.*
* **核心解析**：
当动作的执行者（主语）和动作的承受者（宾语）为同一个人时，需要使用反身代词。`Enjoy oneself` 是一个固定搭配，意为“玩得开心”。注意单复数的变化：Jill 是单数，用 `yourself`（对话中 Mum 对 Jill 说）；We 是复数，用 `ourselves`。
* **结构解析**：`Verb + reflexive pronoun (myself, yourself, himself, herself, ourselves, yourselves, themselves)`

## 写作仿写与思辨口语 (Writing Practice & Critical Thinking)

### 1. ✍️ 提分句型仿写 (Pattern Drill)
* **句型名称**：You mustn't + [动词原形] (你禁止做……)
* **句型公式**：`You mustn't + [verb] + [time/place/object].`
* **仿写练习 1**：你禁止在图书馆大声喧哗。 (You mustn't speak loudly in the library.)
* **仿写练习 2**：你禁止在考试中作弊。 (You mustn't cheat in the exam.)

### 2. 🔍 近义词/易混词辨析 (Word Discrimination)
* **对比辨析**：**mustn't vs. don't have to**
* **使用差异解析**：
  * `mustn't` 表示**禁止**，即“绝对不能做”（Prohibition）。
  * `don't have to` 表示**没有必要**，即“做不做都可以，但没必要做”（Lack of obligation）。
* **地道表达搭配**：
  * You mustn't touch that! (你绝对不能碰那个！——禁止)
  * You don't have to come if you're busy. (如果你忙的话，不必非得来。——没必要)

### 3. 💬 课后思辨讨论题 (Speaking & Writing Prompts)
* **Question 1**: At what age do you think a person should be considered an adult? What are the responsibilities that come with being "not a baby"?
* **Question 2**: How do you balance the need for independence with the rules set by your parents or guardians? Is communication the key?
* **高分表达提示 (Hint Words)**: *independence (独立), responsibility (责任), curfew (宵禁), mutual trust (相互信任), mature (成熟), set boundaries (设定界限)*
