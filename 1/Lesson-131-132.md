# Lesson 131-132: Don't be So Sure

## 课文内容 (Text)

Don't be so sure!
We may go abroad.
I'm not sure.
My wife wants to go to Egypt.
I'd like to go there, too.
We can't make up our minds.
Will you travel by sea or by air?
We may travel by sea.
It's cheaper, isn't it?
It may be cheaper, but it takes a long time.
I'm sure you'll enjoy yourselves.
Don't be so sure.
We might not go anywhere.
My wife always worries too much.
We have this problem every year.
In the end, we stay at home and look after everything!

## 语法要点解析 (Grammar & Key Structures)

### 1. 表示推测的情态动词 may 与 might (Modal Verbs of Possibility)
* **经典原句**：*We may go abroad. / We might not go anywhere.*
* **核心解析**：
`may` 和 `might` 均可表示“可能性”，意为“也许、可能”。`might` 是 `may` 的过去式，但在现代英语中，`might` 常用于表示比 `may` 更小的不确定性，语气更加委婉、谨慎。
* **结构解析**：`情态动词 (may/might) + 动词原形`。在否定句中，通常在情态动词后加 `not`，如 `might not`。

### 2. 反意疑问句 (Tag Questions)
* **经典原句**：*It's cheaper, isn't it?*
* **核心解析**：
反意疑问句用于对陈述句所说的事实提出疑问，寻求对方的确认。其基本原则是“前肯后否”或“前否后肯”。
* **结构解析**：`陈述句部分 (It is cheaper) + 逗号 + 简略问句 (isn't it?)`。如果陈述句谓语是 `be` 动词，反意部分也用 `be` 动词；如果陈述句主语是代词，反意部分直接沿用。

## 写作仿写与思辨口语 (Writing Practice & Critical Thinking)

### 1. ✍️ 提分句型仿写 (Pattern Drill)
* **句型名称**：Make up one's mind (下定决心/做出决定)
* **句型公式**：`Subject + make(s) up one's mind to do something.`
* **仿写练习 1**：他们还没决定去哪里度假。(They haven't made up their minds where to go on holiday.)
* **仿写练习 2**：我下定决心要学好英语。(I have made up my mind to learn English well.)

### 2. 🔍 近义词/易混词辨析 (Word Discrimination)
* **对比辨析**：**sure vs. certain**
* **使用差异解析**：
  * `sure` 侧重于个人的“主观感觉”或“信心”，常用于表达“我确信/我有把握”，如 *I'm sure you'll enjoy it.*
  * `certain` 侧重于“客观事实”或“证据确凿”，语气比 `sure` 更强，表示“毫无疑问”。
* **地道表达搭配**：
  * Be sure to do sth. (务必做某事)
  * Make sure that... (确保……)

### 3. 💬 课后思辨讨论题 (Speaking & Writing Prompts)
* **Question 1**: Do you prefer to plan your trips in detail, or do you like to be spontaneous and "go with the flow"? Why?
* **Question 2**: The text mentions that the couple worries too much and ends up staying home. Is it better to take risks and travel, or is it safer to stay in your comfort zone?
* **高分表达提示 (Hint Words)**: *spontaneous (自发的/随性的), comfort zone (舒适区), itinerary (行程表), hesitation (犹豫), seize the day (活在当下), unpredictable (不可预测的)*
