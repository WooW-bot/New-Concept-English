# Lesson 91-92: Poor Ian

## 课文内容 (Text)

Poor Ian!
Has Ian sold his house yet?
Yes, he has.
He sold it last week.
Has he moved to his new house yet?
No, not yet.
He's still here.
He's going to move tomorrow.
When? Tomorrow morning?
No. Tomorrow afternoon.
I'll miss him.
He has always been a good neighbour.
He's a very nice person.
We'll all miss him.
When will the new people move into this house?
I think that they'll move in the day after tomorrow.
Will you see Ian today, Jenny?
Yes, I will.
Please give him my regards.
Poor Ian! He didn't want to leave this house.
No, he didn't want to leave, but his wife did!

## 语法要点解析 (Grammar & Key Structures)

### 1. 现在完成时与 yet 的用法 (Present Perfect with 'yet')
* **经典原句**：*Has Ian sold his house yet? - Yes, he has.*
* **核心解析**：
现在完成时（have/has + 过去分词）常用于询问“到目前为止”是否已经发生了某事。`yet` 常用于疑问句和否定句中，放在句末，表示“已经”或“还（没）”。
* **结构解析**：`Has/Have + 主语 + 过去分词 + 宾语 + yet?` -> `Yes, 主语 + have/has.` 或 `No, 主语 + haven't/hasn't + yet.`

### 2. 一般将来时：be going to 与 will (Future Tense)
* **经典原句**：*He's going to move tomorrow. / I think that they'll move in...*
* **核心解析**：
`be going to` 常用于表达“计划、打算”做某事，侧重于事先的安排；`will` 常用于表达“意愿”或“对未来的预测”。本课中两者交替使用，体现了对搬家这一事件的不同侧重。
* **结构解析**：`主语 + be going to + 动词原形` (计划)；`主语 + will + 动词原形` (预测/意愿)。

## 写作仿写与思辨口语 (Writing Practice & Critical Thinking)

### 1. ✍️ 提分句型仿写 (Pattern Drill)
* **句型名称**：Please give him/her my regards. (请代我向他/她问好。)
* **句型公式**：`Please give + [宾语] + my regards.`
* **仿写练习 1**：请代我向你的父母问好。 (Please give your parents my regards.)
* **仿写练习 2**：请代我向你的老师问好。 (Please give your teacher my regards.)

### 2. 🔍 近义词/易混词辨析 (Word Discrimination)
* **对比辨析**：**miss vs. lose**
* **使用差异解析**：
  * `miss` 侧重于情感上的“想念”或动作上的“错过”（如 miss the bus, miss a person）。
  * `lose` 侧重于客观上的“丢失”或“失去”（如 lose one's keys, lose a game）。
* **地道表达搭配**：
  * miss someone dearly (非常想念某人)
  * lose one's way (迷路)

### 3. 💬 课后思辨讨论题 (Speaking & Writing Prompts)
* **Question 1**: Moving to a new house can be both exciting and stressful. What are the biggest challenges people face when moving to a new neighborhood?
* **Question 2**: Ian didn't want to leave, but his wife did. How should couples handle disagreements about major life decisions like moving or changing jobs?
* **高分表达提示 (Hint Words)**: *sentimental attachment (情感依恋), compromise (妥协), fresh start (新的开始), comfort zone (舒适区), decision-making (决策)*
