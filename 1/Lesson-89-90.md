# Lesson 89-90: For Sale

## 课文内容 (Text)

Good afternoon.
I believe that this house is for sale.
That's right.
May I have a look at it, please?
Yes, of course.
Come in.
How long have you lived here?
I've lived here for twenty years.
Twenty years!
That's a long time.
Yes, I've been here since 1976.
Then why do you want to sell it?
Because I've just retired.
I want to buy a small house in the country.
How much does this house cost?
￡68, 500.
That's a lot of money!
It's worth every penny of it.
Well, I like the house, but I can't decide yet.
My wife must see it first.
Women always have the last word.

## 语法要点解析 (Grammar & Key Structures)

### 1. 现在完成时：for 与 since 的用法 (Present Perfect with 'for' and 'since')
* **经典原句**：*I've lived here for twenty years. / I've been here since 1976.*
* **核心解析**：
现在完成时（have/has + 过去分词）常用于表示从过去某一时间开始，一直持续到现在的动作或状态。`for` 后接一段时间（如 twenty years），表示持续的时长；`since` 后接时间点（如 1976），表示动作开始的时间。
* **结构解析**：`主语 + have/has + 过去分词 + for + 时间段` 或 `since + 时间点`。

### 2. 情态动词 must 表示必要性 (Modal Verb 'must')
* **经典原句**：*My wife must see it first.*
* **核心解析**：
`must` 在此处表示“必须”，强调主观上的必要性或义务。在肯定句中，它表达说话人认为某事是不可或缺的步骤。
* **结构解析**：`主语 + must + 动词原形`。在否定句中 `mustn't` 表示“禁止”，但在本课语境下，它仅用于陈述必须完成的动作。

## 写作仿写与思辨口语 (Writing Practice & Critical Thinking)

### 1. ✍️ 提分句型仿写 (Pattern Drill)
* **句型名称**：How long have you...? (你……有多久了？)
* **句型公式**：`How long have you + [过去分词] + [地点/状态]?`
* **仿写练习 1**：你在这家公司工作多久了？ (How long have you worked in this company?)
* **仿写练习 2**：你学习英语多久了？ (How long have you studied English?)

### 2. 🔍 近义词/易混词辨析 (Word Discrimination)
* **对比辨析**：**cost vs. worth**
* **使用差异解析**：
  * `cost` 是动词或名词，侧重于“价格、花费”，即买东西时实际支付的金额（How much does it cost?）。
  * `worth` 是形容词，侧重于“价值、值得”，即某物本身的价值是否与价格匹配（It is worth the money）。
* **地道表达搭配**：
  * cost a fortune (花一大笔钱)
  * be worth doing something (值得做某事)

### 3. 💬 课后思辨讨论题 (Speaking & Writing Prompts)
* **Question 1**: If you were planning to buy a house, what are the top three factors you would consider? (e.g., location, price, size). Why?
* **Question 2**: The text mentions "Women always have the last word." Do you think this is a fair stereotype? How do you make important decisions in your family?
* **高分表达提示 (Hint Words)**: *investment (投资), environment (环境), compromise (妥协), decision-making (决策), consensus (共识), budget (预算)*
