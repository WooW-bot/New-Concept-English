# Lesson 115-116: Knock, Knock

## 课文内容 (Text)

Knock, knock!
Isn't there anyone at home?
I'll knock again, Helen.
Everything's very quiet.
I'm sure there's no one at home.
But that's impossible.
Carol and Tom invited us to lunch.
Look through the window.
Can you see anything?
Nothing at all.
Let's try the back door.
Look! Everyone's in the garden.
Hello, Helen. Hello, Jim.
Everybody wants to have lunch in the garden.
It's nice and warm out here.
Come and have something to drink.
Thanks, Carol.
May I have a glass of beer please?
Beer?
There's none left.
You can have some lemonade.
Lemonade!
Don't believe her, Jim.
She's only joking.
Have some beer!

## 重点词汇及词源背景 (Vocabulary & Etymology)

| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **impossible** | `/ɪmˈpɒsəbl/` | adj. 不可能的 | 源自拉丁语 *im-*（不）+ *possibilis*（可能的）。*possibilis* 源于动词 *posse*（能够）。字面意思是“无法做到的”，在英语中常用于表达对某事发生的怀疑或惊讶。 |

## 语法要点解析 (Grammar & Key Structures)

### 1. 不定代词的用法 (Usage of Indefinite Pronouns)
* **经典原句**：*Isn't there anyone at home? / I'm sure there's no one at home.*
* **核心解析**：
不定代词 `anyone`, `someone`, `everyone`, `no one` 等在句中作主语时，谓语动词通常用单数形式。`anyone` 常用于疑问句或否定句中，而 `no one` 本身带有否定含义，句中谓语动词需用肯定形式。
* **结构解析**：`There is + [不定代词] + [地点状语]`。注意 `no one` 相当于 `not anyone`，在 `there be` 句型中，`there is no one` 比 `there isn't anyone` 语气更强。

### 2. 复合不定代词的定语后置 (Post-position of Adjectives)
* **经典原句**：*Come and have something to drink.*
* **核心解析**：
当形容词修饰 `something`, `anything`, `nothing`, `everything` 等复合不定代词时，形容词必须放在这些代词的后面。
* **结构解析**：`不定代词 (something/anything) + 形容词/不定式 (to drink/cold)`。例如：*something hot*（热的东西），*nothing special*（没什么特别的）。

## 写作仿写与思辨口语 (Writing Practice & Critical Thinking)

### 1. ✍️ 提分句型仿写 (Pattern Drill)
* **句型名称**：There is + [不定代词] + [地点] (某处有/没有人)
* **句型公式**：`There is + [someone/anyone/no one] + [in/at/on + 地点]`
* **仿写练习 1**：教室里有人吗？ (Is there anyone in the classroom?)
* **仿写练习 2**：办公室里没有人。 (There is no one in the office.)

### 2. 🔍 近义词/易混词辨析 (Word Discrimination)
* **对比辨析**：**anyone vs. someone**
* **使用差异解析**：
  * `someone` 通常用于肯定句，表示“某人”，带有一定的确定性。
  * `anyone` 通常用于疑问句或否定句，表示“任何人”，带有不确定性。但在表示“任何人都可以”的肯定句中，`anyone` 也可使用。
* **地道表达搭配**：
  * *Is there anyone here?* (这儿有人吗？)
  * *Someone is knocking at the door.* (有人在敲门。)

### 3. 💬 课后思辨讨论题 (Speaking & Writing Prompts)
* **Question 1**: Why do you think Carol and Tom were in the garden instead of the house? How does the setting (garden vs. house) change the atmosphere of a social gathering?
* **Question 2**: Have you ever been "joked" by a friend? How do you distinguish between a friendly joke and something that might be offensive?
* **高分表达提示 (Hint Words)**: *hospitality (好客), social gathering (社交聚会), atmosphere (氛围), misunderstand (误解), sense of humor (幽默感), etiquette (礼仪)*
