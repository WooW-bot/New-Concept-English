# Lesson 119-120: A True Story

## 课文内容 (Text)

I want to tell you a true story.
It happened to a friend of mine a year ago.
While my friend, George, was reading in bed, two thieves climbed into his kitchen.
After they had entered the house, they went into the dining room.
It was very dark, so they turned on a torch.
Suddenly, they heard a voice behind them.
'What's up? What's up?' someone called.
The thieves dropped the torch and ran away as quickly as they could.
George heard the noise and came downstairs quickly.
He turned on the light, but he couldn't see anyone.
The thieves had already gone.
But George's parrot, Henry, was still there.
'What's up, George?' he called.
'Nothing, Henry,' George said and smiled.
'Go back to sleep.'

## 语法要点解析 (Grammar & Key Structures)

### 1. 过去进行时与一般过去时的结合 (Past Continuous vs. Simple Past)
* **经典原句**：*While my friend, George, was reading in bed, two thieves climbed into his kitchen.*
* **核心解析**：
当一个较长的动作（正在阅读）进行时，另一个较短的动作（闯入）突然发生，通常用 `while` 引导从句表示背景动作（过去进行时），主句用一般过去时表示突发动作。
* **结构解析**：`While + 主语 + was/were + doing (过去进行时), + 主语 + did (一般过去时).`

### 2. 过去完成时 (Past Perfect Tense)
* **经典原句**：*The thieves had already gone.*
* **核心解析**：
过去完成时用于表示“过去的过去”，即在过去某个时间点之前已经完成的动作。在本课中，乔治下楼时，小偷已经离开，离开这一动作发生在乔治下楼之前，故使用 `had + 过去分词`。
* **结构解析**：`主语 + had + 过去分词 (past participle).` 常与 `already`, `yet`, `before` 等时间状语连用。

## 写作仿写与思辨口语 (Writing Practice & Critical Thinking)

### 1. ✍️ 提分句型仿写 (Pattern Drill)
* **句型名称**：While..., ... (当……的时候，……)
* **句型公式**：`While + [主语] + was/were + [doing], [主语] + [did].`
* **仿写练习 1**：当我在洗澡时，电话响了。(While I was taking a shower, the phone rang.)
* **仿写练习 2**：当我们正在看电影时，突然停电了。(While we were watching a movie, the power went out suddenly.)

### 2. 🔍 近义词/易混词辨析 (Word Discrimination)
* **对比辨析**：**torch vs. flashlight**
* **使用差异解析**：
  * `torch` 是英式英语，指手电筒，但在美式英语中它通常指“火炬”或“喷灯”。
  * `flashlight` 是美式英语，专门指手电筒。在国际交流中，若在英国使用 *flashlight*，对方也能听懂，但使用 *torch* 会显得更地道。
* **地道表达搭配**：
  * turn on a torch (打开手电筒)
  * carry a torch for someone (暗恋某人/对某人念念不忘，引申义)

### 3. 💬 课后思辨讨论题 (Speaking & Writing Prompts)
* **Question 1**: If you were George and heard a voice in your house at night, what would be your first reaction? Would you confront the intruder or call the police?
* **Question 2**: Do you think pets like parrots or dogs can be effective "home security systems"? Why or why not?
* **高分表达提示 (Hint Words)**: *intruder (入侵者), security (安全), instinct (本能), confrontation (对峙), alert (警觉), emergency (紧急情况), peace of mind (安心)*
