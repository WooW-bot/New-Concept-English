# Lesson 121-122: The Man in a Hat

## 课文内容 (Text)

"I bought two expensive dictionaries here half an hour ago, but I forgot to take them with me."
"Which books did you buy?"
"The books which are on the counter."
"Did you serve this gentleman half an hour ago, Caroline?"
"He says he's the man who bought these books."
"I can't remember. The man who I served was wearing a hat."
"Have you got a hat, sir?"
"Yes, I have."
"Would you put it on, please?"
"All right."
"Is this the man that you served, Caroline?"
"Yes. I recognize him now."

## 语法要点解析 (Grammar & Key Structures)

### 1. 关系代词引导的定语从句 (Relative Clauses with who/that/which)
* **经典原句**：*The man who I served was wearing a hat.*
* **核心解析**：
定语从句用于修饰先行词（名词）。当先行词是人时，常用 `who` 或 `that`；当先行词是物时，常用 `which` 或 `that`。在本课中，定语从句帮助我们精确锁定“哪个人”或“哪本书”。
* **结构解析**：`先行词 (The man) + 关系代词 (who) + 从句 (I served) + 谓语 (was wearing...)`。

### 2. 祈使句的委婉表达 (Polite Imperatives)
* **经典原句**：*Would you put it on, please?*
* **核心解析**：
直接使用祈使句（如 "Put it on!"）显得生硬且不礼貌。在英语中，常使用 `Would you... please?` 或 `Could you... please?` 这种结构，将祈使句转化为委婉的请求，以体现社交礼仪。
* **结构解析**：`Would you + 动词原形 + 宾语 + please?`。这是在服务行业或日常交流中非常地道的提议方式。

## 写作仿写与思辨口语 (Writing Practice & Critical Thinking)

### 1. ✍️ 提分句型仿写 (Pattern Drill)
* **句型名称**：The [person/thing] + who/which + [clause] (……的……)
* **句型公式**：`The + [名词] + (who/which) + [主语 + 谓语]...`
* **仿写练习 1**：那个正在唱歌的女孩是我的妹妹。(The girl who is singing is my sister.)
* **仿写练习 2**：我昨天买的那本书很有趣。(The book which I bought yesterday is very interesting.)

### 2. 🔍 近义词/易混词辨析 (Word Discrimination)
* **对比辨析**：**remember vs. remind**
* **使用差异解析**：
  * `remember` 意为“记得，记起”，侧重于主语自身的记忆能力（如：I can't remember. 我记不起来了）。
  * `remind` 意为“提醒”，侧重于通过外力或提示让某人想起某事（如：Please remind me to call him. 请提醒我给他打电话）。
* **地道表达搭配**：
  * remember to do sth. (记得去做某事)
  * remind sb. of sth. (提醒某人某事)

### 3. 💬 课后思辨讨论题 (Speaking & Writing Prompts)
* **Question 1**: Why did Caroline have trouble recognizing the customer at first? How does our appearance (like wearing a hat or glasses) affect how others identify us?
* **Question 2**: In a service environment, what are some effective ways to handle a situation where a customer claims they left something behind but you are not sure?
* **高分表达提示 (Hint Words)**: *visual cues (视觉线索), identity (身份), memory lapse (记忆缺失), professional integrity (职业操守), verify (核实), customer service (客户服务)*
