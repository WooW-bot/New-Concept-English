# Lesson 111-112: The Most Expensive Model

## 课文内容 (Text)

I like this television very much.
How much does it cost?
It's the most expensive model in the shop.
It costs five hundred pounds.
That's too expensive for us.
We can't afford all that money.
This model's less expensive than that one.
It's only three hundred pounds.
But, of course, it's not as good as the expensive one.
I don't like this model.
The other model's more expensive, but it's worth the money.
Can we buy it on instalments?
Of course.
You can pay a deposit of thirty pounds,
and then fourteen pounds a month for three years.
Do you like it, dear?
I certainly do, but I don't like the price.
You always want the best, but we can't afford it.
Sometimes you think you're a millionaire!
Millionaires don't buy things on instalments!

## 重点词汇及词源背景 (Vocabulary & Etymology)

| 单词 (Word) | 音标 (IPA) | 词性与中文意思 (Meaning) | 词源故事与背景 (Etymology) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **expensive** | `/ɪkˈspensɪv/` | adj. 昂贵的 | 源自拉丁语 *expensum*（支出），由 *ex-*（向外）+ *pendere*（支付/称重）组成。古罗马时期人们用称重金属的方式来支付，引申为“花费巨大的”。 |
| **afford** | `/əˈfɔːd/` | v. 买得起，担负得起 | 源自古英语 *aforthian*，由 *a-*（加强语气）+ *forth*（向前）组成。原意为“推进、完成”，后引申为“有能力提供或支付某物”。 |
| **deposit** | `/dɪˈpɒzɪt/` | n. 押金，订金 | 源自拉丁语 *depositum*，由 *de-*（向下）+ *ponere*（放置）组成，字面意思是“放下、存放”。在商业中指为了预订商品而先“放下”的一笔钱。 |
| **millionaire** | `/ˌmɪljəˈneə(r)/` | n. 百万富翁 | 源自法语 *million*（一百万），加上后缀 *-aire*（表示人）。在18世纪的法国和英国，拥有百万货币单位的人被视为极度富有，该词随之流行。 |

## 语法要点解析 (Grammar & Key Structures)

### 1. 形容词的比较等级 (Comparative and Superlative Degrees)
* **经典原句**：*It's the most expensive model in the shop. / This model's less expensive than that one.*
* **核心解析**：
形容词比较等级用于对事物进行对比。多音节形容词（如 expensive）通过在前面加 `more/most` 表示最高级或比较级，或用 `less` 表示“较不……”。
* **结构解析**：
  * 最高级：`the + most + 形容词原级`（表示“最……”）。
  * 比较级：`less + 形容词原级 + than`（表示“比……不那么……”）。

### 2. 同级比较结构 (The "as...as" Structure)
* **经典原句**：*It's not as good as the expensive one.*
* **核心解析**：
`as...as` 结构用于表示两者在某方面程度相同。在否定句中，`not as...as` 表示前者不如后者。注意 `as` 之间必须使用形容词或副词的**原级**。
* **结构解析**：`主语 + be动词 + not + as + 形容词原级 + as + 比较对象`。

## 写作仿写与思辨口语 (Writing Practice & Critical Thinking)

### 1. ✍️ 提分句型仿写 (Pattern Drill)
* **句型名称**：...is not as [adj.] as... (……不如……那么……)
* **句型公式**：`[Subject A] + is not as + [Adjective] + as + [Subject B].`
* **仿写练习 1**：这件衬衫不如那件衬衫好看。(This shirt is not as nice as that one.)
* **仿写练习 2**：今天的天气不如昨天暖和。(Today's weather is not as warm as yesterday's.)

### 2. 🔍 近义词/易混词辨析 (Word Discrimination)
* **对比辨析**：**expensive vs. dear**
* **使用差异解析**：
  * `expensive` 是最常用的词，指价格高，侧重于客观的“花费大”。
  * `dear` 在英式英语中常用于表示“昂贵的”，但带有主观色彩，常用于感叹价格高得让人心疼（如：Oh, that's very dear!）。
* **地道表达搭配**：
  * expensive taste (昂贵的品味/追求高档)
  * Oh dear! (哎呀！——此时 dear 表示亲爱的或感叹，与价格无关)

### 3. 💬 课后思辨讨论题 (Speaking & Writing Prompts)
* **Question 1**: Do you think buying things on "instalments" (paying in parts) is a good way to manage money? Why or why not?
* **Question 2**: Is it always better to buy the "most expensive" model? What factors should we consider before making a big purchase?
* **高分表达提示 (Hint Words)**: *financial burden (经济负担), budget (预算), quality over quantity (重质不重量), long-term investment (长期投资), impulse buying (冲动消费)*
