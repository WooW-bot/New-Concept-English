# Lesson 129-130: Seventy Miles an Hour

## 课文内容 (Text)

Look, Gary!
That policeman's waving to you.
He wants you to stop.
On a race track?
You must have been driving at seventy miles an hour.
I can't have been.
I was doing eighty when I overtook you.
Didn't you see the speed limit?
I'm afraid I didn't, officer.
I must have been dreaming.
He wasn't dreaming, officer.
I was telling him to drive slowly.
That's why I didn't see the sign.
Let me see your driving licence.
I won't charge you this time.
But you'd better not do it again!
Thank you.
I'll certainly be more careful.
I told you to drive slowly, Gary.
You always tell me to drive slowly, darling.
Well, next time you'd better take my advice!

## 重点词汇及词源背景 (Vocabulary & Etymology)

| 单词 (Word) | 音标 (IPA) | 词性与中文意思 (Meaning) | 词源故事与背景 (Etymology) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **overtake** | `/ˌəʊvəˈteɪk/` | v. 超车；赶上 | 由 *over-*（越过）+ *take*（拿，取）组成。原意指在路上“拿走”前面的位置，即“超过”。在交通语境中，它特指车辆在行驶中超越前车。 |
| **dream** | `/driːm/` | v. 做梦；梦想 | 源自古英语 *drēam*（欢喜，音乐），后受低地德语影响演变为指代睡眠中的幻象。在课文中，Gary用“dreaming”来表示自己注意力不集中，是一种地道的借口。 |
| **licence** | `/ˈlaɪsns/` | n. 执照，许可证 | 源自拉丁语 *licentia*（自由，许可），与 *licere*（被允许）同源。它代表了法律赋予个人的某种“自由”或“特权”，如驾驶执照（driving licence）。 |
| **advice** | `/ədˈvaɪs/` | n. 建议，忠告 | 源自古法语 *avis*，最初意为“观点”或“看法”。它与 *advise*（动词）不同，注意结尾是 /s/ 音。在英语中，它是不可数名词，不能说 an advice。 |

## 语法要点解析 (Grammar & Key Structures)

### 1. 表示对过去情况的推测 (Modal Verbs for Past Deduction)
* **经典原句**：*You must have been driving at seventy miles an hour. I can't have been.*
* **核心解析**：
当我们要对“过去”发生的事情进行推测时，使用 `情态动词 + have + 过去分词` 结构。
* `must have done`：表示对过去事情的肯定推测（一定做了……）。
* `can't have done`：表示对过去事情的否定推测（不可能做了……）。
* **结构解析**：`情态动词 (must/can't) + have + been + v-ing`（表示对过去正在进行动作的推测）。

### 2. 祈使句的委婉表达与建议 (Advice and Warnings)
* **经典原句**：*But you'd better not do it again!*
* **核心解析**：
`had better`（常缩写为 `'d better`）用于表示建议或警告，语气比 `should` 更强，通常带有“如果不这样做就会产生不良后果”的暗示。
* **结构解析**：`had better (not) + 动词原形`。注意在否定句中，`not` 必须放在 `better` 之后，而不是 `had` 之后。

## 写作仿写与思辨口语 (Writing Practice & Critical Thinking)

### 1. ✍️ 提分句型仿写 (Pattern Drill)
* **句型名称**：You'd better (not) do... (你最好（不要）做……)
* **句型公式**：`You'd better + [动词原形] / You'd better not + [动词原形]`
* **仿写练习 1**：你最好现在就走，否则会迟到的。(You'd better leave now, or you'll be late.)
* **仿写练习 2**：你最好不要在开车时看手机。(You'd better not look at your phone while driving.)

### 2. 🔍 近义词/易混词辨析 (Word Discrimination)
* **对比辨析**：**advice vs. advise**
* **使用差异解析**：
  * `advice` 是名词，不可数，表示“建议”的内容。常用搭配：*give someone some advice* (给某人建议), *take one's advice* (听从某人的建议)。
  * `advise` 是动词，表示“建议”这个动作。常用搭配：*advise someone to do something* (建议某人做某事)。
* **地道表达搭配**：
  * *Ask for advice* (征求建议)
  * *Follow my advice* (听从我的建议)

### 3. 💬 课后思辨讨论题 (Speaking & Writing Prompts)
* **Question 1**: Why do you think speed limits are strictly enforced? Do you believe they are always reasonable?
* **Question 2**: In the story, Gary's wife gives him advice, but he ignores it until he gets into trouble. Why is it often difficult for people to take advice from those closest to them?
* **高分表达提示 (Hint Words)**: *traffic regulations (交通法规), road safety (道路安全), speed limit (限速), subjective (主观的), objective (客观的), take advice (听从建议), consequences (后果)*
