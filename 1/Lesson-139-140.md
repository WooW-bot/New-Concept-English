# Lesson 139-140: Is That You, John

## 课文内容 (Text)

Yes, speaking.
Tell Mary we'll be late for dinner this evening.
I'm afraid I don't understand.
Hasn't Mary told you?
She invited Charlotte and me to dinner this evening.
I said I would be at your house at six o'clock, but the boss wants me to do some extra work.
I'll have to stay at the office.
I don't know when I'll finish.
Oh, and by the way, my wife wants to know if Mary needs any help.
I don't know what you're talking about.
That is John Smith, isn't it?
Yes, I'm John Smith.
You are John Smith, the engineer, aren't you?
That's right.
You work for the Overseas Engineering Company, don't you?
No, I don't.
I'm John Smith the telephone engineer and I'm repairing your telephone line.

## 语法要点解析 (Grammar & Key Structures)

### 1. 反意疑问句 (Tag Questions)
* **经典原句**：*That is John Smith, isn't it? / You are John Smith, the engineer, aren't you?*
* **核心解析**：
反意疑问句用于对已知事实进行确认或寻求对方的赞同。其基本原则是“前肯后否”或“前否后肯”。当陈述部分含有 `be` 动词时，反问部分也用相应的 `be` 动词形式。
* **结构解析**：`陈述句 (主语 + be动词) + , + be动词否定式 + 主语代词?` -> `You are..., aren't you?`

### 2. 宾语从句中的疑问句 (Questions in Object Clauses)
* **经典原句**：*My wife wants to know if Mary needs any help.*
* **核心解析**：
当疑问句作为宾语从句时，语序必须由“疑问语序”变为“陈述语序”。若原句是一般疑问句，在从句中需用 `if` 或 `whether` 引导。
* **结构解析**：`主句 (My wife wants to know) + 引导词 (if) + 从句主语 (Mary) + 从句谓语 (needs) + 其他成分 (any help).`

## 写作仿写与思辨口语 (Writing Practice & Critical Thinking)

### 1. ✍️ 提分句型仿写 (Pattern Drill)
* **句型名称**：...aren't you? / ...isn't it? (……不是吗？)
* **句型公式**：`[主语] + be动词 + [名词/形容词], + be动词否定式 + [主语代词]?`
* **仿写练习 1**：你是汤姆，不是吗？ (You are Tom, aren't you?)
* **仿写练习 2**：这是你的电话，不是吗？ (This is your telephone, isn't it?)

### 2. 🔍 近义词/易混词辨析 (Word Discrimination)
* **对比辨析**：**speak vs. tell vs. talk**
* **使用差异解析**：
  * `speak` 侧重于说话的动作或能力，常用于正式场合或语言（speak English）。
  * `tell` 侧重于“告诉、讲述”，后面通常接双宾语（tell sb. sth.），强调信息的传递。
  * `talk` 侧重于双方的交谈、讨论，常与 `with` 或 `to` 连用。
* **地道表达搭配**：
  * `Yes, speaking.` (电话用语：是我。)
  * `Tell me the truth.` (告诉我真相。)
  * `talk about something` (谈论某事)

### 3. 💬 课后思辨讨论题 (Speaking & Writing Prompts)
* **Question 1**: Have you ever experienced a "wrong number" call or a misunderstanding on the phone? How did you handle it politely?
* **Question 2**: In the digital age, do you prefer phone calls or text messages? Why do you think misunderstandings happen more easily in one than the other?
* **高分表达提示 (Hint Words)**: *misunderstanding (误解), communication breakdown (沟通障碍), tone of voice (语调), clarity (清晰度), professional etiquette (职业礼仪), instant messaging (即时通讯)*
