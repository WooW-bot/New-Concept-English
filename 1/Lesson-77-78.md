# Lesson 77-78: Terrible Toothache

## 课文内容 (Text)

Good morning, Mr. Croft.
Good morning, nurse.
I want to see the dentist, please.
Do you have an appointment?
No, I don't.
It's very urgent.
I feel awful.
I have a terrible toothache.
Can you come at 10 a.m. on Monday, April 24th?
I must see the dentist now, nurse.
The dentist is very busy at the moment.
Can you come at 2 p.m.?
That's very late.
Can the dentist see me now?
I'm afraid that he can't, Mr. Croft.
Can't you wait till this afternoon?
I can wait, but my toothache can't!

## 语法要点解析 (Grammar & Key Structures)

### 1. 情态动词 must 与 can 的用法 (Modal Verbs: Must & Can)
* **经典原句**：*I must see the dentist now, nurse.* / *Can you come at 2 p.m.?*
* **核心解析**：
`must` 表示说话人的主观意愿或强烈的必要性，意为“必须”。`can` 在此处用于询问对方是否有能力或时间做某事，表示请求或询问可能性。
* **结构解析**：情态动词后直接接动词原形。`must` 强调主观迫切感，`can` 强调客观能力或可能性。

### 2. 时间状语的表达方式 (Time Expressions)
* **经典原句**：*Can you come at 10 a.m. on Monday, April 24th?*
* **核心解析**：
在英语中，时间的表达遵循“从小到大”的原则。`at` 用于具体时刻（10 a.m.），`on` 用于具体的日期或星期（Monday, April 24th）。
* **结构解析**：`at + 具体时刻 + on + 星期/日期`。注意在书写日期时，月份首字母需大写。

## 写作仿写与思辨口语 (Writing Practice & Critical Thinking)

### 1. ✍️ 提分句型仿写 (Pattern Drill)
* **句型名称**：I have a ... (我患有……)
* **句型公式**：`I have a + [疾病名词] + (ache/pain).`
* **仿写练习 1**：我头痛得很厉害。(I have a terrible headache.)
* **仿写练习 2**：我背痛。(I have a backache.)

### 2. 🔍 近义词/易混词辨析 (Word Discrimination)
* **对比辨析**：**urgent vs. important**
* **使用差异解析**：
  * `urgent` 侧重于**时间上的紧迫性**，强调“现在就要做，不能拖延”。
  * `important` 侧重于**价值上的重要性**，强调“这件事意义重大，但未必非要现在立刻完成”。
* **地道表达搭配**：
  * an urgent matter (紧急事件)
  * make an appointment (预约)

### 3. 💬 课后思辨讨论题 (Speaking & Writing Prompts)
* **Question 1**: Why is it important to make an appointment before visiting a doctor or a professional? What are the benefits for both the service provider and the patient?
* **Question 2**: If you were the nurse, how would you handle an impatient patient who is in pain but has no appointment? What kind of communication skills are needed?
* **高分表达提示 (Hint Words)**: *professionalism (专业素养), empathy (同理心), time management (时间管理), medical resources (医疗资源), patience (耐心)*
